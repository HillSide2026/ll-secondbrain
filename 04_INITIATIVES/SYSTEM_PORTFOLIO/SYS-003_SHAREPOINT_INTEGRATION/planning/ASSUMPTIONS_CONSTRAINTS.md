# SYS-003 SharePoint Integration - Assumptions and Constraints

## Assumptions
- Site-class distinctions remain the right mechanism for SharePoint governance
- Managed-site wrappers are the right mechanism for broad but bounded site operations
- SharePoint remains an external governed workspace, not canonical doctrine storage

## Constraints
- No cross-site authority by implication
- No tenant-wide mutation authority through this packet
- No assumption that doctrine wording automatically equals implemented runtime
- No SharePoint-native state as a substitute for ML2 canonical records
