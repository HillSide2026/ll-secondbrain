# Stage 4.5 — Simple Ontario Incorporation (D3)

## Purpose
Execute a “simple” OBCA incorporation as a D3 deliverable under the Stage 4 release protocol.

## In Scope
- Standard share structure (single class common)
- ≤ 2 founders
- No vesting
- No USA
- No tax rollover
- No custom share rights drafting
- Single system propagation (Drive + matter metadata only)
- Filing required (handled as irreversible; corrective filing only)

## Out of Scope
- Custom share rights or complex structures
- USA drafting or tax‑sensitive restructuring
- Multi‑system propagation beyond Drive + matter metadata

## Inputs & Dependencies
- `02_PLAYBOOKS/_assets/execution/rubrics/quality_rubric/README.md`
- `02_PLAYBOOKS/execution/supervised_execution_runbook/README.md`
- `02_PLAYBOOKS/_assets/execution/worksheets/ml1_approval_worksheet/README.md`
- `02_PLAYBOOKS/_assets/execution/schemas/action_proposal_schema/README.md`

## Workflow Summary
Classify D3 → Assemble incorporation packet → QA scoring → Approval artifact → Supervised execution → Confirmation → Log

## Quality Gate
Execution allowed only if **Total score ≥ 11/12** and **Correctness = 2**, **No Hallucinations = 2**, **Proper Scope & Authority = 2**.

## Entry Criteria
- Stage 4.3 complete
- D3 incorporation template and checklist available
- Approval artifact prepared

## Exit Criteria
- D3 incorporation executed and logged
- Artifact consistency validated
- No corrective filing required

## Artifacts Produced
- Articles of Incorporation (standard)
- Resolutions packet
- Share subscription/issuance records
- Matter metadata update
- Execution log

## Audit & Logging Requirements
- Log QA scores, approvals, and confirmations
- Record filing receipt and irreversible‑filing note
- Maintain execution logs under `06_RUNS/`
