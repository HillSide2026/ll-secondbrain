# SYS-002 Gmail Integration - Assumptions and Constraints

## Assumptions
- The current Gmail surface is useful enough to govern explicitly
- Controlled label writes are materially narrower than general mailbox mutation
- Matter Admin depends on Gmail as a communications signal, not as a canonical matter authority

## Constraints
- No send, draft-send, delete, archive, or content-mutation authority
- No new Gmail write class without a separate ML1 decision
- Gmail may not redefine matter identity or doctrine authority
- Approval and audit expectations must remain explicit for write behavior
