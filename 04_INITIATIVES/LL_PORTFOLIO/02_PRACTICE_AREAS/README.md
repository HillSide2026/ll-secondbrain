# 02_PRACTICE_AREAS — Durable Legal Operating Knowledge

## Purpose

Durable legal operating knowledge by domain.

## Scope

### In Scope

- Playbooks
- Checklists
- Standards
- ML1-approved doctrine
- Version tracking

### Out of Scope

- Client-specific material
- Pricing
- Strategy
- "Common practice" treated as authority

## ML1 Authority Statement

ML1 approves all doctrine. ML2 may maintain playbooks, checklists, and standards but must not update doctrine without formal change control.

## Explicit Prohibitions

ML2 must NOT:

- Store client-specific material here
- Include pricing information
- Develop strategy
- Treat "common practice" as authority
- Update doctrine without ML1 approval
