# LLP-044 Finance Success Criteria

LLP-044 succeeds when:

1. Finance doctrine, invariants, and policies are housed under
   `01_FINANCIAL_MANAGEMENT/LLP-044_FINANCE`.
2. Finance remains distinct from bookkeeping, management accounting,
   budgeting, and operating cash/collections.
3. Finance outputs remain advisory unless ML1 approves a specific decision.
4. CFO advisory outputs have a governed home.
5. Cross-links to management-accounting cash-risk categories remain intact.
