# LLP-044 Finance Problem Statement

LL finance materials previously lived under `06_FINANCIAL_PORTFOLIO`, while
accounting and budgeting lived in adjacent financial domains. That structure
made the finance/accounting distinction visible, but it also split the
financial-management operating picture across top-level programs.

ML1 approved consolidation into `01_FINANCIAL_MANAGEMENT` and approved
`LLP-044_FINANCE` as the governed finance packet. This packet is needed so
finance remains governed after consolidation rather than becoming a loose
folder of principles and policies.
