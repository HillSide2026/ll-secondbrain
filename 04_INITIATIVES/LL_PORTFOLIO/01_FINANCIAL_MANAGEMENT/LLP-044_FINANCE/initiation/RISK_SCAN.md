# LLP-044 Finance Risk Scan

| Risk | Type | Severity | Mitigation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Finance outputs are treated as approved decisions | Authority | High | Keep ML1 authority statement in README and core artifacts |
| Finance collapses back into accounting or budgeting | Boundary | Medium | Maintain separate packet and explicit out-of-scope list |
| Trust balances are mistaken for operating liquidity | Professional / cash | High | Preserve finance invariant that trust is not cash |
| Revenue streams are compared only by nominal dollars | Analysis | Medium | Link finance analysis to management-accounting cash-risk categories |
| CFO advisory outputs become operational instructions | Governance | Medium | Keep CFO outputs advisory unless ML1 approves action |
