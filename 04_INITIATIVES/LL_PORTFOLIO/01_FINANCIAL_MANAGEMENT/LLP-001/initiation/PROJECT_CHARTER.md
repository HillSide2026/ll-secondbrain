# Project Charter

Project ID: LLP-001
Project Path: 01_FINANCIAL_MANAGEMENT/LLP-001
Project Type: Operational Project
Stage: Planning

## 1. Purpose
- Govern Levine Law's accounting fact layer so historical financial records are captured, reconciled, and reviewable without crossing into modeling or decision authority.

## 2. Nature of Project
- Operational accounting control packet for historical fact, reconciliations, and source-bounded recordkeeping.

## 3. Strategic Rationale
- The firm needs a clean separation between accounting facts, financial modeling, and strategic judgment.
- Active accounting work should be represented by a governed project packet rather than only an unlabeled operating lane.

## 4. High-Level Deliverable
- A governed accounting packet covering booked transactions, reconciliations, statements, invoices, payments, expense records, and the accounting-to-modeling boundary.

## 5. Authority
Final approval authority: ML1.

## 6. Promotion Path
- Planning is authorized. Advance to Executing once ML1 confirms the accounting scope, reconciliation cadence, evidence sources, and handoff rules into LLP-002.
