# Risk Scan

Project ID: LLP-003
Project Path: 05_MATTER_DOCKETING/LLP-003_WEEKLY_REPORT

## Top 5 Risks
1. Strategic risk: the weekly report may emphasize the wrong operating signals and become less useful for ML1 review.
2. Scope risk: the report may drift into authoritative matter status, billing, intake, or other out-of-scope domains.
3. Schedule risk: weekly production may become inconsistent and reduce trust in the packet.
4. Budget risk: preparing the report may require more manual effort than assumed.
5. Financial risk: missed blockers or stale delivery signals may indirectly create cost through delay or rework.

## Key Assumptions
- Required delivery signals can be gathered in a read-only way from existing systems.
- The report can remain an operating visibility layer rather than a source-of-truth status system.
- Weekly cadence is sustainable with the available operating capacity.
- ML1 will use the report as a decision aid, not as replacement for source review.
- Escalation paths can be captured cleanly enough to keep the report actionable.
