# Risk Scan

Project ID: LLP-009
Project Path: 05_MATTER_DOCKETING/LLP-009_CLERK_SUPERVISION

## Top 5 Risks
1. Strategic risk: the supervision model may not align with the intended clerk role and authority boundary.
2. Scope risk: clerk work may drift into substantive legal judgment or lawyer-only tasks.
3. Schedule risk: review cadence may be too slow to catch queue exceptions or escalation triggers.
4. Budget risk: supervision overhead may exceed the planned operating capacity for the role.
5. Financial risk: expected leverage gains may not offset the review burden imposed on ML1 or supervising lawyers.

## Key Assumptions
- Clerk duties can be defined and bounded without ambiguity.
- Escalation routes for legal judgment calls will be explicit and consistently followed.
- Supervising lawyers can sustain the review cadence required by the workflow.
- Queue and docketing signals will be visible enough to identify supervision exceptions.
- The role will create net operational leverage rather than only additional oversight load.
