# Risk Scan

Project ID: LLP-010
Project Path: 05_MATTER_DOCKETING/LLP-010_ASSOCIATE_SUPERVISION

## Top 5 Risks
1. Strategic risk: the supervision model may not preserve the intended associate role and quality standard.
2. Scope risk: associate work may expand beyond approved authority or supervision boundaries.
3. Schedule risk: review bottlenecks may delay delivery decisions and feedback loops.
4. Budget risk: supervision time may exceed baseline capacity assumptions.
5. Financial risk: increased supervision cost may outpace the economic benefit of the associate layer.

## Key Assumptions
- Associate authority and escalation boundaries can be defined explicitly.
- Supervising lawyers can review work quickly enough to support delivery needs.
- Quality-control expectations will be documented and consistently applied.
- The workflow will generate enough leverage to justify added supervision effort.
- Matter docketing signals will support timely identification of supervision issues.
