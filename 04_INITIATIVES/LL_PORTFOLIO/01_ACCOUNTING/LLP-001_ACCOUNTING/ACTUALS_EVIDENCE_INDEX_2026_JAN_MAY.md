## ML1 Actuals-Level Finding

ML1 has identified Q1 2026 as unsuccessful on actuals. The core accounting-level problem is insufficient and uneven revenue conversion.

At the law-firm level, there are two fundamental inputs: the lawyer(s) and the matters. The Q1 2026 actuals indicate problems at both levels of analysis:

- Lawyer-side production: insufficient and/or uneven lawyer production reaching billable/collectible form.
- Matter-side conversion: insufficient and/or uneven matter flow converting into billed and collected revenue.

This finding is recorded from the actuals evidence only. It does not, by itself, approve a revised budget, create a scenario, prescribe staffing, or recommend an operational fix.
