# LLP-001_ACCOUNTING — Accounting Operations

## Purpose

Govern the accounting fact layer for Levine Law so booked transactions, reconciliations, statements, invoices, payments, and expense records are maintained as historical fact under ML1 authority.

## Scope

### In Scope

- Historical accounting records and close support materials
- Reconciliations and variance capture against booked facts
- Invoices, payments, expense logs, and supporting schedules
- Boundaries and handoffs from accounting facts to downstream model consumers

### Out of Scope

- Forecasting and scenario modeling
- Pricing decisions
- Recommendations or optimization logic
- Treating accounting records as authority for strategic decisions without ML1 review
