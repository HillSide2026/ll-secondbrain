# Scope Statement

## In Scope

- Define the internal operating model for the CAD 8,000/month, CAD 12,000/month,
  and CAD 20,000/month fintech retainer mandate types.
- Define included advisory categories and capacity assumptions.
- Define excluded work and separate-scope triggers.
- Define delivery standardization requirements needed to reduce average monthly delivery time toward 15 hours/client.
- Define skill mastery requirements for core retainer delivery and premium adjacent work.
- Define metrics for effective hourly yield, overages, renewals, client concentration, and scope leakage.
- Align the project with LLPRN-26 and POL-069.

## Mandate Types

| Mandate Type | Monthly Retainer | Scope Treatment | Capacity Treatment |
|---|---:|---|---|
| Tier 1 | CAD 8,000 | Scope to be determined | Capacity limit to be determined |
| Tier 2 | CAD 12,000 | Core bounded fintech advisory scope | Maximum 6 hours/week |
| Tier 3 | CAD 20,000 | Same scope envelope as Tier 2 | Maximum 10 hours/week |

## 2026 Scope Lock

Until December 31, 2026, LL should only deliver the basic bounded advisory scope
under this retainer model.

Premium adjacent matters are outside the 2026 retainer delivery model. They may
be issue-spotted or preserved for later separate scope, but they should not be
accepted as active 2026 retainer work without express ML1 exception approval.

## Core Scope for CAD 12,000 and CAD 20,000 Mandates

Included retainer work may include bounded:
- discrete legal advice;
- document review and mark-up;
- short-form written advice;
- regulatory characterization and risk framing;
- FINTRAC reporting-trigger advisory support;
- banking relationship support materials;
- selected core policy, terms, and standard-form documentation refinement; and
- up to one defined regulatory strategy initiative where expressly scoped.

The CAD 20,000/month mandate does not expand the substantive scope envelope. It
expands the weekly capacity limit from 6 hours/week to 10 hours/week. Work
outside the scope envelope remains separate-scope even for the CAD 20,000/month
mandate.

## Excluded / Separate-Scope Work

The following are outside the standard retainer unless separately scoped:
- regulatory exams, investigations, enforcement, remediation, and exposure management;
- regulator, supervisor, bank, or financial-institution interface beyond ordinary document support;
- incident and crisis management;
- new product launches;
- entry into new jurisdictions;
- material transactions;
- significant business-model changes;
- RPAA safeguarding design for PSPs that hold, settle, clear, or otherwise control client/end-user funds;
- full Canada market-entry or jurisdiction-expansion execution;
- tax advice;
- securities law advice; and
- litigation, disputes, or contentious regulatory work.

## Out of Scope

- Executing client-facing marketing campaigns.
- Finalizing engagement-letter language without ML1 approval.
- Building full delivery templates for every excluded premium workstream.
- Performing legal research for a live client matter.
- Changing POL-069 without separate doctrine approval.
