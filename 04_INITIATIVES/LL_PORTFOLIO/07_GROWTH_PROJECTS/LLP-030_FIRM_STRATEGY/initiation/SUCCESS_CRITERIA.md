# Success Criteria

Project ID: LLP-030
Project Path: 07_GROWTH_PROJECTS/LLP-030_FIRM_STRATEGY
Stage: Initiating

## This project succeeds when:

1. **`FIRM_STRATEGY.md` is ML1-approved** and contains:
   - A clear statement of what the firm is optimizing for (and in what priority order)
   - ICP definitions consistent with LLP-025
   - A 3–5 year vision for the firm
   - Explicit strategic exclusions (what the firm does not do)

2. **`BUSINESS_PLAN.md` is ML1-approved** and contains:
   - A target revenue scenario (from LLP-002 budget) with a timeline
   - An acquisition architecture sequencing F01 → F02 → F03
   - A capacity model defining ML1's ceiling and staffing trigger points
   - A staffing roadmap (setter, senior lawyer — when, at what cost, against what revenue threshold)
   - Quarterly milestones

3. **`FINANCIAL_MODEL.md` is ML1-approved** and contains:
   - A P&L projection linking marketing spend → matter volume → revenue → margin under each scenario
   - Break-even analysis by scenario
   - Financial impact of staffing additions (setter, senior lawyer)
   - A model that answers: "at current acquisition cost and matter value, what does margin look like?"

4. **Portfolio alignment is confirmed**: LLP-025, LLP-011, LLP-012 have each been reviewed and confirmed consistent with the approved Firm Strategy.

## What does NOT constitute success:

- A Firm Strategy that describes the firm as it is today (not aspirational)
- A Business Plan that restates project workplans without a governing financial model
- A Financial Model that cannot answer: "at $X marketing spend, targeting $Y revenue scenario, what is the projected net margin?"
- Approved documents that are not actively referenced in downstream project gate decisions
