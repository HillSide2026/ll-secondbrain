# Risk Scan

Project ID: LLP-030
Project Path: 07_GROWTH_PROJECTS/LLP-030_FIRM_STRATEGY
Stage: Initiating

| # | Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| R-01 | **Strategy without conviction**: Firm Strategy is drafted as a governance artifact but ML1 has not committed to a genuine direction (e.g., "all of the above" on optimization priorities) | Medium | High — without real trade-offs, the strategy cannot govern project authorization decisions | Force explicit choices: the strategy must state what the firm is NOT optimizing for |
| R-02 | **Financial model complexity exceeds usefulness**: A detailed P&L model that requires constant updating is abandoned quickly | Medium | Medium | Keep the financial model scenario-based and decision-focused, not a live spreadsheet |
| R-03 | **Downstream projects do not align**: LLP-025, LLP-011, LLP-012 continue executing on their own trajectories even after Firm Strategy is approved | Low | High | Build alignment review into each project's next gate decision |
| R-04 | **Staffing decisions made before financial model exists**: Setter or senior lawyer is added before the capacity model confirms the trigger point | Medium | Medium — cost added without confirmed revenue capacity | Gate staffing decisions against Business Plan approval |
| R-05 | **No data for financial model**: Matter value floor and conversion rates are currently undefined or estimated; the model may be based on assumptions that are quickly invalidated | High | Medium | Document assumptions explicitly; flag which inputs require 30-day data validation before the model is used for decisions |
| R-06 | **Owner-compensation target may depend on cross-entity crediting**: Levine Law may not reach the current `CAD 80,000` owner-compensation target on direct firm economics alone, creating pressure to credit Andersen Service Line economics even though Andersen fee receipts land in `17513721 Canada Inc` | Medium | High | Keep Andersen receipts out of direct Levine Law revenue by default and require an explicit ML1 crediting rule before they are used to support Levine Law compensation planning |

## Go Decision

**Go** — opening initiation stage. R-05 (data quality) and R-06 (owner-compensation dependence on cross-entity crediting) are the most live risks; both are mitigated by keeping Andersen receipts out of direct Levine Law revenue by default and documenting any exception as an explicit ML1 decision.
