# Levine Law — Business Plan

---

## 1. Executive Overview

Levine Law is a corporate/commercial legal practice designed for high-realization, low-variance execution of repeatable mandates within a controlled capacity model.

The firm is built on three principles:
- **Throughput discipline** — predictable intake → execution → billing cycles
- **Template-driven delivery** — standardized, reusable legal products
- **Capacity-gated growth** — hiring only when utilization thresholds are exceeded

2026 is a production validation year, with no leverage beyond ML1. The objective is to establish stable revenue generation, validate acquisition channels, and define the operating ceiling of a single-lawyer platform.

## 3. Acquisition Architecture

The firm operates three acquisition channels (funnel definitions are canonical across the LL Portfolio):

### F01 — Google Ads → Reactive Intake

- Google Ads driving inbound inquiries via GHL
- Reactive, undifferentiated demand — matters vary in quality and fit
- Qualification gate: revenue ≥ $1M, employees ≥ 5, accountant involved

**Role**: Current primary revenue source; funds operations while F02 is built

**Contribution target**
- Q1–Q2: 70–80% of revenue
- Q3–Q4: 40–50% (declining as F02 activates)

---

### F02 — Corporate Health Check → ICP-01 Acquisition

- Paid diagnostic entry offer ($1,500–$2,500)
- Distributed primarily through accountant referral network
- ICP-01 qualified leads only; intake gate enforced at booking
- Health Check findings convert to defined engagement scope

**Role**: Primary corporate acquisition channel once operational; replaces F01 as revenue anchor

**Contribution target**
- Q2: early launch, minimal contribution
- Q3: 20–30%
- Q4: 40–50%

---

### F03 — Payments / MSB Authority → ICP-02 Acquisition

- Authority content targeting payments, MSB, and fintech operators
- Entry offer: AML / MSB registration engagement
- Longer build time; Blog/SEO engine does not activate until Health Check landing page is live

**Role**: Vertical growth channel; authority-driven, longer conversion cycle

**Contribution target**
- Q3: 10–20% (early)
- Q4: 20–30%

---

### Channel Sequencing Logic

F01 → F02 → F03 is not simultaneous scaling:
- F01 funds survival
- F02 stabilizes flow with higher-quality clients
- F03 introduces the vertical specialization layer

## 4. Capacity Model

ML1 operates as the sole fee earner in 2026.

**Estimated Capacity**
- Active matters: 12–18 concurrent
- Weekly throughput: 6–8 meaningful deliverables
- Monthly ceiling: ~$25K–$30K

**Capacity Thresholds**

| Condition | Trigger | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Sustained overload | >18 active matters for 4+ weeks | Tighten intake / increase pricing |
| Pipeline overflow | Lead response delay >48h | Introduce intake filtering |
| Revenue ceiling hit | Consistent $25K+ months | Prepare staffing layer |

**Key Principle**: Capacity is a design constraint, not a problem to solve prematurely. No hiring occurs until revenue is stable, work is systematized, and the bottleneck is persistent — not temporary.

## 5. Staffing Roadmap

### 2026: No Core Hires

ML1 remains sole operator.

Permitted support: ad hoc contractors (admin, formatting, filings). No fixed salary commitments.

---

### Future Roles (Defined, Not Activated)

**Setter (Acquisition Role)**
- Function: qualify and book leads; reduce ML1 intake burden
- Trigger: [X] consecutive months at $20K+/month with intake as the identifiable constraint
- Gate: role definition, script, and GHL handoff workflow must be ML1-approved before activation

**Senior Lawyer (Delivery Role)**
- Function: absorb execution workload; extend matter delivery capacity
- Trigger: sustained capacity breach at $300K+ annualized revenue with persistent overflow work
- Gate: matter eligibility list, supervision model, and compensation structure ML1-approved before activation

**Staffing Doctrine**: Hiring is triggered by constraint, funded by existing revenue, and justified by throughput gain.

## 6. Quarterly Milestones

**Q1 — Foundation**

**Q4 — Lock-In**
- Achieve consistent run rate
- Identify repeatable matter types
- Prepare 2027 scale architecture
- Target: $22K–$25K/month sustained

## 8. Operating Doctrine

Levine Law is not built as a traditional law firm. It is a controlled production system focused on repeatable legal work, designed for predictable revenue generation and structured to scale only after stability is established.

---

## 9. End-State (2026)

By year-end, the firm should achieve:
- Stable $22K–$25K monthly revenue
- Defined acquisition channels (F01 active, F02 operational, F03 early-stage)
- Documented workflows and templates
- Clear capacity ceiling established for ML1
- No operational chaos or backlog

This creates the foundation for 2027 growth (increased throughput) and 2028 (bringing in a partner).
