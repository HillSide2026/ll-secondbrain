# Communication Plan

Project ID: `LLP-030`
Project Path: `04_INITIATIVES/LL_PORTFOLIO/07_GROWTH_PROJECTS/LLP-030_FIRM_STRATEGY`
Stage: `Planning`

## Communication Objective

Keep LLP-030 usable as a governed strategy packet by making sure material
changes are reviewed by ML1, linked projects are updated when needed, and the
project does not silently drift away from the rest of the LL operating model.

## Core Cadence

| Cadence | Audience | Purpose | Primary Artifact |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| On material edit | ML1 | review any change to operating target, ICP threshold, staffing sequence, or Andersen treatment | root LLP-030 docs plus this planning packet |
| Weekly while planning is open | ML1 | review planning progress, open contradictions, and unresolved financial-model questions | `PROJECT_PLAN.md`, `METRICS.md` |
| As needed on linked-project drift | owners of `LLP-002`, `LLP-025`, `LLP-011`, `LLP-012`, `LLP-013`, `LLP-033` | confirm alignment or surface conflict | `DEPENDENCIES.md` |
| At management-cycle refresh | LL Chief of Staff and governance layer | keep portfolio-level reporting synchronized with LLP-030's actual state | `initiation/APPROVAL_RECORD.md`, management rollups |

## Escalation Triggers

Escalate to ML1 immediately if any of the following occur:

- the `CAD 240,000` operating baseline changes
- owner-compensation assumptions change materially
- staffing logic changes from associate-lawyer-first to another sequence
- Andersen crediting or reconciliation assumptions change
- `LLP-025`, `LLP-011`, `LLP-012`, `LLP-013`, or `LLP-033` now conflict
  materially with LLP-030
- the financial model still cannot answer basic profitability questions after
  the current planning sprint

## Communication Boundaries

- LLP-030 does not authorize external communications by itself
- LLP-030 does not authorize hiring, spend changes, or funnel launch by itself
- downstream packets must not be treated as aligned merely because LLP-030 was
  updated; alignment must be checked explicitly

## Record Rule

Any material LLP-030 planning decision should be reflected in:

1. the affected root document(s)
2. the relevant planning-stage control document(s)
3. `initiation/APPROVAL_RECORD.md` if the change affects stage posture or gate
   readiness
