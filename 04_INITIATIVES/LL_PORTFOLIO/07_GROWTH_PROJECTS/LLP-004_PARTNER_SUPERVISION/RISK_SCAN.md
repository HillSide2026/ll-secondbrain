# Risk Scan

## Top 5 Risks
1. Strategic risk: draft metrics may be perceived as performance enforcement rather than support.
2. Strategic risk: metrics may distort partner behavior in unintended ways.
3. Strategic risk: measurement design may prioritize what is easy to count rather than what matters.
4. Strategic risk: the governance model may appear to supervise ML1 inappropriately.
5. Strategic risk: partners may interpret the system as surveillance rather than capacity protection.

## Key Assumptions
- Partner supervision metrics can be framed as support and capacity controls rather than enforcement.
- Metric design will stay subordinate to judgment and context.
- The governance model will preserve ML1 authority boundaries clearly.
- Partners will receive enough context to understand the purpose of the system.
- Cultural trust can be maintained while still introducing visibility controls.
