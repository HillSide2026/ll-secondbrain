# LLP-031 — Corporate Entity Management

## Purpose

Determine whether Levine Law should procure third-party entity-management
software, build an internal entity-management capability, or defer investment
until the economics and operating model are stronger.

## Scope

### In Scope

- build-vs-buy framing for entity-management capability
- operating requirements for recurring entity tracking and deadline control
- affordability and value assessment relative to current firm scale
- dependency mapping to future staffing and supervision projects
- recommendation packet for ML1 decision

### Out of Scope

- live procurement or implementation without ML1 approval
- product build execution
- client rollout of an entity-management service line
- treating vendor research or system sketches as approved operating doctrine
