# Risk Scan


| Risk | Severity | Note |
|---|---|---|
| Retainer scope expands into embedded compliance or crisis response | High | Explicit exclusions required |
| Premium mandates exceed ML1 capacity | High | Capacity model must govern concurrent mandate count |
| Pricing does not reflect weekly access burden | Medium | Tie mandate tiers to hours and realization |
