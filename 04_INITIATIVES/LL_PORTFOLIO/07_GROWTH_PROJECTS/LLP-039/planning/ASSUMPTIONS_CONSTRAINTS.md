# Assumptions and Constraints

Project ID: LLP-039
Stage: Planning


## Assumptions

- A bounded fintech advisory retainer can stabilize recurring revenue if scope is narrow.
- Weekly capacity limits are the controlling design variable.

## Constraints

- 2026 model excludes crisis response, embedded compliance, exams, launches, and separate-scope premium adjacent work.
- ML1 remains the authority for pricing, acceptance, and scope changes.
