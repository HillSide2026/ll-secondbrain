# Risk Scan

## Top 5 Risks
1. Strategic risk: malpractice exposure may increase if the supervision model is not robust enough.
2. Strategic risk: legal reasoning or drafting quality may vary beyond acceptable standards.
3. Strategic risk: firm standards may dilute through cultural misalignment or expectation drift.
4. Financial risk: overhead may increase without matching revenue contribution.
5. Schedule risk: ML1 supervision capacity may become a bottleneck for timely review and feedback.

## Key Assumptions
- The associate role will be paired with a workable supervision framework.
- Quality standards can be taught, reviewed, and enforced consistently.
- The role will strengthen rather than dilute firm culture.
- Revenue opportunity exists to support the added overhead.
- ML1 or delegated supervisors can sustain the cadence needed for review.
