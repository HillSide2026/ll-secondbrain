## levinellp.ca Surface Map (2026-04-14)

The following is the working surface map for levinellp.ca, confirmed by ML1 on 2026-04-14.

### 1. ll-task-tracker
**Role:** Canonical admin matter system
**Owns:**
- Matter creation
- Matter identity
- Matter lifecycle
- Matter state semantics
- Admin workflow control
- Operational readiness and handoffs

### 2. ll-corporate
**Role:** Client portal
**Owns:**
- Client-safe matter views
- Bounded client actions
- Controlled exposure of backend truth

**Does not own:**
- Matter lifecycle definitions
- Alternate matter states
- Commercial pipeline logic

### 3. Public website
**Role:** Brand and traffic capture
**Pages:** `/`, `/clients`, `/clients/LP1–LP3`, `/services`, `/services/LP1–LP3`, `/inquiries`, `/blog`
**Owns:**
- Public content
- CTA entry points into marketing/commercial flow

### 4. Marketing surface
**Role:** Commercial relationship pipeline
**Owns:**
- Contacts
- Opportunities
- Opportunity progression
- Pre-matter commercial workflow
- Onboarding-stage commercial coordination

**Key distinction:** The marketing surface may own the opportunity pipeline, but it must not own the matter lifecycle.

### 5. NDA Esq (this project)
**Role:** Upsell pipeline
**Owns:**
- Contacts
- Re-sale opportunities
- Re-sale opportunity progression
- Re-engagement commercial workflow
- Extension-stage commercial coordination

**Key distinction:** The sales surface may own the upsell pipeline, but it must not own the matter lifecycle.
