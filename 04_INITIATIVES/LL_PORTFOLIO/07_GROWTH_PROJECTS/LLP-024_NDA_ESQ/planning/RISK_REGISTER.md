# Risk Register

Project ID: LLP-024
Project Path: 04_INITIATIVES/LL_PORTFOLIO/07_STRATEGIC_PROJECTS/LLP-024_NDA_ESQ
Stage: Planning

## Implementation Risk Register

| Risk | Category | Likelihood | Impact | Goal at Risk | Mitigation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| MVP feature set expands beyond generation, dashboard, payment, and reporting boundary | Scope | H | H | 60-day MVP target and controllable launch scope | Enforce explicit scope lock and require ML1 approval for any feature expansion |
| Consult escalation path blurs into the base offer | Scope | M | H | Product clarity and operating discipline | Keep consult follow-on separate from the generation product with explicit escalation rules |
| AI generation quality falls below target during validation | Schedule | M | H | MVP readiness and user trust | Run bounded output validation early and revise templates/prompts before launch approval |
| AI, payment, auth, or storage integrations slip and delay the build | Schedule | M | H | MVP delivery on target date | Sequence integration dependencies early and escalate blockers in weekly review |
| CAC exceeds the approved threshold and slows payback | Budget | H | H | Efficient customer acquisition and qualified-consult generation | Limit early spend, review channel performance weekly, and prune low-performing segments quickly |
| External usage fails to convert into qualified consults or retained-client adoption | Budget | M | H | Qualified-consult and retained-client usage targets | Tighten routing, targeting, and offer framing before expanding spend |
| Support, billing, or usage-management load exceeds operating capacity post-launch | Strategic | M | H | Support and repeat-usage targets | Define support workflow, escalation ownership, and dashboard visibility before launch |
| Acquisition channels underperform or become noisy | Strategic | M | M | Qualified-consult and adoption targets | Use focused channel testing and stop untargeted expansion outside approved channels |

## Escalation Rule

Any High-impact risk without an active owner or mitigation decision must be
escalated to ML1 in the next planning review.
