# Initial Risk Assessment

## 1. Tool-Driven Doctrine Risk
Software workflow dictating legal process before doctrine is formalized.

## 2. Vendor Lock-In
Data portability constraints or pricing escalation risk.

## 3. False Security Risk
Assumption that software eliminates malpractice exposure.

## 4. Overbuild Risk
Investing in infrastructure before validating service demand.

## 5. Delegation Risk
Improper task routing to non-lawyer roles.

## 6. Integration Risk
Misalignment with existing docketing and accounting systems.
