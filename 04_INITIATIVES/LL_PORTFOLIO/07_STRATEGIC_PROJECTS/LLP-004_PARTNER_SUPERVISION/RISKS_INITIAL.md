# Initial Risk Assessment

## 1. Misinterpretation Risk
Draft metrics perceived as performance enforcement.

## 2. Incentive Distortion
Metrics influencing behavior in unintended ways.

## 3. Over-Quantification
Measuring what is easy rather than what matters.

## 4. Governance Paradox
System attempting to supervise its own ultimate authority.

## 5. Cultural Impact
Metrics perceived as surveillance rather than capacity protection.
