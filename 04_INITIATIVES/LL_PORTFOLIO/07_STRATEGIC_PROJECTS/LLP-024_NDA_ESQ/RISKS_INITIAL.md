# Initial Risk Assessment

## 1. MVP Delivery Risk

The product stack may take longer than expected to deliver because web app,
AI-review, payment, dashboard, and reporting features must all work together.

## 2. AI Quality Risk

The review engine may underperform on clause detection, risk flagging, or user
trust before the target accuracy threshold is met.

## 3. Scope Creep Risk

The program could sprawl into manual drafting, full-service legal consulting, or
overbroad acquisition channels that slow MVP execution.

## 4. Acquisition Economics Risk

Paid acquisition and outreach may fail to hit user growth and CAC targets quickly
enough to validate the model.

## 5. Operations Load Risk

Support load, billing issues, churn, or lawyer-review escalation could outpace
the systems intended to manage them.
