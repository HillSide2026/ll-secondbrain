# Ongoing Compliance for a Canadian Subsidiary: What Gets Missed

Incorporation is a starting point, not an endpoint.

A Canadian subsidiary must maintain corporate records, file annual returns, and document decisions through resolutions.

Minute books must be updated. Shareholder and director actions must be recorded.

These obligations are often overlooked once operations begin.

The risk is not immediate. It appears when records are required.
