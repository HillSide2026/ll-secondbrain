# Director Residency Requirements in Canada: What Has Changed

Director residency requirements have historically limited who could serve on a Canadian board.

Federal changes have removed the residency requirement under the CBCA.

Provincial rules differ. Ontario no longer imposes a residency requirement for most corporations.

The practical issue is not compliance alone.

It is how governance is structured when directors are not physically present in Canada and how decisions are documented.
