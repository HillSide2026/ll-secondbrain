# We've Been Doing It This Way for Years: Legacy Decisions That Create Risk

Longevity creates confidence.

It also creates blind spots.

## How Legacy Issues Form

Early decisions are made under different conditions.

The business is smaller. Risk is lower. Structure is simpler.

Those decisions remain in place as the business evolves.

What was once practical becomes misaligned.

## Stability Masks Misalignment

Long-standing practices feel correct because they have not been challenged.

Contracts have not been tested.

Relationships have not broken down.

Structure has not been scrutinized.

The absence of conflict is treated as validation.

It is not.

## Accumulated Assumptions

Over time, assumptions build on each other.

Each decision relies on the last.

If the underlying structure is flawed, the effect compounds.

## When It Surfaces

Legacy issues emerge when the business changes direction.

A transaction is considered.

Ownership changes.

External review occurs.

At that point, past decisions are examined in a new context.

## The Practical Takeaway

Time does not resolve structural issues.

It embeds them.

A health check identifies where legacy decisions no longer reflect current reality.
