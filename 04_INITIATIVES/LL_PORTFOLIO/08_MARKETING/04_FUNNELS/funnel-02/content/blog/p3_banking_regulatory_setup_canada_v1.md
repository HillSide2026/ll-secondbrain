# Banking and Regulatory Setup for a Canadian Subsidiary

Opening a bank account is often more difficult than expected.

Financial institutions require clear documentation of structure, ownership, and control.

Where activities involve payments or money movement, additional regulatory considerations may apply.

In some cases, registration requirements arise.

These issues are not theoretical. They affect whether operations can begin.
