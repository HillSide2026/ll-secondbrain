# Corporate Health Check vs Legal Audit

The distinction matters.

## Legal Audit

A legal audit is comprehensive, checklist-driven, and focused on completeness.

## Health Check

A health check is selective, risk-driven, and focused on alignment.

## The Practical Takeaway

A health check identifies where attention is needed.

An audit confirms everything.

They serve different purposes.
