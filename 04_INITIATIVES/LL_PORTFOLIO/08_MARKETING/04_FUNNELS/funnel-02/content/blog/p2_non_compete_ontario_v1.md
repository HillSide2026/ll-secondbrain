# Non-Compete Agreements in Ontario: What Still Works After the Ban

Ontario's 2021 changes significantly restricted non-compete agreements.

Most are now unenforceable.

## The Shift

Non-competes are prohibited in most employment relationships, with limited exceptions.

This does not eliminate all restrictive covenants. It changes which ones are available.

## Non-Solicitation Remains

Non-solicitation clauses are still enforceable if they are reasonable in scope and duration.

These clauses focus on protecting relationships rather than restricting competition broadly.

## Misclassification Risk

If a contractor is later found to be an employee, a non-compete drafted for a contractor relationship may become unenforceable.

The classification affects enforceability.

## The Practical Takeaway

The enforceability of restrictive covenants depends not only on drafting, but on the underlying relationship.

If that relationship is misclassified, the covenant may not hold.
