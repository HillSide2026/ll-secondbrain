# When to Conduct a Corporate Health Check

A health check is rarely initiated proactively.

It is triggered.

## Common Triggers

Preparation for a sale or financing.

Shareholder changes.

Rapid growth or hiring.

Contract disputes or enforcement issues.

These events expose structural gaps.

## Timing Matters

Conducting a review after issues surface limits available options.

Earlier review allows for restructuring before pressure is applied.

## The Practical Takeaway

The right time for a health check is before the business is forced to rely on its existing structure.
