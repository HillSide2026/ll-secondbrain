# Representations and Warranties in Ontario Business Sales

Representations and warranties allocate risk.

They are statements about the condition of the business.

If they are inaccurate, the buyer may have recourse.

These provisions do not guarantee perfection. They define responsibility.

The scope of the representations and the limits on claims determine how risk is shared.
