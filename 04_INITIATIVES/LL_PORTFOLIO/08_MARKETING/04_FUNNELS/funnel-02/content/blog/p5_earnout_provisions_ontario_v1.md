# Earnout Provisions in Ontario Business Sales

Earnouts defer part of the purchase price.

Payment depends on future performance.

They are used where valuation is uncertain or where parties have different expectations.

Earnouts do not eliminate disagreement.

They move it into the future.

The structure of the earnout determines how that disagreement is resolved.
