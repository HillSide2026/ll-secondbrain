# Hiring in Canada Through a Subsidiary: What Actually Changes

Hiring through a Canadian entity creates local employment obligations.

Employment standards, payroll requirements, and statutory benefits apply.

Classification between contractor and employee must align with Canadian law, not foreign assumptions.

Employment agreements must reflect local requirements.

The structure of the subsidiary determines how these obligations are met.
