# Non-Compete Agreements in Ontario Business Sales

Non-competes in a sale context are treated differently from employment.

They are generally enforceable if reasonable.

The restriction protects the value being transferred.

Scope and duration must still align with what is necessary to protect that value.

Overreach creates risk of unenforceability.
