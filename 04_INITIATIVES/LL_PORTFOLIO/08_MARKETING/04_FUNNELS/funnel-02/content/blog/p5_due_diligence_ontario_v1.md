# Due Diligence in Ontario Business Purchases: What Gets Reviewed

Due diligence is not a checklist.

It is an investigation into how the business actually operates.

Financial records, contracts, employment arrangements, intellectual property, and regulatory compliance are all reviewed.

The objective is not completeness.

It is to identify issues that affect value, structure, or risk.

What is missed matters less than what is discovered too late.
