# Selling Your Business in Ontario: What Needs to Be Ready

Preparation affects outcome.

A business with organized records, clear ownership, and consistent contracts moves through diligence more efficiently.

Gaps do not prevent a sale. They change its terms.

Buyers price risk.

Preparation reduces uncertainty, which affects both price and structure.
