# What Happens to Your Contracts When Your Business Is Sold or Restructured

Contracts are not automatically transferable.

They must permit assignment.

## Assignment Restrictions

Many agreements restrict assignment without consent.

This means that a sale or restructuring may require approval from counterparties.

## Change of Control

Some contracts treat a change in ownership as a deemed assignment.

Even if the contracting entity remains the same, the agreement may be triggered.

## Practical Impact

This becomes critical in transactions.

Key contracts may need to be renegotiated or consented to before closing.

Delays and uncertainty often arise at this stage.

## The Practical Takeaway

Contracts do not move with the business unless they are structured to do so.

Assignment and change-of-control provisions determine whether they can follow the transaction.
