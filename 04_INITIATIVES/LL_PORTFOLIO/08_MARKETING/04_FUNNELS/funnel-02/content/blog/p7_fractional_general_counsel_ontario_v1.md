# Fractional General Counsel in Ontario: When It Makes Sense for a Growing Business

Legal needs do not scale linearly.

Early-stage businesses operate with minimal structure. Larger organizations build internal legal teams.

Between those points, complexity increases without a corresponding increase in internal capacity.

That is where fractional counsel becomes relevant.

## The Gap

Mid-sized businesses often face recurring contract negotiation, employment issues, governance questions, and transaction preparation.

These are not one-time events.

They require ongoing input, but not necessarily a full-time role.

## The Model

Fractional counsel provides ongoing availability, familiarity with the business, and continuity across issues.

This differs from project-based legal work.

The value is in context, not just execution.

## The Practical Takeaway

Fractional counsel is not a cost-saving measure.

It is a way to maintain alignment as the business evolves.
