# What a Corporate Health Check Reviews (And What It Doesn't)

A health check is not an audit.

It is targeted.

## What Gets Reviewed

The review covers corporate structure and records, shareholder arrangements, key contracts, employment classification, and regulatory exposure.

## What It Does Not Do

It does not attempt to review every document in detail.

It focuses on structural alignment.

## The Practical Takeaway

The objective is not completeness.

It is identifying where misalignment creates risk.
