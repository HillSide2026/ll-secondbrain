# SEO Pillars — Funnel 02

Funnel: Ontario Corporate Health Check
Target: Owner / CEO / CFO, Ontario operating businesses, $1M–$8M, 5–30 employees
Last Updated: 2026-03-16
Research Method: Web search — competitor content mapping, SERP topic analysis. No volume data. Volume and difficulty data requires Ahrefs/SEMrush.

---

## Architecture Note

Five SEO-driven pillars, each anchored to confirmed real search terms with active competitor content. A sixth section covers brand framework content — not SEO plays. "Structural drift" is a brand concept, not a search term.

Competitive context: Ontario.ca and Canada.ca dominate generic informational queries (e.g., "employee vs contractor ontario"). The opportunity is in employer-framed, consequence-and-action-oriented content that government pages don't produce.

## Pillar 2 — Contractor vs Employee Ontario

The employer-consequence angle (misclassification risk, CRA penalties, employment standards liability) is where law firms are competing and where the target audience sits. Non-compete and termination clause content is extremely active in 2025 due to multiple Ontario court decisions invalidating common clause language.

## Pillar 5 — Corporate and Commercial Transactions Ontario

**Confirmed search behavior:** Very active SERP for share vs. asset purchase — Hansra Law, Barriston Law, Jahan Law, Unified LLP, t2inc, Shajani CPA all competing. "Buying a business ontario" and "selling a business ontario" produce law firm service pages but limited plain-English educational content. Letter of intent, due diligence, and representations and warranties have active content from Pacific Legal, Hummingbird Law, Pawlina Law, Cote Law. Earnout and vendor take-back have lower coverage — opportunity.

**Why this pillar belongs in Funnel 2:** The $1M–$8M segment is the primary buyer and seller market for small-to-mid Ontario business transactions. A business owner considering acquisition needs legal review. A business owner preparing to sell needs their corporate records, contracts, and structure clean — which is exactly what the Corporate Health Check produces. This pillar captures both directions of the transaction and bridges directly to the offer.

**Pillar page anchor:** `buying a business ontario` / `selling a business ontario`

**Content note:** The seller-preparation angle (Posts 4 and 6) is the highest-value bridge to the Corporate Health Check offer. A buyer doing due diligence on your company will find everything the Health Check finds — except you won't have time to fix it. That framing converts.
