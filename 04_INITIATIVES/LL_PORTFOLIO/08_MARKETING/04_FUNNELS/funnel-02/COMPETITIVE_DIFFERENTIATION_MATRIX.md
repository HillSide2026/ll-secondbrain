# Funnel 02 Competitive Differentiation Matrix

## Purpose
Codify Funnel 02 positioning against common alternatives so market-facing messaging remains consistent and non-generic.

## Canonical Funnel 02 Position
- Category: Preventative structural review for Ontario operating companies.
- Entry offer: Ontario Corporate Health Check (paid diagnostic).
- Target buyer: Stage 3-4 operating business leadership ($1M-$8M, 5-30 employees).
- Promise: Structured governance, risk exposure, and bounded regulatory-review clarity with prioritized remediation path where relevant.

## Alternatives vs Funnel 02

| Alternative buyer may choose | Why it is attractive | Structural weakness at Stage 3-4 | Funnel 02 differentiator |
|---|---|---|---|
| Free legal consultation | No upfront cost; low friction | Low diagnostic depth; weak qualification; often reactive | Paid diagnostic with defined scope, artifacts, prioritization, and bounded regulatory review where needed |
| Incorporation-only / one-off corporate package | Familiar and inexpensive | Startup-era docs do not reflect current operating complexity | Evaluates current operating reality, not just incorporation footprint |
| Litigation-first legal engagement | Clear response in conflict scenarios | Enters too late; does not prevent structural drift | Prevention-first model catches issues before disputes |
| In-house ad hoc contract review | Fast tactical support | Fragmented review without governance-level integration | Cross-pillar structural assessment with remediation roadmap |
| Accountant-only governance comfort | Existing trusted advisor | Accounting lens may miss legal enforceability gaps | Legal governance and exposure analysis tied to operational use |
| Template-only DIY legal cleanup | Cheap and immediate | Document substitution without risk prioritization | Structured review + risk-tiered action plan |

## Why Funnel 02 Wins for the ICP
1. It matches buyer psychology: capable operators seeking structure, not panic response.
2. It uses a paid filter to qualify serious buyers and protect delivery quality.
3. It produces concrete outputs (risk summary, prioritized action plan, scorecard), including bounded regulatory review where client facts warrant it.
4. It creates a coherent bridge to remediation and fractional counsel.

## Stage-Based Category Messaging
- Awareness: Preventative structural review for growing Ontario operators.
- Consideration: Paid diagnostic to measure governance alignment and legal exposure.
- Conversion: Corporate Health Check purchase as structured commitment event.
- Post-conversion handoff: Fulfillment onboarding sequence (outside marketing ownership).

## Messaging Guardrails
- Do not use comparative superlatives ("best", "guaranteed", "risk-free").
- Do not imply outcome certainty.
- Do not blur marketing with legal advice.
- Keep positioning calm, commercial, and maturity-led.
