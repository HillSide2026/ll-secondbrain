# When Corporate Documents No Longer Match Business Reality

## Static Documents

Corporate documents are fixed at the time they are created.

They reflect a specific moment.

## Evolving Operations

Business activity does not remain fixed.

Decisions, roles, and economics evolve continuously.

## Dual Reality

A gap emerges between what documents state and how the business operates.

This gap may remain unexamined. The broader pattern of [how this divergence develops and why it persists](/business-structure/drift-invisible-until-it-matters) is examined in detail separately.

## Consequence

When reviewed, both versions must be reconciled.

The analysis is not limited to documents alone.

Conduct becomes relevant.
