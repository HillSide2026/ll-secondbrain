# Funnel 03 BD Networking Plan

## Purpose

Add a controlled networking-based business development lane to Funnel 03 for
payments, MSB, PSP, and stablecoin-related legal work.

This plan complements the existing content-driven funnel. It does not replace
content, intake controls, or ML1 approval boundaries.

## Objective

Generate more qualified consults and retained matters by building direct
relationships with operators, founders, executives, referral sources, and
ecosystem intermediaries in the payments and regulated-fintech market.

## Focus Areas

Primary focus:

- core Canadian regulatory institutions shaping the payments, MSB, PSP, AML,

## Operating Plan

### 1. Target Selection

- prioritize networking environments where regulated-fintech decision-makers
  are concentrated
- focus on high-probability relationship environments, not broad social reach

### 2. Networking Motion

- attend relevant events or curated meetings
- hold targeted one-to-one follow-ups after introductions
- use a consultative, authority-based positioning approach rather than a
  generic sales pitch

### 3. Capture and Follow-Up

- every qualified networking contact should be captured in the active funnel
  tracking system
- follow-up should route contacts toward the correct Funnel 03 entry offer,
  consult, or retained engagement path
- follow-up messaging must remain within approved marketing and intake
  boundaries

### 4. Qualification Rule

- networking activity counts only when it creates a trackable qualified lead,
  consult, or retained opportunity
- low-signal social contact without a defined next step should not be treated
  as pipeline progress

## Success Signals

- networking becomes a documented acquisition lane inside Funnel 03
- qualified networking contacts are captured and attributable
- networking-sourced consults can be distinguished from content-sourced consults
- networking improves the quality of high-intent conversations entering Funnel 03

## Constraints

- no legal advice in networking settings unless and until the engagement
  boundary is properly crossed
- no acceptance decisions outside approved intake and ML1 controls
- no ungoverned outbound claims about specialization, outcomes, or guarantees

## Immediate Build Requirements

- define target networking venues and relationship categories
- define the capture method for networking-sourced leads
- define the follow-up sequence from networking contact to consult
- define how networking-origin leads will be tagged and reviewed
