# MSB Registration Canada Requirements Checklist

Registering as an MSB in Canada requires submitting specific information to FINTRAC and aligning that information with how the business operates.

This checklist reflects what is typically required.

## Business Information

- legal entity details
- business addresses
- nature of activities

The activity description must reflect actual transaction flows, not marketing language.

## Ownership and Control

- directors and officers
- beneficial owners
- ownership structure

Complex or international structures require clear disclosure.

## Description of Services

- types of MSB activities conducted
- how funds move through the system
- role of third parties

Ambiguity here is a common source of delay.

## Compliance Program Overview

- confirmation of AML program
- compliance officer designation
- high-level description of controls

While not fully assessed at registration, inconsistencies can create issues later.

## Supporting Information

- contact details
- operating jurisdictions
- expected business activity

## Common Issues

- incomplete ownership information
- unclear business model description
- misalignment between operations and stated activities

## The Practical Takeaway

MSB registration is a disclosure exercise.

The quality of the submission depends on how clearly the business can describe what it actually does.

Where that description is precise and aligned with operations, the process is straightforward.

Where it is not, delays and follow-up questions are common.
