# How Long Does MSB Registration Take in Canada?

MSB registration in Canada can be completed relatively quickly in straightforward cases.

In practice, timelines vary significantly depending on how well the application is prepared.

## Typical Timeline

In simple cases, registration may take approximately 2 to 4 weeks.

In more complex cases, it can extend longer due to follow-up and clarification.

## What Actually Affects Timing

The process itself is not slow. Preparation is.

Delays are typically driven by:

- unclear business activity descriptions
- incomplete ownership information
- inconsistencies in submission details

FINTRAC is reviewing for clarity and completeness, not negotiating terms.

## Business Model Clarity Matters Most

Applications that clearly describe:

- how funds move
- who controls the flow
- what services are provided

tend to move faster.

Applications that rely on high-level or marketing descriptions tend to generate follow-up.

## Internal Readiness Is the Hidden Variable

Many delays occur before submission.

Common issues include:

- uncertainty about classification
- incomplete compliance program setup
- lack of internal alignment on how the product works

These are not filing issues. They are preparation issues.

## The Practical Takeaway

MSB registration timing is not fixed.

Well-prepared applications move quickly. Poorly defined ones stall.

The difference is usually clarity, not complexity.
