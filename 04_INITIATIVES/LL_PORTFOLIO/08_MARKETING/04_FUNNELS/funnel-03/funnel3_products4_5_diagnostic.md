# Funnel 03 — Diagnostic Products

Products 4 and 5 are materially different from Entry Offers 1-3. EO1-EO3 are
direct legal engagements at $3,500-$18,000. Products 4 and 5 are low-ticket
diagnostic/info products ($49-199) used as lead qualification systems,
authority builders, and paid discovery mechanisms that feed into consultation
and larger engagement.

Strategic observation: these are probably intended less as passive info products
and more as lead qualification systems, authority builders, paid discovery
mechanisms, and consulting engagement feeders.

---

## Product 4 — FINTRAC Review

### Positioning

A low-friction regulatory diagnostic for Canadian MSBs, fintechs, payment
businesses, crypto-adjacent operators, and businesses exposed to AML/ATF
obligations.

Positioned as:
- compliance risk check
- readiness review
- remediation pre-assessment
- "before FINTRAC finds it" product

Likely converts into:
- paid remediation projects
- CAMLO engagements
- ongoing compliance retainers
- independent effectiveness review coordination

### Deliverables

**Intake Questionnaire**
- Business activities
- Products/services
- Geographic exposure
- Customer types
- Transaction methods
- Existing AML controls

**Document Review (possible)**
- AML policy
- Risk assessment
- KYC procedures
- STR/LCTR processes
- Compliance training materials
- Recordkeeping systems

**FINTRAC Gap Scan**
High-level identification of:
- missing controls
- weak procedures
- documentation deficiencies
- governance gaps
- registration exposure

**Risk Summary Report**
Short practical report:
- key risks
- likely deficiencies
- priority fixes
- operational recommendations

**Consultation Call (Core CTA)**
30-60 minute paid or credited consultation:
- review findings
- discuss exposure
- roadmap next steps
- identify remediation priorities

### Funnel Logic

Lead-gen product → diagnostic credibility → consultation → larger compliance
engagement.

Most likely upsells:
- AML program drafting
- CAMLO support
- FINTRAC registration
- remediation project
- independent review prep
- ongoing advisory

### Funnel Structure

**Traffic source:** Google Search Ads (high-intent keywords only)
- FINTRAC compliance review
- FINTRAC audit preparation
- AML compliance assessment
- fintech compliance Canada

**Step 1 — Lead Magnet Landing Page**

Goal: capture email before presenting paid product.

Offer: "FINTRAC Compliance Risk Checklist for Canadian MSBs & Fintechs"
CTA: Download Free Guide

**Step 2 — Thank You Page + Immediate Upsell**

Present low-ticket diagnostic/info product immediately on thank you page.

FINTRAC Review Toolkit:
- Pricing: TBD
- Includes:
  - compliance questionnaire
  - risk scoring worksheet
  - common FINTRAC deficiencies
  - policy checklist
  - remediation roadmap
  - optional consultation credit

CTA: "Get the Full FINTRAC Review Toolkit"

**Step 3 — Email Nurture Sequence**

Primary objective: book consultation. Not consume content indefinitely.

- Email 1: Deliver lead magnet
- Email 2: Show common mistakes
- Email 3: Case-study style scenario
- Email 4: Explain regulatory risk
- Email 5: Soft CTA: "Book Compliance Review Call"

**Step 4 — Consultation Booking**

Likely structure: paid consult credited toward engagement, or free
qualification call followed by paid assessment.

Paid consults preferred because:
- filters tire-kickers
- increases buyer seriousness
- frames expertise correctly

## Product 5 — RPAA Readiness Check

### Positioning

A regulatory readiness assessment for businesses potentially impacted by the
Retail Payment Activities Act (RPAA).

Targeting:
- fintechs
- payment facilitators
- wallet/payment operators
- embedded finance businesses

Positioned around:
- uncertainty reduction
- regulatory exposure
- operational readiness
- "does RPAA apply to us?"

### Deliverables

**Regulatory Exposure Questionnaire**
Assessment of:
- payment flows
- custody/control of funds
- user relationships
- operational structure
- third-party processor relationships

**RPAA Applicability Assessment**
High-level opinion on:
- whether business may qualify as PSP
- potential registration exposure
- operational risk areas

**Operational Compliance Review**
Review areas:
- safeguarding frameworks
- incident response
- risk management controls
- third-party dependencies
- recordkeeping

**Readiness Score / Risk Matrix**
Traffic-light style output:
- low / medium / high exposure
- immediate action items
- likely next steps

**Consultation Call (Primary Conversion Event)**
Strategic consultation covering:
- RPAA implications
- registration considerations
- operational restructuring
- compliance roadmap

### Funnel Logic

Diagnostic → founder concern/clarity → consultation → larger implementation
engagement.

Likely upsells:
- RPAA compliance roadmap
- policy drafting
- operational remediation
- registration support
- fintech advisory retainer
- outsourced compliance support
