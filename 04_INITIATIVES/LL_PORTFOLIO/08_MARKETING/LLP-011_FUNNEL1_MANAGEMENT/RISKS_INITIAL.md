# Initial Risk Assessment

Project #: LLP-26-24
Repo: 08_MARKETING/LLP-011_FUNNEL1_MANAGEMENT

## 1. Primary Risk
Reactive-acquisition drift: lead quality degrades if qualification controls are weak.

## 2. Control Risk
Unauthorized or off-strategy messaging appears in campaign assets.

## 3. Execution Risk
Asset production and QA sequencing failures reduce conversion performance.

## 4. Governance Risk
Insufficient run logging and stage classification weakens auditability.

## 5. Residual Risk
Channel volatility and external platform changes may impact conversion outcomes despite controls.
