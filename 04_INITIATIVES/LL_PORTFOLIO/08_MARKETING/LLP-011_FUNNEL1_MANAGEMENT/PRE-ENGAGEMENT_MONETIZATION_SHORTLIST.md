# Pre-Engagement Monetization Shortlist — Funnel 1

> Working notes. ML1 review and approval required before any build or deployment.

## Context

Funnel 1 audience is reactive/distressed Ontario SMB owners — not the preventative F2 ICP.
These products are calibrated to that observed reality.
Deliverable constraints: GHL + Stripe only. No new infrastructure. Build within 7–14 days.

---

## Shortlisted Products

---

### PRODUCT A — Shareholder Dispute Readiness Guide

**Status:** Shortlisted  
**Format:** PDF, 8–12 pages  
**Price point:** $9 intro / $97 regular  
**Estimated build time:** 3–4 hours

#### Concept

"What to document, protect, and stop saying when your business partnership is breaking down."

Covers:
- What to preserve immediately (communications, financial records, decisions)
- What not to do that creates personal liability or weakens your position
- What the shareholder agreement actually controls (and what it doesn't)
- When the situation requires counsel vs. when it doesn't yet
- The difference between a shareholder dispute and a breach — and why it matters

#### Target Persona

Ontario SMB owner in a deteriorating business partner relationship. Typically
2–4 year operating history. Partnership worked when things were simple;
equity, compensation, or direction is now contested. Has not yet retained
anyone. Googled "corporate lawyer" because the situation crossed a threshold.

#### Conversion Trigger

Recognition. The persona buys because the product title describes their
exact situation. At $9, the price is not the decision — the decision is
whether someone understands their problem. This product signals that ML1
does.

#### Copy Angle

"Most shareholder disputes don't start as legal problems. They start as
communication problems that become legal problems. Here's what to do — and
not do — in the early stages."

#### GHL Build

1. Landing page — no opt-in gate. Headline + 2-sentence framing + CTA.
2. Stripe order form — single product, $9.
3. Thank-you page — delivery confirmation + soft CTA to Product B or
   consult booking.
4. Email automation — immediate PDF delivery on purchase confirmed.

#### Compliance Framing

Position as a practical guide to navigating a difficult situation — not
legal advice. Explicit disclaimer: "This guide is educational. It does not
create a lawyer-client relationship and is not a substitute for legal advice
specific to your situation."

#### Upsell Path

Natural upsell to Product B (Business Structure Diagnostic) or directly to
a retained consult. A buyer of this product has demonstrated a specific, high-
urgency legal need — they are a strong consult candidate.

### PRODUCT B — Business Structure Diagnostic

**Status:** Shortlisted  
**Format:** Async written output delivered within 48 hours  
**Intro price:** $49 (regular price: $259)  
**Estimated build time:** 4–6 hours

#### Concept

Buyer submits a GHL intake form describing their corporate structure, what
has changed since incorporation, and what they are worried about. ML1
reviews and returns a 1-page written observation identifying the top 2–3
structural issues that warrant attention.

Not a legal opinion. Not a comprehensive audit. A structured, written
assessment of where to focus — calibrated to the F1 persona who knows
something is off but cannot articulate what.

This product is a standalone. It is not a gateway or teaser for the
Corporate Health Check. It answers a defined question: what structural
issues exist and what warrants attention. The upsell path is to a
consultation, not to the Health Check directly.

The output explicitly acknowledges that the issues it surfaces are the same
kind the Corporate Health Check addresses in full depth — with document
review, structured analysis, and a prioritized action plan. This creates a
natural price-tier rationale without positioning Product B as incomplete:
the Diagnostic identifies; the Health Check investigates.

#### Price Tier Rationale (to be stated in Product B output)

The Diagnostic is scoped to the information the buyer provides. It does
not involve document review, independent verification, or a structured
legal analysis across the full range of corporate exposure. Where the
Diagnostic identifies issues that warrant closer examination, the
appropriate next step depends on complexity.

For a focused question, a consultation with counsel is the most efficient
path. For a business that has grown significantly or is approaching a
capital event, the Corporate Health Check provides the document-backed,
comprehensive structural assessment the Diagnostic cannot.

#### Target Persona

Ontario SMB owner with an existing incorporated business who has a nagging
sense that their structure no longer reflects how the company actually
operates. May have added shareholders informally, changed compensation,
taken on debt, or grown headcount without updating legal documentation.
Not in active crisis — but aware enough to look.

#### Conversion Trigger

Agency. The format ("you tell us your situation, we assess it") matches the
F1 persona's mindset: they want an answer about their specific situation,
not a general pitch or a free call where they feel sold to. The $49 intro
price filters tire-kickers without requiring an established trust
relationship. The $259 regular price signals that this is a professional
service, not a commodity PDF.

#### Copy Angle

"Not sure if your business structure still fits how you actually operate?
Submit your situation. We'll identify the top 2–3 things worth your
attention. Written. Delivered in 48 hours."

#### GHL Build

1. Landing page — describe the format explicitly. Buyer knows exactly what
   they are getting before purchase. State intro price and regular price
   visibly.
2. Stripe order form — single product, $49 intro.
3. On purchase: GHL automation triggers intake form delivery to buyer +
   internal notification to ML1.
4. Intake form fields:
   - Original incorporation date and structure (shares, shareholders)
   - What has changed since incorporation
   - Current number of employees / contractors
   - What they are most worried about
   - Optional: upload articles, SHA, or key contract (GHL file upload)
5. ML1 reviews and sends written output within 48 hours (GHL email or
   direct email).
6. Output template: 1 page, 4 sections:
   - (1) What we observed
   - (2) Top issues to prioritize
   - (3) What a fuller review would examine (scope/depth rationale)
   - (4) Suggested next step — consultation or Health Check depending on complexity

#### Compliance Framing

Written output is framed as "structural observations" — not a legal opinion
and not advice on a specific legal matter. Explicit framing in the output
header: "These are general structural observations based on the information
provided. They do not constitute legal advice and do not create a lawyer-
client relationship."

ML1 controls the output entirely — no automated legal content generation.

#### Upsell Path

Primary upsell from the written output:
- Consultation with associate (appropriate for focused, bounded questions)
- Consultation with ML1 at $475 (for more complex structural matters or
  where senior judgment is required)

Secondary upsell (where output identifies broad structural complexity):
- Corporate Health Check ($2,500–$4,500) — positioned as the document-
  backed investigation of what the Diagnostic surfaced at the observation
  level.

Because ML1 has already reviewed the buyer's specific situation, consult
conversion rates should be significantly higher than cold F1 leads. The
buyer is not being pitched a service; they are being given a natural next
step that follows from what was already identified.
