## Phase 1 — Baseline Collection (Day 0-30)

PHASE: Baseline Collection  
WINDOW: 30 days  
EVALUATION DATE: 2026-02-25

Track:

- Impressions
- Engagement rate
- Lead magnet downloads
- Qualified leads
- Consult bookings
- Diagnostic purchases (Funnel 02)
- Entry offer conversions (Funnel 03)

Rules:

- No optimization decisions
- Collect clean data only

## Phase 2 — Provisional Targets (Day 30-45)

After 30 days, set provisional minimums (not permanent):

- Minimum viable CPL
- Minimum show rate
- Minimum diagnostic conversion
- Minimum entry-offer conversion

## Phase 3 — True KPI Lock (Day 60-90)

- Acceptable CAC
- Retainer conversion thresholds
- Funnel replacement decisions (Funnel 01 -> Funnel 02)
