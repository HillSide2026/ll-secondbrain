# LLP-028 CONSULTS

## Purpose

Manages the second stage of the client intake pipeline — consultation
scheduling, conduct, and conversion from prospect to retained client. Receives
qualified inquiries from LLP-027 and hands off retained clients to LLP-029.

## Scope

### In Scope

- Consultation scheduling: booking system, workflow, confirmation and reminder
  protocol
- Consultation conduct: structure, outcome categories, decision framework
- Conversion criteria: conditions defining a retained client; fee discussion
  and engagement letter workflow
- Handoff from LLP-027: what a complete incoming inquiry handoff requires
- Handoff to LLP-029: what a complete retained-client handoff delivers

### Out of Scope

- Inquiry receipt and triage — owned by LLP-027
- Client onboarding — owned by LLP-029 and LLP-004
- Matter delivery — governed by LLP-005 and LLP-006
- Fee setting and engagement letter drafting — matter-specific
