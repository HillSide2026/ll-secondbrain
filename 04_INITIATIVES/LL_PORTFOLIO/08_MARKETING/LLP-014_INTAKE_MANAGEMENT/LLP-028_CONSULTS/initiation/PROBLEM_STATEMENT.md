# Problem Statement

Project ID: LLP-028
Project Path: 04_INITIATIVES/LL_PORTFOLIO/08_MARKETING/LLP-014_INTAKE_MANAGEMENT/LLP-028_CONSULTS

The second stage of the intake pipeline — initial consultations — operates
without a governed system. There is no defined scheduling workflow, no
standard consultation structure, no documented conversion criteria, and no
formal handoff protocol connecting the inquiry stage (LLP-027) to the
onboarding stage (LLP-029).

The consequence is that consultation outcomes are not predictable or
measurable. A prospect who arrives from a qualified inquiry may book a
consultation, but whether that consultation results in a retained client
depends entirely on how the conversation goes in the moment — with no
structured framework, no defined outcome categories, and no pipeline record
of what happened or why.

As intake volume grows from F01, F02, and F03 funnel activity, an ungoverned
consultation stage becomes the bottleneck in the pipeline. Inquiries arrive;
consultations happen informally; some clients are retained; many are not; and
the firm has no systemic view of consult-to-client conversion or why it is
what it is.

This project initiates the work to govern the consultation stage.
