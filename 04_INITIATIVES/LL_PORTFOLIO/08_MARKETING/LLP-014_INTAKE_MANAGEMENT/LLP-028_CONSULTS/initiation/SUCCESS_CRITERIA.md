# Success Criteria

Project ID: LLP-028
Project Path: 04_INITIATIVES/LL_PORTFOLIO/08_MARKETING/LLP-014_INTAKE_MANAGEMENT/LLP-028_CONSULTS

This project is successful if:

- All qualified inquiries from LLP-027 are scheduled for a consultation
  within the defined booking SLA
- Every consultation has a recorded outcome (retain, decline, refer, defer,
  or other defined disposition)
- Clients who are retained proceed to onboarding via the defined handoff
  protocol to LLP-029
- Consult-to-client conversion rate is measurable and reportable to LLP-014
- No consultation outcome falls through without a recorded disposition
- The consultation stage does not create a bottleneck that accumulates
  unscheduled qualified inquiries

## Metric Dependency

Consult-to-client conversion rate thresholds and booking SLA definitions
are Planning deliverables subject to ML1 approval. These cannot be finalized
until:

1. LLP-014 metric schema (WS-3) is approved by ML1
2. F01/F02/F03 funnel metric definitions are approved by ML1

Conversion criteria and disposition categories must align with the metric
schema defined by LLP-014 (WS-3).
