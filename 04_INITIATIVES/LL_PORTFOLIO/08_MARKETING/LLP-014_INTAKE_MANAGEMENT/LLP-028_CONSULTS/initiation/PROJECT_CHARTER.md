# Project Charter

Project ID: LLP-028
Project Path: 04_INITIATIVES/LL_PORTFOLIO/08_MARKETING/LLP-014_INTAKE_MANAGEMENT/LLP-028_CONSULTS
Project Type: Intake Operations
Parent Project: LLP-014 Intake Management

Project Name: Consults

## Purpose

Govern the second stage of the client intake pipeline — consultation
scheduling, conduct, and conversion from prospect to retained client. Receives
qualified inquiries from LLP-027 (Inquiries) and hands off retained clients
to LLP-029 (Onboarding).

## Current ML1 Direction

- This project is initiated and authorized to advance to Planning.
- Consultation scheduling logic, conduct protocols, conversion criteria, and
  fee discussion frameworks are Planning deliverables and are not yet approved.
- No consultation workflow is authorized until Planning artifacts are approved
  by ML1.

## In Scope

- Consultation scheduling: booking system, scheduling workflow, confirmation
  and reminder protocol
- Consultation conduct: structure of the initial consultation, what is
  covered, what outcome is determined (retain, decline, refer, defer)
- Conversion criteria: what conditions define a retained client; what fee
  discussion and engagement letter workflow follows a decision to retain
- Handoff protocol from LLP-027: what a complete incoming handoff looks like;
  what LLP-028 requires to accept an inquiry
- Handoff protocol to LLP-029: what a complete outgoing handoff looks like;
  what information LLP-029 requires to begin onboarding

## Out of Scope

- Inquiry receipt and triage — owned by LLP-027
- Client onboarding — marketing or operational side (LLP-029, LLP-004)
- Matter delivery — governed by LLP-005 and LLP-006
- Fee setting and engagement letter drafting — matter-specific; not a
  pipeline operations function

## Authority

ML1 is the sole authority on consultation structure, conversion criteria,
fee discussion frameworks, and retention decision logic.
