# Success Criteria

Project ID: LLP-029
Project Path: 04_INITIATIVES/LL_PORTFOLIO/08_MARKETING/LLP-014_INTAKE_MANAGEMENT/LLP-029_ONBOARDING

This project is successful if:

- Every retained client receives a defined welcome communication within the
  specified time window after signed retainer
- Every retained client receives expectation-setting materials that cover
  matter process, timeline framing, and communication protocol
- The onboarding experience sequence is documented, repeatable, and does not
  depend on ML1 remembering to take any manual step
- The scope boundary between LLP-029 (marketing-side) and LLP-004
  (operational-side) is defined, agreed, and documented
- Handoff from LLP-028 is received via the defined protocol; no client
  arrives at onboarding without the context package from LLP-028
- Handoff to LLP-004 is delivered via the defined protocol with all required
  information
- Client onboarding satisfaction is measurable (at minimum: retention of
  clients through matter activation tracked as a proxy)

## Metric Dependency

Onboarding SLA definitions and client satisfaction metrics are Planning
deliverables subject to ML1 approval. These cannot be finalized until
LLP-014 metric schema (WS-3) is approved by ML1.
