# Project Charter

Project ID: LLP-029
Project Path: 04_INITIATIVES/LL_PORTFOLIO/08_MARKETING/LLP-014_INTAKE_MANAGEMENT/LLP-029_ONBOARDING
Project Type: Intake Operations
Parent Project: LLP-014 Intake Management
Operational Counterpart: LLP-004 Onboarding

Project Name: Onboarding (Marketing)

## Purpose

Govern the marketing side of client onboarding — the client experience,
communications, and materials from signed retainer through to matter
activation. This project covers the client-facing and relationship dimensions.
The operational counterpart (LLP-004) covers matter setup, Clio provisioning,
and intake forms.

## Current ML1 Direction

- This project is initiated and authorized to advance to Planning.
- The scope boundary between LLP-029 (marketing-side) and LLP-004
  (operational-side) is a Planning deliverable and must be confirmed before
  either project can finalize its onboarding scope.
- No onboarding communications, welcome materials, or client-facing workflows
  are authorized until Planning artifacts are approved by ML1.

## In Scope

- Welcome communications: first contact after signed retainer, what is said
  and when
- Expectation-setting materials: what a new client is told about the matter
  process, timelines, and communication protocol
- Onboarding experience design: the sequence and tone of touchpoints from
  signed retainer through matter activation
- Handoff from LLP-028: what a complete incoming retained-client handoff
  requires
- Handoff to LLP-004 (Operational Onboarding): what this project delivers
  to LLP-004 and when; scope boundary to be confirmed in Planning

## Out of Scope

- Matter setup, Clio provisioning, intake forms — owned by LLP-004
- Consultation and conversion — owned by LLP-028
- Matter delivery and ongoing client management — governed by LLP-005,
  LLP-006
- Engagement letter drafting — matter-specific

## Authority

ML1 is the sole authority on client communication content, experience design,
and any scope decisions at the LLP-029 / LLP-004 boundary.
