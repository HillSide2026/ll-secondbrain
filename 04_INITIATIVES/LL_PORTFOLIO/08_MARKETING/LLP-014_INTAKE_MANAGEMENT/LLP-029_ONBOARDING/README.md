# LLP-029 ONBOARDING (Marketing)

## Purpose

Manages the marketing side of client onboarding — the experience,
communications, and materials a new client encounters from signed retainer
through to matter activation. Covers the client-facing and relationship
dimensions of onboarding.

## Scope

### In Scope

- Welcome communications: first contact after signed retainer
- Expectation-setting materials: matter process, timeline framing,
  communication protocol
- Onboarding experience sequence: touchpoints from signed retainer through
  matter activation
- Handoff from LLP-028: what a complete incoming retained-client handoff
  requires
- Handoff to LLP-004: scope boundary and transfer protocol (defined in
  Planning in coordination with LLP-004)

### Out of Scope

- Matter setup, Clio provisioning, intake forms — owned by LLP-004
- Consultation and conversion — owned by LLP-028
- Matter delivery — governed by LLP-005 and LLP-006
- Engagement letter drafting — matter-specific

## Relationship to LLP-004

> **Operational counterpart: [LLP-004 ONBOARDING](../../../../03_FIRM_OPERATIONS/LLP-004_ONBOARDING/)**
>
> LLP-029 covers the **marketing and client experience side** of onboarding
> (welcome communications, expectation-setting, relationship framing). LLP-004
> covers the **operational side** (matter setup, Clio provisioning, intake
> forms, file opening). The scope boundary between these two projects is a
> Planning deliverable — it must be agreed before either project designs its
> onboarding workflow.
