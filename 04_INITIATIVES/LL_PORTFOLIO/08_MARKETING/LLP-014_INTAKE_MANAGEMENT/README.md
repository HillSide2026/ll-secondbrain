# LLP-014 INTAKE MANAGEMENT

## Purpose

Governs the full client intake pipeline from first inquiry through to onboarding. Manages and coordinates the three intake-stage subprojects: Inquiries, Consults, and Onboarding.

## Scope

### In Scope
- Inquiry handling and triage (LLP-027)
- Consultation management and conversion (LLP-028)
- Client onboarding — marketing side (LLP-029)
- Pipeline integrity and stage-gate compliance across all three stages

### Out of Scope
- Lead capture (upstream — see LLP-026)
- Operational client onboarding — matter setup, Clio provisioning (see LLP-004)
- Funnel management (see LLP-011, LLP-012, LLP-013)

## Subprojects

| Project | Description |
|---|---|
