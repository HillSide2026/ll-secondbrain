# Success Criteria

Project ID: LLP-027
Project Path: 04_INITIATIVES/LL_PORTFOLIO/08_MARKETING/LLP-014_INTAKE_MANAGEMENT/LLP-027_INQUIRIES

This project is successful if:

- All incoming inquiries are logged within the defined time window, regardless
  of channel
- Each logged inquiry is triaged and assigned a practice area and urgency
  classification
- Initial response is sent within the defined SLA for each urgency tier
- Inquiries that qualify for consultation proceed via the defined handoff
  protocol to LLP-028
- Inquiries that do not qualify are dispositioned with a recorded outcome
- Inquiry volume, triage outcomes, and response SLA compliance are measurable
  without manual reconciliation
- No inquiry falls through without a recorded disposition

## Metric Dependency

Specific SLA targets, triage category definitions, and inquiry volume
benchmarks are Planning deliverables. Inquiry segmentation by source funnel
must align with the event and conversion definitions used in F01 (LLP-011),
F02 (LLP-012), and F03 (LLP-013). Planning must not define SLA tiers or
triage thresholds before funnel-level metric definitions are approved by ML1,
as the funnel metrics govern what a qualified inquiry looks like at each
funnel's conversion point.
