# Project Charter

Project ID: LLP-014
Project Path: 04_INITIATIVES/LL_PORTFOLIO/08_MARKETING/LLP-014_INTAKE_MANAGEMENT
Project Type: Marketing Operations — Pipeline Coordination

Project Name: Intake Management

## Purpose

Govern the full client intake pipeline from first inquiry through to
onboarding. Coordinate and enforce pipeline integrity across three
subprojects: LLP-027 (Inquiries), LLP-028 (Consults), and LLP-029
(Onboarding).

## Current ML1 Direction

- This project is initiated and authorized to advance to Planning.
- Stage handoff protocols, conversion metrics, and pipeline reporting
  are Planning deliverables and are not yet approved.
- No pipeline configuration or subproject coordination protocol is
  authorized until Planning artifacts are approved by ML1.

## In Scope

- Pipeline governance: stage definitions, handoff protocols, and stage-gate
  compliance across all three intake stages
- Cross-stage reporting: inquiry-to-consult and consult-to-client conversion
  metrics; pipeline health reporting to ML1
- Coordination between LLP-027, LLP-028, and LLP-029 — ensuring each
  subproject operates within its defined scope boundaries
- Pipeline integrity: ensuring no lead falls out between stages without a
  recorded disposition

## Out of Scope

- Lead capture infrastructure (LLP-026)
- Funnel content production and SEO (LLP-011, LLP-012, LLP-013)
- Operational client onboarding — matter setup, Clio provisioning (LLP-004)
- Individual subproject execution (owned by LLP-027, LLP-028, LLP-029
  respectively)

## Authority

ML1 is the sole authority on pipeline design, stage gate definitions,
handoff protocols, and conversion targets.
