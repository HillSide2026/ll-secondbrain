# Stakeholders

Project ID: LLP-014
Project Path: 04_INITIATIVES/LL_PORTFOLIO/08_MARKETING/LLP-014_INTAKE_MANAGEMENT

## Core Stakeholders

- ML1: decision authority on pipeline design, stage gate definitions,
  handoff protocols, and conversion targets; primary consumer of pipeline
  health reporting
- ML2 / System: maintains pipeline coordination infrastructure, cross-stage
  reporting, and stage-gate compliance monitoring

## Secondary Stakeholders

- LLP-026 (Lead Capture): upstream — feeds inquiry volume into the pipeline;
  routing from lead capture must align with LLP-027 intake requirements
- LLP-027 (Inquiries): first intake stage; operates under governance defined
  by this project
- LLP-028 (Consults): second intake stage; operates under governance defined
  by this project
- LLP-029 (Onboarding): third intake stage; operates under governance defined
  by this project
- LLP-004 (Operational Onboarding): downstream — receives clients from
  LLP-029; the boundary between marketing-side and operational onboarding must
  be defined in coordination with LLP-004
