# Success Criteria

Project ID: LLP-014
Project Path: 04_INITIATIVES/LL_PORTFOLIO/08_MARKETING/LLP-014_INTAKE_MANAGEMENT

This project is successful if:

- Stage handoff protocols are defined and operating between all three
  subprojects (LLP-027 → LLP-028 → LLP-029)
- Cross-stage conversion metrics (inquiry-to-consult, consult-to-client) are
  tracked and reportable without manual reconciliation, using metric
  definitions aligned with the upstream funnel metric schemas
- No lead falls out of the pipeline without a recorded disposition at the
  stage where it stopped
- LLP-027, LLP-028, and LLP-029 each operate within their defined scope
  boundaries without overlap or gap
- Pipeline health is visible to ML1 in a single view that does not require
  reading three separate subproject reports
- The coordination layer does not execute what belongs to the subprojects —
  it governs and reports only
