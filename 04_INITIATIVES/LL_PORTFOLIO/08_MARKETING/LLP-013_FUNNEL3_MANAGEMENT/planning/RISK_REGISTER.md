## Escalation Conditions

Any of the following require immediate ML1 review before proceeding:

- LinkedIn Ads CPL exceeds 2× the approved threshold after 45 days (R-02).
- Total active matter count exceeds 15 within 30 days of F3 channel launch (R-03).
- SE-01 rejects website or content copy (<0.60 composite score) and revision takes more than 10 business days (R-04).
- A complaint or regulatory body inquiry arises related to F3 content (R-08).
