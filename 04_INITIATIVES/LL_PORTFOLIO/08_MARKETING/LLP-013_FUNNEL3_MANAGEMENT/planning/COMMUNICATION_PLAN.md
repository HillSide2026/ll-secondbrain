## Approval Routing

| Artifact Type | Routing |
|---------------|---------|

## Escalation Path

All escalation goes directly to ML1. There is no intermediate approval layer.

Escalation triggers (from RISK_REGISTER.md):
- LinkedIn Ads CPL > 2× approved threshold after 45 days
- Active matter count > 15 within 30 days of F3 launch
- SE-01 rejects content and revision takes >10 business days
- Compliance inquiry related to F3 content

- `PROJECT_HEALTH_ROLLUP.md` — generated by LLM-004 at each management cycle
- `PORTFOLIO_STATUS_DASHBOARD.md` — generated by LLM-005
