# Risk Scan

Project ID: LLP-013
Project Path: 08_MARKETING/LLP-013_FUNNEL3_MANAGEMENT
Stage: Initiating

## Top 5 Risks
1. Regulatory-positioning risk: messaging drifts beyond the approved payments / MSB / PSP / RPAA counsel boundary.
2. Offer-architecture risk: entry offers and core offers drift out of alignment across funnel assets and management artifacts.
3. Business-development control risk: networking-led acquisition generates low-signal activity without disciplined targeting, capture, and follow-up.
4. Attribution risk: conversion and revenue evidence remain too weak to support confident optimization decisions.
5. Governance risk: draft planning materials are mistaken for planning authorization or live execution approval.

## Key Assumptions
- Funnel 03 can sustain a coherent regulated-market position across payments, MSB, PSP, RPAA, and AML-adjacent surfaces.
- Networking and content can be governed as one acquisition system instead of separate motions.
- The canonical offer map can remain stable long enough to support controlled testing and reporting.
- Stronger attribution and QA controls can be designed before any planning-stage promotion.

## Go / No-Go Judgment
Decision: Proceed with Conditions
Rationale: Funnel 03 should proceed through the initiation stage only. Planning promotion, optimization changes, and live execution adjustments remain conditional on ML1 approval and a completed planning packet.
