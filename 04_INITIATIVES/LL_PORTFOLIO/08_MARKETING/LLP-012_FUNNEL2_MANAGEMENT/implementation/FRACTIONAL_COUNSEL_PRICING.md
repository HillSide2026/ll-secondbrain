# Fractional Counsel Pricing Model — Funnel 2

## Current Model

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Model | Monthly retainer |
| Range | $2,000–$8,000 CAD/month |
| Status | Crude — entry-level model; tiers and scope not yet defined |

## Notes

- Range is indicative. Specific tiers, scope definitions, and engagement
  terms are not yet defined.
- Retainer pricing reflects the F02 ICP: Ontario businesses with $1M–$8M
  annual cash flow; accountant-referred; willing to pay for ongoing
  commercial legal relationship.
- Floor ($2,000/month) is the minimum engagement for a recurring fractional
  counsel relationship.
- Ceiling ($8,000/month) reflects the upper range of what the F02 ICP can
  sustain without friction.
- Stripe products for fractional counsel retainer tiers are not yet created.
  Tier structure and scope definitions required before Stripe configuration.

## Relationship to Entry Offer

The fractional counsel retainer is the downstream revenue product that the
Corporate Health Check (entry offer) feeds. A client who completes the
Health Check and has identified remediation needs is a natural candidate
for an ongoing retainer relationship.
