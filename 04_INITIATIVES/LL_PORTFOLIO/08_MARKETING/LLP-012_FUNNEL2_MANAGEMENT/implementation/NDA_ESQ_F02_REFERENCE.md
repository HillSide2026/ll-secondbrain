# NDA Esq — F02 Reference

## What This Is

NDA Esq is a Canada-focused, AI-powered NDA generator that surfaces as an
offer within Funnel 2. It is governed as a standalone project under LLP-024.
This file records the F02-specific context only.

Canonical project and offer model:
`04_INITIATIVES/LL_PORTFOLIO/07_GROWTH_PROJECTS/LLP-024_NDA_ESQ/implementation/OFFER_MODEL.md`

## F02 Offer Role

Within Funnel 2, NDA Esq is relevant only for the subset of SMB users who also
meet the F02 qualification criteria. The broader MVP testing surface for NDA
Esq should not be treated as identical to the F02 ICP.

| Tier | Who | Access |
|------|-----|--------|
| Client | Levine Law retained clients | Lifetime free |
| Non-client | Any user | One use free, then paid (price TBD) |

NDA Esq functions as a lead capture mechanism within F02. The F02 ICP
(Ontario $1M–$8M operating businesses) has recurring NDA and commercial
agreement needs. A non-client who uses NDA Esq is a candidate for:

- Corporate Governance Triage Consult ($859 CAD list)
- Corporate Health Check (entry offer, price TBD)
- Fractional counsel retainer ($2,000–$8,000 CAD/month)

## Scope Boundary

NDA Esq is a standalone product — it does not collapse into F02 and is
governed separately under LLP-024. Changes to NDA Esq offer structure,
pricing, or access model must be made at the LLP-024 level, not here.
