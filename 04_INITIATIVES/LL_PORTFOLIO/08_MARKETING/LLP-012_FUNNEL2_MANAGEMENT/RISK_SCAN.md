1. Positioning-transition risk: Funnel 02 fails to attract the intended growth-stage corporate-law prospect profile.
2. Domain-migration risk: moving the acquisition surface from `levinelegal.ca` to `levine-law.ca` creates confusion, leakage, or broken routing.
3. Traffic-economics risk: higher-cost Google Ads traffic improves fit but does not produce acceptable downstream conversion economics.
4. Intake-control risk: inconsistent reporting, weak setter design, or unclear senior-lawyer handoff reduces speed-to-contact and intake quality.
5. Governance risk: planning or launch behavior begins before ML1 approves `Initiating -> Planning`.

## Key Assumptions
- `levine-law.ca` can become the controlled primary acquisition surface for Funnel 02.
- Higher-quality paid traffic can justify higher acquisition cost if fit and conversion improve.
- NDA Esq overlap can supplement acquisition without collapsing project boundaries.
- Setter and senior-lawyer operating design can be defined before any live launch decision.

## Go / No-Go Judgment

Rationale: Funnel 02 should proceed through the initiation stage only. Planning promotion, launch preparation, and live execution remain conditional on ML1 approval and a completed planning packet.
