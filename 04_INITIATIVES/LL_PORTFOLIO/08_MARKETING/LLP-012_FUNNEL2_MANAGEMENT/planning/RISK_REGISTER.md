## Risk Table

| ID | Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Severity | Mitigation | Owner |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

## Escalation Conditions

Immediate ML1 review is required if any of the following occur:

- referral activity remains too weak to justify the primary-channel decision
- low-value or poor-fit matters are repeatedly reaching ML1 despite the gate
- the paid-diagnostic framing is being diluted in live copy or scripts
- F02 routing starts attracting clearly regulatory/F03 inquiries at scale
- the project cannot show evidence for the current conversion path or pricing logic
