# Success Criteria

Project ID: LLP-012
Project Path: 08_MARKETING/LLP-012_FUNNEL2_MANAGEMENT

The project will be successful if:

1. Funnel 02 scope, authority, and launch boundaries are explicitly documented.
2. Entry-offer model and qualification gates are operationally defined.
3. Relocation from `levinelegal.ca` to `levine-law.ca` is defined as a controlled Funnel 02 transition.
4. Higher-quality Google Ads traffic assumptions are defined and approved, including acceptance of higher acquisition cost for better-fit leads where justified.
5. Authority-content / SEO production is defined as a governed acquisition lane.
6. NDA Esq overlap rules are explicit: Funnel 02 may acquire traffic and leads through the NDA Esq landing page on `levine-law.ca` without collapsing project boundaries.
7. Associate listing requirements for website and landing page are defined and approved.
8. Intake reporting is consistent enough to support weekly decision use.
9. Intake-stage KPI targets are implemented in metric doctrine:
   - `>= 90%` leads worked within 24 hours of opt-in
   - `>= 30%` leads scheduled
10. Conversion-stage KPI targets are implemented in metric doctrine:
   - Consulting stage events are captured and reportable
   - Closing/Engaging (Conversion) achieves `>= 1/10` paid leads (`>= 10%`)
11. A setter role and handoff model are defined.
12. Senior-lawyer staffing and legal-delivery readiness are defined as part of the Funnel 02 operating design.
13. Conversion evidence and reporting requirements are defined for governed runs, with explicit handoff reference into fulfillment onboarding.
14. ML1 approves promotion to Planning with clear launch-readiness criteria.
