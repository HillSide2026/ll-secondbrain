# Initial Risk Assessment

Project #: LLP-26-25
Repo: 08_MARKETING/LLP-012_FUNNEL2_MANAGEMENT

## 1. Primary Risk
Positioning-transition risk: preventative funnel fails to attract intended maturity profile.

## 2. Control Risk
Launch pressure bypasses qualification gates or asset QA controls.

## 3. Execution Risk
Required documents and intake readiness criteria create friction that reduces conversion.

## 4. Governance Risk
Funnel deployed before explicit ML1 go/no-go approval.

## 5. Residual Risk
Longer adoption curve versus reactive funnel may temporarily reduce lead volume.
