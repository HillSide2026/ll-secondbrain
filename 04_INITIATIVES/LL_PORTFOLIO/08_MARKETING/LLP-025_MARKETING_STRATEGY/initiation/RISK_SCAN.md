# Risk Scan

Project ID: LLP-26-25
Project Path: 08_MARKETING/LLP-025_MARKETING_STRATEGY
Stage: Initiating

## Top Risks

1. **Strategic drift**: Without a governed strategy project, positioning erodes incrementally as individual funnel decisions optimize locally — the firm's differentiated identity dilutes over time without a coherence authority.
2. **ICP-01 channel gap persists**: No acquisition channel currently reaches mature Ontario operators. If the channel strategy problem is not resolved at the planning stage, F02 launches into an undefined distribution path and generates no qualified intake.
3. **F02 remains unbuilt indefinitely**: The Corporate Health Check entry offer has no defined owner, scope, or deadline. Without a project milestone forcing build and launch, F01 continues as the de facto permanent channel.
4. **KPI targets never formalized**: Baseline data exists but Phase 2 and Phase 3 targets have not been set. Without a project deadline, the firm operates without defined success thresholds indefinitely.
5. **Agent architecture drift**: Six agents are deployed or specified without a governing strategy project. Without a coherence anchor, agents optimize locally, generate contradictory outputs, or drift from approved positioning.

## Key Assumptions

- Baseline KPI data (30+ days) is available and usable for provisional target-setting.
- ML1 has sufficient capacity to review and approve planning artifacts and KPI targets within the planning window.
- The Corporate Health Check product concept is sufficiently defined to move to build — scope is not starting from zero.
- Funnel 01 remains active and continues to generate some qualified intake during the planning stage.
- The payments vertical (F03) remains active and continues to develop during the planning stage.

## Go / No-Go Judgment

Decision: Proceed
Rationale: Initiation artifacts are complete and internally consistent. The core problem (ICP-01 channel gap, F02 unbuilt) is clearly defined. The risk of inaction exceeds the risk of proceeding. Planning stage is authorized.
