# LLP-025 — Success Criteria

## Project-Level Success

LLP-025 succeeds when:

1. **KPI baseline is locked** — formal KPI targets are set at the Day 60–90 milestone, reviewed and approved by ML1
2. **F01 → F02 transition plan is defined** — with explicit trigger conditions, milestones, and an ML1-approved wind-down decision framework
3. **Payments vertical program is active** — Funnel 03 has a governed authority-building program with defined content cadence and conversion targets
4. **Agent architecture is governed** — all six marketing agents operate under approved doctrine, with Strategic Editor reviews occurring at a defined cadence
5. **ICP intake quality improves** — intake skews toward maturity-gated, document-ready, structural engagements vs. crisis-driven matters

## Strategic Transition Milestone

The primary strategic milestone is the **F01 → F02 handoff decision**, defined as:

- F02 generates a defined minimum volume of qualified diagnostics per period (target TBD at KPI lock)
- F02 retainer conversion rate meets or exceeds F01 retention rate
- F01 ad spend can be reduced without qualified intake falling below minimum

## What Does NOT Constitute Success

- Increased raw lead volume without ICP quality filter
- High conversion rates from crisis-driven or pre-revenue startup inquiries
- Content output without Strategic Editor review
- KPI targets set before 30-day baseline is complete
