# LLP-025 — Project Charter

## Purpose

Govern Levine Law's marketing strategy as a managed project — maintaining positioning, objectives, goals, and strategic doctrine; owning the transition from reactive to preventative acquisition; and anchoring the marketing agent architecture to approved direction.

## Target Client Profile (ICP)

**ICP-01 — Ontario Operating Company**
- Revenue: $1M–$8M
- Employees: 5–30
- Operating: 2+ years
- Has an accountant involved
- Document-ready; not in active crisis

**ICP-02 — Fintech / Payments Operator**
- Canada exposure
- Commercially minded; structural clarity preference
- Regulatory awareness; decision-maker access

## Market Categories

| Category | Description |
|---|---|
| Primary | Fractional Counsel / Legal Function as a Service (Ontario SMB operators) |
| Secondary | Payments / MSB / PSP Regulatory Counsel (Canada exposure) |

## Differentiation Thesis

- Maturity-gated intake (not crisis intake)
- Structural review and governance orientation
- Regulatory systems literacy (payments/MSB/PSP)
- Operator-aligned counsel (not academic, not alarmist)
- Productized entry points as fit filter (Corporate Health Check, MSB registration, AML health check)

## Marketing Objectives

| ID | Objective | Entry Vehicle | Target Service |
|---|---|---|---|
| OBJ-01 | Acquire qualified Ontario operator leads | Fractional Counsel consult | Fractional Counsel / Corporate |
| OBJ-02 | Shift from reactive to preventative entry point | Paid Corporate Health Check | Health Check → Remediation → Fractional Counsel |
| OBJ-03 | Establish regulatory authority in payments/MSB | MSB/AML entry services | Ongoing AML Counsel Retainer |

## Funnel Portfolio

| Funnel | Channel | Entry Offer | Core Offer | Strategic Role |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| F01 | Google Ads (reactive) | Fractional Counsel consult | Ongoing retainer | Sustains qualified intake during F02 build |
| F02 | Content + lead magnets | Paid Corporate Health Check | Remediation + Fractional Counsel | Intended primary corporate acquisition path |
| F03 | Authority content | MSB/AML entry services | Ongoing AML Counsel | Vertical authority and payments conversion |

**Transition Thesis**: F01 is temporary. F02 is the structural replacement. The trigger for F01 wind-down is defined by KPI targets (CAC, retainer conversion) established at Day 60–90 baseline lock.

## Governing Constraints

- **Capacity**: Maturity-gated, qualified intake only. Do not optimize for volume at the cost of ICP quality.
- **Compliance**: Marketing does not provide legal advice or accept matters autonomously.
- **Tone**: Calm authority. Preventative. Operator-aligned. Not alarmist, not academic.
- **Governance**: No new funnel may be created without defined ICP, entry offer, measurable KPIs, and upstream objective mapping.

## Scope

### In Scope
- Maintenance and evolution of positioning, objectives, goals, and strategy artifacts
- F01 → F02 transition plan and milestone governance
- Payments vertical authority-building program
- KPI target formalization cadence
- Marketing agent architecture governance
- Governance of all new funnel creation

### Out of Scope
- Funnel-specific execution (owned by LLP-011, LLP-012, LLP-013)
- Lead capture operations (LLP-026)
- Intake pipeline management (LLP-014)

## Agent Architecture

Five agents operate under this strategy, governed by the Strategic Editor as coherence authority:

| Agent | Role | Activation Phase |
|---|---|---|
| Selective Provocation Engine | Tension generator / top-of-funnel attention | Phase 1 (Month 1) |
| Blog & SEO Engine | Authority builder / long-form content | Phase 1 (Month 1) |
| SEO & Metrics Master | Intelligence layer / performance monitoring | Phase 2 (Month 2) |
| Conversion Architect | Economic optimizer / conversion improvement | Phase 3 (Month 3) |
| Strategic Editor | Coherence authority / final gate (all phases) | Day 1 (lightweight → full) |

**Authority hierarchy**: Strategic Editor > Positioning > ICP quality > metrics/volume.

## KPI Cadence

| Phase | Window | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Phase 1 — Baseline Collection | Day 0–30 | Track only. No optimization. |
| Phase 2 — Provisional Targets | Day 30–45 | Set provisional minimums (CPL, show rate, diagnostic conversion) |
| Phase 3 — KPI Lock | Day 60–90 | Define CAC, retainer conversion thresholds, F01→F02 replacement decision |
