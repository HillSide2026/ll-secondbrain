## OQ-02 — SEO Keyword Direction: Exits vs Structuring

**Question**: Which SEO keyword cluster should LL pursue to reach ICP-02 operators — Exits or Structuring?

**Context**: Current keyword is "toronto corporate lawyer" (reactive, undifferentiated). Two alternative clusters have been proposed:

| Cluster | Keywords | ICP Signal |
|---|---|---|
| **Exits** | "M&A lawyer Toronto", "Business succession planning" | Operators thinking about exit, sale, or ownership transition |
| **Structuring** | "Executive compensation", "Corporate governance" | Operators focused on internal structure, governance, and compliance |

**Considerations**:
- Exits cluster signals a specific transaction event — high intent, but episodic (not recurring). May attract one-time engagements rather than ongoing retainers.
- Structuring cluster signals ongoing operational maturity — more aligned with preventative/fractional counsel positioning. Lower event-driven urgency but higher ICP fit.
- Both clusters are more differentiated than "toronto corporate lawyer" and filter for operator maturity vs. crisis.
- May not be mutually exclusive long-term; question is which to prioritize first.

**Research needed**: Search volume, competition level, and conversion relevance for both clusters. Alignment with F02 entry offer framing (see OQ-03).

**Decision needed**: ML1 selects Exits or Structuring as primary SEO direction (or defines a third path).

**Status**: Open

## OQ-03 — F02 Entry Offer Framing

**Question**: What is the right name and framing for the F02 entry offer?

**Context**: The F02 entry offer is currently referred to internally as the "Corporate Health Check." Alternative framings proposed:

| Framing | Positioning Signal | ICP Interpretation |
|---|---|---|
| **Corporate Health Check** | Clinical, diagnostic, systems-oriented | "I'll review what you have and tell you what needs fixing" |
| **Premium Consultation** | High-touch, advisory, relationship-led | "A serious paid session with a senior lawyer" |
| **Strategic Consultation** | Counsel-as-advisor, business-oriented | "I'll think through your legal situation strategically with you" |

**Considerations**:
- "Corporate Health Check" is most differentiated — signals a defined, repeatable product. Aligns with preventative/structural positioning. May be unfamiliar to buyers who need to be educated on the concept.
- "Premium Consultation" is familiar but risks being interpreted as a higher-priced version of a free consult — not structurally different enough.
- "Strategic Consultation" is a middle path — familiar enough, but signals seniority and business orientation. Could work better in an Exits context where operators are solving a specific high-stakes problem.
- Framing choice should be tested against the SEO direction decision (OQ-02): Structuring may pair best with "Health Check" framing; Exits may pair better with "Strategic Consultation."

**Decision needed**: ML1 selects entry offer framing, or authorizes a naming test.

**Status**: Open
