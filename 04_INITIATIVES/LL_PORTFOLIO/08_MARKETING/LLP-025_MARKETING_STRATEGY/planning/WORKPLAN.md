### WS-01 Open Decisions

**OQ-01 — ICP-02 Channel Hypothesis**
How does the firm reach mature Ontario operating companies (more than $1M revenue, maturity-gated)? This population is not search-intent-driven. Candidate channels:
- Accountant/advisor referral network — operators already have accountants; accountant is the gatekeeper
- LinkedIn — founder/operator audience; relationship and content-driven
- Targeted SEO on operational problems (search volume and competition TBD — see OQ-02)
- Paid diagnostics distributed through trusted professional channels (accountant co-marketing, bar association)

*Decision required before F02 distribution architecture can be defined.*

**OQ-02 — SEO Keyword Direction: Exits vs Structuring**
Current keyword: "toronto corporate lawyer" (reactive, undifferentiated). Two alternative clusters proposed:

| Cluster | Keywords | ICP Signal | Strategic Fit |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Exits** | "M&A lawyer Toronto", "Business succession planning" | Operators approaching sale, transition, or ownership change | High intent, episodic — may attract one-time engagements over retainers |
| **Structuring** | "Executive compensation", "Corporate governance" | Operators focused on internal structure, oversight, compliance | Recurring maturity signal — higher alignment with preventative/fractional counsel positioning |

Research needed: search volume, competition, and conversion relevance for both clusters.
*Decision required. Structuring cluster is hypothesized to be higher ICP-02 fit. Exits cluster may complement the F03 payments audience secondarily.*

### WS-02 Open Decisions

**OQ-03 — F02 Entry Offer Framing**
The F02 entry offer is internally called "Corporate Health Check." Alternative framings under consideration:

| Framing | Positioning Signal | Risk |
|---|---|---|
| **Corporate Health Check** | Defined, repeatable diagnostic product; structural, preventative | May require buyer education on the concept |
| **Premium Consultation** | High-touch paid session with senior lawyer | Reads as an expensive version of a free consult — insufficient differentiation |
| **Strategic Consultation** | Advisor-mode counsel, business-oriented | Works better in an Exits context; less aligned with structural/governance framing |

*Decision required. "Corporate Health Check" is preferred hypothesis given preventative positioning. Confirm or select alternative before offer build begins.*

**Note**: OQ-02 and OQ-03 are linked — the keyword cluster selected in OQ-02 should directly inform the framing chosen in OQ-03.

## Implementation Stage — Adopted Priorities (from LLM-001 COS Review, 2026-03-17)

These tasks are adopted as the execution agenda for the implementing stage and are reflected in the updated scope of LLP-012_FUNNEL2_MANAGEMENT.

### IMP-01 — Build Accountant Referral Network (Primary ICP-02 Channel)

**Decision adopted**: Accountant referral network is the primary ICP-02 acquisition channel. LinkedIn is secondary. SEO is not the primary ICP-02 channel.

**Rationale**: ICP-02 operators (more than $1M revenue, accountant involved) do not search for lawyers. Their accountant is the gatekeeper. A productized Corporate Health Check is far easier for an accountant to refer than an open-ended retainer.

Tasks:
- Identify 10–15 Ontario accounting firms serving operating companies above $1M revenue (mid-market CA firms, not Big Four)
- Initiate direct outreach to 2–3 partners per firm
- Define the referral mechanic: what the accountant says, what the client receives, how the handoff works
- Tie the referral offer to F02 — the Health Check is the referral product, not a general "call my lawyer" invitation
- LinkedIn: do not launch content program until referral architecture has at least 3 active relationships

### IMP-02 — F02 Minimum Viable Build

**Decision adopted**: F02 is built around the Corporate Health Check, distributed through the accountant referral network. Google Ads is not the primary F02 acquisition channel.

Tasks (in sequence — each step is blocked until prior step is resolved):
1. **ML1 defines Health Check scope**: What gets reviewed, what does the client receive (written memo, scored framework, or verbal debrief), how long does it take ML1 to deliver
2. **ML1 sets price**: Target range $1,500–$2,500. Required before landing page build
3. **ML1 defines monthly delivery capacity ceiling**: How many Health Checks per month without capacity risk. This gates F02 volume targets
4. **Build landing page**: Single page on levine-law.ca with GHL paid booking form
5. **Activate ICP-02 intake gate**: Three pre-booking questions (see IMP-03)
6. **Activate distribution**: First 3–5 accountant referral relationships from IMP-01

### IMP-03 — F01 Intake Gate Enforcement (Immediate — Does Not Wait for Planning Gate)

**Decision adopted**: Low-value matter problem is a filtering failure. The GHL qualification questionnaire already exists but is not being sent consistently. Fix is enforcement, not creation.

Tasks:
- Audit GHL intake flow to identify where the questionnaire is being skipped or bypassed
- Hardwire the qualification questions into the opt-in / booking button on the landing page so they cannot be bypassed (see LLP-011 Stage 2 scope)
- Confirm disqualification thresholds are defined and active: revenue < $1M, employees < 5, no accountant = do not proceed to consult
- **This is a configuration task, not a build task. Implement immediately.**

### IMP-04 — Capacity Constraints (ML1 Decisions Required Before F02 Launch)

F02 success without capacity limits will create a capacity crisis. Three decisions required from ML1:

| Decision | Question | COS Starting Point |

| Matter value floor | Minimum projected matter value for new corporate work | $5,000+ projected value |
