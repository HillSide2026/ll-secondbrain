# LLP-025 MARKETING STRATEGY

## Purpose

Consolidated project governing Levine Law's market positioning, objectives, goals, and overall marketing strategy. Provides the foundational direction that downstream marketing projects (funnels, lead capture, intake) operate within.

## Scope

### In Scope
- Market positioning and differentiation
- Marketing objectives and KPI framework
- Annual goals and baseline measurement
- Overall marketing strategy and agent architecture

### Out of Scope
- Funnel-specific execution (see LLP-011, LLP-012, LLP-013)
- Lead capture operations (see LLP-026)
- Intake pipeline management (see LLP-014)
