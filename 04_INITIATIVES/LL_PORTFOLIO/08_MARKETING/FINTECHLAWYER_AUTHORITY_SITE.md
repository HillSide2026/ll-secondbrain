# fintechlawyer.ca — Authority Site Strategy

This document defines the strategic role of fintechlawyer.ca within the Levine
Law marketing architecture. It is the controlling artifact for all content,
structural, and positioning decisions on that domain. It supersedes all implicit
or conflicting assumptions in funnel.yaml and other artifacts.

**Owner:** Levine Professional Corporation

---

## 1. Role in the System

fintechlawyer.ca is the discovery and education layer within Levine Law Funnel 3.
It is a top-of-funnel authority and trust surface for fintech, payments, and
FINTRAC-related legal issues.

It is not a product surface. It is not a standalone business. It is not a
direct-response funnel. It does not sell MSB registration.

Its primary purpose is to generate qualified regulatory and compliance-related
demand for Levine Law. It operates inside Funnel 3, not upstream or separate
from it.

**Positioning statement:**

> "Canada's most practical regulatory content hub for payments, MSBs, and
> fintech operators."

---

## 2. Audience Definition

fintechlawyer.ca addresses a defined, non-lawyer audience:

- Founders
- CFOs
- Operators in payments, fintech, and crypto-adjacent businesses

The content is written for decision-makers who are operating in or entering
Canada and who carry regulatory or compliance exposure. These are not passive
readers. They are operators with active legal needs.

**Commercial intent anchors:**

- Entering or operating in Canada as a payments or fintech business
- Managing FINTRAC compliance obligations
- Responding to audits, compliance reviews, or Administrative Monetary Penalties
- Structuring PSP or payment flows under Canadian law
- Managing banking access or de-risking exposure
- Navigating crypto-to-fiat regulatory boundaries

**Phase 1 goal:** traffic volume and general awareness. The site builds search
presence and category authority as the foundation for qualified demand generation.

---

## 3. Content Architecture

All content on fintechlawyer.ca maps to one of five pillars. Content that does
not belong to a defined pillar is not published on this domain.

### Pillar 1: FINTRAC Reporting, Compliance Reviews, AMPs, and Remediation

The obligations, mechanics, and enforcement environment for businesses subject
to PCMLTFA. This includes reporting obligations, compliance program requirements,
self-assessment processes, AMP risk, and regulatory remediation.

**Existing assets:** The current 21 EO1 blog posts belong to this pillar. They
are the seed cluster for Pillar 1. They serve as the education layer, not as a
service offering.

### Pillar 2: Payments Structuring

The regulatory classification of business models in the Canadian payments
landscape, including the distinction between PSPs, agents, and technology
providers, and the compliance implications of each structure.

### Pillar 3: Banking Relationships and De-Risking

The practical reality of banking access for MSBs, PSPs, and fintech operators
in Canada, including account termination risk, correspondent banking dynamics,
and strategies for maintaining or restoring banking relationships.

### Pillar 4: Cross-Border Operations

Regulatory sequencing and structural considerations for companies operating
across the Canada, UK, and EU regulatory environments, including market entry,
multi-jurisdiction compliance, and regulatory arbitrage risks.

### Pillar 5: Digital Assets, Fiat Rails, and RPAA

The regulatory treatment of crypto-adjacent business models that interact with
Canadian payment infrastructure or fiat currency, including RPAA obligations,
MSB classification triggers, and AML program requirements.

**Rule:** All future content maps to one of these five pillars before it is
produced. No orphan or standalone content is permitted on this domain.

## 4. Funnel Integration

Funnel 3 is a two-layer system owned by Levine Law:

| Layer | Domain | Function |
|---|---|---|
| Discovery and Education | fintechlawyer.ca | Authority content, issue education, trust building, initial lead capture |
| Credibility and Conversion | Levine Law site | Firm validation, consultation booking, mandate conversion |

fintechlawyer.ca is part of Funnel 3. It captures leads directly for Levine Law.
It supports the firm's core conversion environment; it does not replace it.

Levine Law landing pages with information capture are linked from fintechlawyer.ca.
fintechlawyer.ca also captures information directly and routes it to Levine Law.

## 5. System Relationship

| Domain | Role |
|---|---|
| fintechlawyer.ca | Search acquisition, issue education, trust formation, early-stage lead capture |
| Levine Law main site | Firm validation, lawyer credibility, consultation booking, mandate formation |

Funnel 3 spans both surfaces. Conversion is completed through Levine Law, not
through the authority site alone.

## 6. Practice Area Positioning Constraint

Levine Law's FINTRAC-related work is focused on:

- Reporting obligations
- Compliance reviews
- Administrative Monetary Penalties
- Regulatory remediation
- Enforcement response

fintechlawyer.ca does not position MSB registration as a primary service.
Registration is discussed as context within content addressing the broader
compliance and regulatory environment. It is not framed as a mandate or a
standalone deliverable.
