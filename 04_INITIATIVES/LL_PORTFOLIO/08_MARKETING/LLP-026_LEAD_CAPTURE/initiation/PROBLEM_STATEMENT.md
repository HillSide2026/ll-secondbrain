# Problem Statement

Project ID: LLP-026
Project Path: 04_INITIATIVES/LL_PORTFOLIO/08_MARKETING/LLP-026_LEAD_CAPTURE

Three marketing funnels (F01, F02, F03) are producing or will produce organic
traffic to Levine Law pillar and supporting content. There is currently no
governed lead capture layer — no defined CTA infrastructure, no opt-in
mechanics, no lead magnet delivery system, and no routing logic to hand leads
from funnel traffic to the intake pipeline (LLP-014).

Without this layer, funnel traffic does not convert to inquiry volume.
Funnels can be fully built and ranking, but if there is no governed capture
mechanism, the traffic produces no pipeline activity.

The problem is not that the funnels are missing content. The problem is that
when a visitor arrives and is ready to act, there is nothing to capture that
intent and route it into the intake system.

This project initiates the work to close that gap.
