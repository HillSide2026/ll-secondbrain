# Risk Scan

Project ID: LLP-026
Project Path: 04_INITIATIVES/LL_PORTFOLIO/08_MARKETING/LLP-026_LEAD_CAPTURE

## Top Risks

1. No defined offer: funnels launch without an approved lead magnet or CTA,
   so capture rate is zero despite live funnel content.
2. Attribution failure: leads are captured but source funnel is not tagged,
   making funnel-level conversion reporting impossible.
3. Routing mismatch: captured leads do not reach LLP-014 correctly — form
   submissions arrive in an unmonitored channel or with no triage trigger.
4. Scope creep: lead capture work expands into funnel content ownership or
   CRM provisioning, blurring ownership boundaries with LLP-011/012/013 and
   LLP-004.
5. Platform dependency: capture infrastructure is built on a single tool with
   no fallback, creating a single point of failure for all lead volume.

## Key Assumptions

- F01, F02, and F03 funnels will have live content pages on which CTAs can
  be placed before this project completes Planning
- The intake pipeline (LLP-014) will define what it needs from incoming leads
  (format, fields, routing) before capture configuration is finalized
- ML1 will approve a lead magnet offer for at least one funnel before
  opt-in mechanics are built

## Go / No-Go Judgment

Go — advance to Planning.
