# Success Criteria

Project ID: LLP-026
Project Path: 04_INITIATIVES/LL_PORTFOLIO/08_MARKETING/LLP-026_LEAD_CAPTURE

This project is successful if:

- Contact forms and CTAs are live on all active funnel pages across F01, F02,
  and F03
- Lead magnet opt-in mechanics are operational for each active funnel
- Incoming leads are attributed to source funnel at point of capture
- All captured leads route correctly into the intake pipeline (LLP-014)
- Conversion rate per funnel is measurable (opt-in and contact form
  submissions tracked separately)
- No lead is captured without a source attribution tag
- The capture layer operates without requiring ML1 manual intervention per lead

## Metric Dependency

Specific conversion rate thresholds, opt-in rate benchmarks, and attribution
schema definitions are Planning deliverables. They cannot be finalized until
the funnel metric definitions for F01 (LLP-011), F02 (LLP-012), and F03
(LLP-013) are approved by ML1. Lead capture metrics must use the same event
definitions and naming conventions as the funnel metrics they sit inside.
Planning must not lock capture-layer metric thresholds before funnel-level
metric approval is confirmed.
