# Project Charter

Project ID: LLP-026
Project Path: 04_INITIATIVES/LL_PORTFOLIO/08_MARKETING/LLP-026_LEAD_CAPTURE
Project Type: Marketing Operations

Project Name: Lead Capture

## Purpose

Govern lead capture operations across all marketing funnels — landing pages,
lead magnets, opt-ins, and top-of-funnel conversion assets. Bridges funnel
traffic from LLP-011 (F01), LLP-012 (F02), and LLP-013 (F03) to the intake
pipeline (LLP-014).

## Current ML1 Direction

- This project is initiated and authorized to advance to Planning.
- Lead capture infrastructure does not exist yet; planning will define the
  build scope.
- No CTA, form, or opt-in configuration is authorized until Planning artifacts
  are approved.

## In Scope

- CTA and contact form configuration on all active funnel pages (F01, F02, F03)
- Lead magnet opt-in mechanics and delivery
- Top-of-funnel conversion assets (lead magnets, checklist downloads,
  consultation booking CTAs)
- Lead routing logic — which form submission goes where in the intake pipeline
- Attribution tagging for lead source tracking across funnels
- Landing page conversion infrastructure

## Out of Scope

- Funnel content production and SEO (LLP-011, LLP-012, LLP-013)
- Intake pipeline management (LLP-014)
- CRM provisioning and Clio setup (LLP-004)
- Paid advertising management

## Authority

ML1 is the sole authority on CTA design, offer selection, routing decisions,
and any external platform configuration.
