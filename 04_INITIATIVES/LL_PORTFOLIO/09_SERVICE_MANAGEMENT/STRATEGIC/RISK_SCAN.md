# Risk Scan

Project ID: LLP-26-30
Project Path: 09_SERVICE_MANAGEMENT/STRATEGIC

## Top 5 Risks
1. Strategic risk: the strategic-tier definition may blur advisory, governance, and delivery posture.
2. Scope risk: strategic-tier commitments may expand beyond approved service boundaries.
3. Schedule risk: high-touch expectations may exceed a sustainable review and delivery cadence.
4. Budget risk: strategic-tier support demands may exceed baseline resourcing assumptions.
5. Financial risk: strategic-tier economics may not offset the added delivery overhead.

## Key Assumptions
- The strategic tier can be defined without collapsing into bespoke unbounded service.
- Boundaries between strategic service and legal-delivery decisions will remain explicit.
- Capacity exists to support a higher-touch tier without destabilizing other work.
- Additional support demands can be resourced within acceptable limits.
- Higher-tier economics will justify the added operational burden.
