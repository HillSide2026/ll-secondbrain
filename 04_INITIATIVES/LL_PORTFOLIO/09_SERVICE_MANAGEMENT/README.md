# 09_SERVICE_MANAGEMENT

## Purpose

Maintain a controlled service-tier view across matters and services.

## Scope

### In Scope
- Tier categorization and tracking for service posture.
- Cross-matter visibility by service tier.
- ML1 escalation signals for tier movement decisions.

### Out of Scope
- Replacing Clio, Gmail, or SharePoint source-of-truth records.
- Autonomous service strategy decisions.
- Client-facing publication without ML1 approval.

## ML1 Authority Statement

ML1 is the sole authority for service-tier definitions, tier assignment policy, and tier changes.

## Explicit Prohibitions

- Do not treat this folder as source-of-truth for matter registry or document control.
- Do not auto-promote or auto-demote services between tiers.
- Do not publish external outputs from this folder without ML1 approval.
