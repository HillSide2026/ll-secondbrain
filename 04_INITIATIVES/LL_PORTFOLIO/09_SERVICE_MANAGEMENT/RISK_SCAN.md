# Risk Scan

Project ID: LLP-037
Project Path: 09_SERVICE_MANAGEMENT

## Top 5 Risks
1. Strategic risk: the service-tier model may not align with actual firm service posture.
2. Scope risk: tier boundaries may remain unclear across matters and service states.
3. Schedule risk: rollout may stall pending ML1 decisions on tier criteria and transition rules.
4. Budget risk: service administration effort may exceed planned operating capacity.
5. Financial risk: the tier structure may not improve economics enough to justify its overhead.

## Key Assumptions
- Tier logic can be defined clearly enough to support repeatable classification.
- Service management will remain a control map and not replace matter records.
- ML1 decisions on tier criteria and transitions will arrive in time for rollout.
- Administrative load from tier tracking can be sustained within baseline capacity.
- The tier model will improve service clarity enough to justify ongoing overhead.
