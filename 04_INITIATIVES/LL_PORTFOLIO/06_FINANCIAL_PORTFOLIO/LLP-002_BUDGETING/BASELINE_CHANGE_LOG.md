# Baseline Change Log

## Purpose

Track any approved change to the Levine Law 2026 budget baseline.

This log exists to prevent sensitivity scenarios, working forecasts, or
temporary operating views from replacing the approved budget baseline by
implication.

## Baseline Change Rule

The approved baseline remains operative unless ML1 records a specific reason,
explanation, and justification for using a different baseline.

Sensitivity scenarios may inform review, but they do not replace the approved
baseline by implication.
