# Success Criteria

Project ID: LLP-008
Project Path: 03_FIRM_OPERATIONS/LLP-008_CLOSING

The project will be successful if:

1. ML1 approves the closing-stage boundary and purpose.
2. The required closure preconditions are explicitly documented.
3. Financial and record closure evidence requirements are clear.
4. The handoff from fulfillment closing to archive/non-fulfillment handling is explicit.
5. The project is ready for planning-stage design of closeout workflow, controls, and measures.
