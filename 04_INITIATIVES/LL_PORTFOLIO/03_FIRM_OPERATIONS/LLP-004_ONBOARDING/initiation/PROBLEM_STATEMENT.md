# Problem Statement

Project: LLP-26-05 - Onboarding
> ML2 DRAFT - Awaiting ML1 review and approval.

## Problem

Onboarding between qualified lead and engagement authorization is not consistently governed as a formal gate process.

Observed failure modes:
- qualified leads are not always documented with explicit Gate 1 evidence
- engagement agreement drafting/circulation is inconsistent
- pending matter setup in Clio is delayed or omitted
- onboarding exceptions are discovered late and escalate into opening delays
- boundary violations occur when work starts before authorization

## Impact of Not Solving

- matters enter opening with missing authorization artifacts
- opening cycle starts with avoidable defects and rework
- ML1 cannot reliably assess readiness at Gate 2
- risk of unauthorized pre-engagement work increases
