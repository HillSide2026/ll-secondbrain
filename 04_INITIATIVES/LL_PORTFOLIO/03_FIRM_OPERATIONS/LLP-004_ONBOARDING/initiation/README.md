# LLP-004 - Onboarding

## Scope

LLP-004 governs the onboarding phase of the matter lifecycle.
It starts at Gate 1 (Qualified Lead) and ends at Gate 2 (Engagement).

### In scope

- Gate 1 qualification control:
  - consultation scheduled or completed
  - client confirms intent to proceed
- Lead -> client onboarding transition.
- Engagement agreement drafting and circulation tracking.
- Administrative setup required before authorization.
- Clio matter creation in `Pending` status.
- Gate 2 readiness decision packet for ML1.

### Out of scope

- Opening execution tasks after engagement is signed (LLP-005).
- Ongoing matter maintenance after Gate 3 (LLP-006).
- Closing-stage compliance workflow (deferred pending further ML1 instruction).
- Legal work before engagement authorization.

## Lifecycle Boundary

- Entry gate: Gate 1 - Qualified Lead.
- Exit gate: Gate 2 - Engagement (agreement signed).
- Handoff target: `03_FIRM_OPERATIONS/LLP-005_OPENING`.

## Authority

ML1 is the sole authority for gate activation, exceptions, and promotion from onboarding to opening.
