# Scope Definition

Project ID: LLP-004
Project Path: 03_FIRM_OPERATIONS/LLP-004_ONBOARDING
Stage: Planning

## Implementation Objective
Implement a controlled onboarding workflow from Gate 1 (Qualified Lead) to Gate 2 (Engagement Signed), with auditable evidence, pending-matter setup, and authorization-ready handoff to opening.

## In Scope
- Gate 1 trigger validation (consultation scheduled/completed + intent to proceed).
- Lead to onboarding transition controls.
- Engagement agreement drafting and circulation workflow.
- Clio matter creation and validation in `Pending` status.
- Onboarding exception detection and escalation.
- Gate 2 readiness packet generation for ML1 decisioning.

## Out of Scope
- Opening tasks after signed engagement (LLP-005).
- Post-open maintenance and support (LLP-006).
- Closing-stage workflow design.
- Substantive legal-delivery decisioning.
