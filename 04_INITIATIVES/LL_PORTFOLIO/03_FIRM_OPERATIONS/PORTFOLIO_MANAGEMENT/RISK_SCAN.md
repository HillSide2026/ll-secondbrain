# Risk Scan

Project ID: LLP-26-11
Project Path: 03_FIRM_OPERATIONS/PORTFOLIO_MANAGEMENT

## Top 5 Risks
1. Strategic risk: portfolio priorities may drift before ML1 defines the governing management intent.
2. Scope risk: portfolio management may absorb execution work that belongs inside individual projects.
3. Schedule risk: review cadence may be too slow to support timely portfolio decisions.
4. Budget risk: tracking and coordination overhead may exceed baseline operating capacity.
5. Financial risk: resource allocation decisions may fail to produce enough portfolio value relative to management overhead.

## Key Assumptions
- ML1 will define portfolio governance intent and decision criteria before active expansion.
- Project status and dependency data are available in a form that supports portfolio-level review.
- Portfolio management remains a coordination layer and does not replace project-level execution ownership.
- Review cadence can be sustained without destabilizing active work.
- Management overhead will remain proportionate to the decisions supported.
