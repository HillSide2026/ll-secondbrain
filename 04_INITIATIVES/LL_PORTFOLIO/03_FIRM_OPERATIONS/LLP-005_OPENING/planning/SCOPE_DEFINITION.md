## Implementation Objective
Prepare implementation of Opening so every Gate 2-authorized matter is operationally ready for Gate 3 approval and handoff to LLP-006 Maintenance.

## In Scope
- Gate 2 intake validation from LLP-004 handoff artifacts.
- Opening checklist definition with role ownership (AL, AM, EA).
- Financial readiness planning:
  - billing setup requirements
  - trust/retainer readiness requirements (where applicable)
  - accounts ownership and evidence requirements
- Record/metadata readiness planning:
  - required Clio fields and status consistency
  - Asana Opening task requirements
  - SharePoint folder/document readiness requirements
- Gate 3 approval packet requirements and evidence completeness rules.
- Handoff specification to LLP-006 after Gate 3 approval.

- Post-Gate-3 maintenance/reconciliation operations (LLP-006).
- Closing-stage workflow design.
- Substantive legal strategy/delivery decisions.
