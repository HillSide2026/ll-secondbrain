# Problem Statement

Project: LLP-26-06 - Opening
> ML2 DRAFT - Awaiting ML1 review and approval.

## Problem

After engagement authorization, opening steps are not always executed as a controlled, complete gate before legal work and billing proceed.

Observed failure modes:
- opening tasks start without a standardized completion checklist
- financial readiness is incomplete or late
- record/compliance setup is inconsistent
- matters are treated as open without full operational readiness proof
- transition to maintenance occurs with unresolved setup defects

## Impact of Not Solving

- legal delivery starts on partially configured matters
- billing and compliance risk increases
- maintenance absorbs avoidable remediation workload
- ML1 cannot rely on Gate 3 as a true operational-ready signal
