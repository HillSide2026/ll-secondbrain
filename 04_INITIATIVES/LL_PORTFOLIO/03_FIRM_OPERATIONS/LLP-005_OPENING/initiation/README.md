# LLP-005 - Opening

## Scope

LLP-005 governs the opening phase of the matter lifecycle.
It starts at Gate 2 (Engagement signed) and ends at Gate 3 (Matter Open).

### In scope

- Gate 2 authorization intake from LLP-004 onboarding outputs.
- Clio transition control for authorized matters (`Pending` -> `Open`) where applicable to opening workflow.
- Opening task execution and completion tracking.
- Financial readiness checks.
- Record-status and compliance setup checks.
- Internal approval packet for Gate 3.

### Out of scope

- Qualified-lead and engagement-circulation work before Gate 2 (LLP-004).
- Ongoing post-open maintenance and support (LLP-006).
- Closing-stage compliance workflow (deferred pending further ML1 instruction).
- Legal strategy or legal-delivery decisions.

## Lifecycle Boundary

- Entry gate: Gate 2 - Engagement (agreement signed).
- Exit gate: Gate 3 - Matter Open (all opening steps complete and approved).
- Handoff target: `03_FIRM_OPERATIONS/LLP-006_MAINTENANCE`.

## Authority

ML1 is the sole authority for Gate 3 completion approval and transition to maintenance.
