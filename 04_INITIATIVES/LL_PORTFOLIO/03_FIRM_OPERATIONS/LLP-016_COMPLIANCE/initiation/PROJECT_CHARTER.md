# Project Charter

Project ID: LLP-016
Project Path: 03_FIRM_OPERATIONS/LLP-016_COMPLIANCE
Project Type: Operational Project
Stage: Initiating

## 1. Purpose
- Govern the operational compliance control layer for recurring firm obligations so compliance work is explicit, reviewable, and bounded by ML1 authority.

## 2. Nature of Project
- Operational compliance packet for execution and tracking.
- Internal governance and deadline-control work only.
- No legal interpretation, policy making, or autonomous exception approval.
- Evidence and escalation design, not substantive compliance judgment.

## 3. Strategic Rationale
- Compliance work already exists as a real operating responsibility, and it should be governed as an explicit project before deadlines, evidence, or responsibilities drift informally.

## 4. High-Level Deliverable
- A defined compliance packet covering obligation classes, evidence requirements, escalation paths, and operational boundaries for recurring firm compliance work.

## 5. Authority
Final approval authority: ML1.

## 6. Promotion Path
- Advance to Planning once ML1 confirms the initial compliance boundary, the main obligation classes in scope, and the source systems or records that support compliance evidence.
