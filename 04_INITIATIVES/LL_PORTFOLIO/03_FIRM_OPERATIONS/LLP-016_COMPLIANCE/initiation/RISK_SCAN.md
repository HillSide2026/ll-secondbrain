# Risk Scan

Project ID: LLP-016
Project Path: 03_FIRM_OPERATIONS/LLP-016_COMPLIANCE

## Top 5 Risks
1. Strategic risk: the project may target the wrong compliance obligations and fail to improve real compliance readiness.
2. Scope risk: the packet may drift into policy interpretation, legal analysis, or strategic risk decisions that do not belong in an operational project.
3. Schedule risk: deadline and evidence mapping may take longer than expected because obligations are currently scattered or informal.
4. Budget risk: compliance tracking may become too manual or too broad for the available operating capacity.
5. Financial risk: weak compliance execution or evidence capture may contribute to penalties, rework, or avoidable professional risk.

## Key Assumptions
- A bounded operational compliance packet can be defined without deciding every substantive policy question up front.
- ML1 wants compliance execution and evidence handling made explicit before more automation or scheduling logic is added.
- The initial scope can be framed around recurring obligations rather than one-off exceptions.
- The project can remain operational and governance-oriented without overstating compliance completion.
- Existing firm records can support later planning of evidence and reminder workflows.
