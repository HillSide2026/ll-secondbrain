# Problem Statement

Project ID: LLP-018
Project Path: 04_RISK/LLP-018_FINANCIAL_RISK

## Current Problem
- Financial risks exist across revenue, margin, spend, and cash timing, but the LL portfolio did not yet have a canonical packet dedicated to governing those exposures.

## Impact
- Without a defined financial-risk packet, important exposures can remain implicit, fragmented across budget and strategy files, or escalated too late for ML1 review.

## Why Now
- LLP-002 budget scenarios and LLP-030 firm strategy already depend on financial assumptions, making the absence of a dedicated risk view more costly.
