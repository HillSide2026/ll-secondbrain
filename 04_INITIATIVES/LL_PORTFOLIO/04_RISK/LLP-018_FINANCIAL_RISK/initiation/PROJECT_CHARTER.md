# Project Charter

Project ID: LLP-018
Project Path: 04_RISK/LLP-018_FINANCIAL_RISK
Project Type: Management Project
Stage: Initiating

## 1. Purpose
- Govern financial risk visibility and escalation for Levine Law so core financial exposures are identified, bounded, and reviewable by ML1.

## 2. Nature of Project
- Management risk packet focused on financial exposures, thresholds, and escalation logic rather than accounting recordkeeping or pricing decisions.

## 3. Strategic Rationale
- As budgeting, marketing, and strategy work mature, the firm needs a dedicated financial-risk view separate from both accounting facts and advisory financial models.

## 4. High-Level Deliverable
- A governed financial-risk packet covering the major exposure categories, escalation boundaries, and relationships to LLP-001, LLP-002, and LLP-030.

## 5. Authority
Final approval authority: ML1.

## 6. Promotion Path
- Advance to Planning once ML1 confirms the financial-risk categories, reporting cadence, and threshold/measurement design that should govern this packet.
