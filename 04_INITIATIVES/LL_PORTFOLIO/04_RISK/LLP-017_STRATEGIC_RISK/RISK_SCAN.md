# Risk Scan

Project ID: LLP-26-13
Project Path: 04_RISK/LLP-017_STRATEGIC_RISK

## Top 5 Risks
1. Strategic risk: the strategic-risk framework may drift from actual firm decision needs.
2. Scope risk: risk tracking may expand into unsupported compliance declarations or judgment calls.
3. Schedule risk: risk review cycles may lag behind portfolio changes and reduce usefulness.
4. Budget risk: maintaining the risk program may consume more operating capacity than assumed.
5. Financial risk: the decision value generated by the framework may not justify the ongoing management burden.

## Key Assumptions
- ML1 will define what strategic-risk monitoring should influence and what remains out of scope.
- The project remains a governed tracking and doctrine function rather than an autonomous control layer.
- Portfolio inputs needed for risk review can be gathered consistently.
- Review cadence can remain current enough to support active decisions.
- The overhead of maintaining the framework stays proportionate to its value.
