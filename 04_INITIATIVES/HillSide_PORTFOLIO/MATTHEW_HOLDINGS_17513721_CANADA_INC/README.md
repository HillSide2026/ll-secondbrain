# Matthew Holdings

17513721 Canada Inc

Matthew Holdings is the primary corporation within the HillSide portfolio
branch tracked here.

`17513721 Canada Inc` will be registered as `Dominion Partners`. Matthew
Holdings remains the internal portfolio label for the corporation in this
folder.

Its primary role is to hold and operate the collaborating firm agreement with
Andersen Consulting. Its secondary role is to hold selected portfolio entities
and operating lines that support the Dominion Partners platform.

It is not the only holdco in the broader structure:

## Andersen Relationship Architecture

The relationship to Andersen is tied to Sidera, an Andersen member firm. The
175 / Dominion Partners relationship is dependent on Sidera.

Sidera wants 175 / Dominion Partners to do trade remedies work on North
American files. That lane is acceptable in principle, but the current strategy
needs to account for two constraints: Sidera does not currently have North
American files, and 175 / ML1 does not yet have deep trade remedies experience.
The principal of Sidera is a former classmate of ML1 who has become highly
successful in Brazil, which makes the relationship valuable but also important
to handle with care.

Sidera is owned by the Saldanha family. It was founded by Mr. Saldanha and has
been grown by his daughter. Ms. Saldanha is married to Mr. Ures. Mr. Saldanha,
Ms. Saldanha, and Mr. Ures are wealthy, highly skilled, and formidable
collaborators.

They have extensive C-suite relationships with Brazilian industry, especially
agri-food, as well as European and, to some extent, North American investors
and traders into Brazil.

175 / Dominion Partners needs to identify a Canadian offering that is:

- repeatable for 175;
- linked to Canada;
- seen as valuable by Sidera; and
- within international trade, because Sidera's relationship to Andersen hinges
  on developing an International Trade Services Line.

The five Dominion / 175 service lines are:

- trade remedies
- market access
- data centers
- mining
- payment services

Each service line should be positioned clearly before being advanced.

Current offering work:

- `DOMINION_PLATFORM/CANADA_TRADE_READINESS_MARKET_DIAGNOSTIC.md` — product
  for foreign manufacturers, exporters, investors, distributors, and industry
  groups evaluating or expanding Canadian trade activity.
- `DOMINION_PLATFORM/GLOBAL_TRADE_DIVERSIFICATION_MARKET_ACCESS_DIAGNOSTIC.md`
  — companion product for Canadian manufacturers, distributors, exporters,
  investors, and industrial groups evaluating international expansion, sourcing
  diversification, or non-U.S. market development.

One priority is to find additional allies within Andersen without damaging the
Sidera relationship. Potential channels include:

- other Canadian Andersen member firms or collaborating firms, including:
  - accounting firms; and
  - trade-adjacent consultants, especially customs consultants; and
- Andersen growth segments, meaning large industry areas such as energy or
  pharma.

Any ally-building strategy should preserve Sidera trust, avoid channel conflict,
and remain consistent with the collaborating firm agreement and Andersen brand
permissions.

Operational matters tied to the Andersen Service Line are tracked inside
Levine Law. The Andersen Service Line continues to follow the Levine Law
time-for-money delivery model, and the Andersen relationship is credited to
Levine Law in the 2026 operating picture even though the Andersen fees are
received by `17513721 Canada Inc`.

FinSure and the Payment Services Consulting Line are owned and operated
through `17513721 Canada Inc`.

## Enterprise Value Strategy

175 needs a strategy for developing its own enterprise value, separate from the
personal goodwill and time-for-money economics of Levine Law.

The strategy should assess how 175 can convert the Andersen Consulting
collaboration and Dominion Partners platform into durable corporate value,
including:

- repeatable service lines that can be delivered under Dominion Partners;
- client, referral, and channel relationships owned or controlled by 175;
- reusable platform assets, workflows, templates, and operating infrastructure;
- revenue that is not solely dependent on ML1 personal delivery;
- a credible pipeline from Andersen-originated work into 175-owned offerings;
  and
- whether FinSure, the Payment Services Consulting Line, and Dominion Platform
  assets increase 175 enterprise value or remain merely adjunct service tools.

Current 175-level workstreams:

- reviewing the Andersen Consulting collaborating firm agreement;
- developing Dominion Partners branding to maximize credibility and synergy
  with Andersen, subject to what the agreement permits and the risk of
  overreliance on the Andersen brand;
- improving the `dominionpartners.ca` website; and
- considering whether renting or leasing space for Dominion Partners would
  increase brand value.

The controlled HillSide project for this business line is:

- `../BUSINESS_PROJECTS/HBP-014_175_DOMINION_ANDERSEN_SERVICE_LINE/`

This strategy is separate from, but relevant to, the Q4 Target Restructuring.
Upon sale of 174 and / or Ontario MSB Corp, and before the Q4 Target
Restructuring, 175 should assess whether capital is better used for Kendal
Securities, passive investing through Matthew Capital, or strengthening 175's
own enterprise value.
