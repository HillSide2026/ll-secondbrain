# Canada Trade Readiness & Market Diagnostic

This is the working draft for the core Dominion Partners / 175 offering.

The offering must be:

- repeatable for 175;
- linked to Canada;
- seen as valuable by Sidera; and
- within international trade, because Sidera's relationship to Andersen hinges
  on developing an International Trade Services Line.

---

## Core Offering

Canada Trade Readiness & Market Diagnostic is a standardized assessment for
foreign manufacturers, exporters, investors, distributors, and industry groups
evaluating or expanding Canadian trade activity.

The diagnostic identifies:

- customs and tariff exposure;
- Canadian import structure vulnerabilities;
- trade remedies exposure;
- importer / distributor structure weaknesses;
- valuation and transfer-pricing alignment risks; and
- operational gaps affecting Canadian market access.

It produces:

- a structured risk map;
- a prioritized implementation roadmap; and
- coordinated referral pathways across customs, tax, legal, accounting,
  brokerage, logistics, and compliance functions.

---

## Decision Support Focus

The product should help clients answer operational and strategic questions:

- Can we import this into Canada efficiently?
- Are we exposed to AD / CVD risk?
- Is our importer / distributor structure defensible?
- Could sanctions or forced labour rules affect us?
- Is Canada commercially viable under current tariff exposure?
- Are we creating customs valuation risk through our pricing model?
- What structure minimizes operational friction?
- What functions must be localized in Canada?
- What implementation workstreams are required?

The product should be a strategic advisory product, not merely a rules-analysis
memo.

---

## Repeatable Architecture

The offering should be built as a modular framework.

### Phase 1 — Rapid Screen

Timing: 2-5 days.

Purpose: low-cost intake product and lead-generation engine.

Deliverables:

- intake review;
- preliminary trade-risk flags;
- customs / tariff snapshot;
- sanctions screening;
- sector-specific issue triggers;
- preliminary import pathway mapping; and
- high-level recommendations.

Goal: determine whether Canada entry is viable, whether deeper work is needed,
and which Andersen / Sidera functions should engage.

### Phase 2 — Full Diagnostic

This is the core scalable product.

Modules:

- Trade Flow Mapping: supplier, exporter, importer, distributor, end customer,
  Incoterms, importer of record structure, customs valuation mechanics, and
  related-party flows.
- Tariff & Customs Exposure: tariff classification exposure, origin
  considerations, valuation issues, duty optimization opportunities, and
  drawback / relief potential.
- Trade Remedies Exposure: Canadian SIMA exposure, anti-dumping /
  countervailing sensitivity, country / product risk mapping, and competitor
  complaint vulnerability.
- Sanctions & Supply-Chain Review: Russian sanctions, forced labour exposure,
  supply-chain sourcing flags, and restricted party exposure.
- Regulatory Perimeter: sector-specific review for pharma, energy, agri-food,
  industrial products, critical minerals, and environmental controls.
- Commercial Structure Assessment: distributor structure, Canadian subsidiary
  need, importer structure, agency vs. reseller, and warehousing / logistics
  structure.
- Implementation Pathway: what must be fixed, sequencing, required advisors,
  and estimated implementation burden.

## Differentiated Module

### Cross-Border Pricing & Import Structure Review

This module integrates:

- customs valuation;
- transfer pricing;
- intercompany contracting;
- importer structure;
- duty allocation; and
- commercial operating model.

It assesses:

- related-party pricing;
- customs valuation vulnerability;
- retroactive adjustment risk;
- importer / distributor alignment;
- landed cost allocation; and
- intercompany structure compatibility.

This module is especially relevant for manufacturers, industrial groups,
energy, infrastructure, pharma, Latin American exporters, and European groups.

- energy and infrastructure;
- critical minerals;

- construction materials; and

## Operational Principle

Do not let this become custom memo writing.

The output should be:

- consulting-grade;
- matrix-driven;
- operational;
- visually standardized; and
- implementation-oriented.

Expected output forms include:

- heat maps;
- risk scoring;
- pathway diagrams;
- implementation tables;
- referral matrices; and
- traffic-light indicators.
