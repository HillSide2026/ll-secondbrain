# Global Trade Diversification & Market Access Diagnostic

This is the working draft for the companion Dominion Partners / 175 offering
for Canadians looking to export out.

---

## Core Offering

Global Trade Diversification & Market Access Diagnostic is a structured
assessment for Canadian manufacturers, distributors, exporters, investors, and
industrial groups evaluating international expansion, sourcing diversification,
or non-U.S. market development.

The diagnostic identifies:

- tariff and market-access barriers;
- foreign customs and import structure risks;
- sanctions and supply-chain vulnerabilities;
- trade agreement utilization opportunities;
- distributor and operating structure considerations;
- country-specific trade remedies exposure;
- transfer pricing and customs valuation alignment issues; and
- operational constraints affecting cross-border expansion.

It produces:

- a prioritized market-access and diversification risk map;
- a jurisdictional comparison framework;
- operating structure recommendations; and
- implementation pathways across customs, tax, legal, logistics, compliance,
  and commercial functions.
