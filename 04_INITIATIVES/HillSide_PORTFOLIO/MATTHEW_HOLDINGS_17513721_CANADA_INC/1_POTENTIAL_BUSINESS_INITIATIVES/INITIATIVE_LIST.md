## Stage Definitions

- `initiating`: initiative is being framed, screened, and initially monitored before execution commitment
- `planning`: initiative scope, roadmap, assumptions, risks, and decision criteria are being formalized
- `executing`: approved work is actively being carried out, with increased monitoring during execution
- `closing`: primary execution is complete and the initiative is in close-out, residual follow-through, or post-closing handling
