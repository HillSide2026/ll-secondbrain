# Matthew Capital

Matthew Capital is a planned new holding corporation in the broader HillSide
structure.

It is separate from Kendal Securities. Kendal Securities is the planned
dealer-registration entity. Matthew Capital is the planned holding corporation.

It is not yet incorporated.

---

## Trigger

Matthew Capital will be incorporated concurrently with the Kendal Securities
dealer-registration funding plan.

The plan is tied to the sale of `17409052 Canada Inc` (174 / Granville).

---

## Role

Matthew Capital will acquire shares of `17513721 Canada Inc` (Matthew Holdings
/ 175).

After 175 invests the dealer-registration capital in Kendal Securities, the
remainder of the Granville sale proceeds will be distributed by dividend from
175 to Matthew Capital.

---

## Relationship to Kendal Securities

Kendal Securities will hold funds invested by 175 to support dealer-registration
criteria. Kendal Securities is expected to pursue exempt market dealer
registration, with restricted dealer remaining possible.

Matthew Capital will not be the dealer-registration entity.

---

## Status

Not yet incorporated. Planning not yet started.
