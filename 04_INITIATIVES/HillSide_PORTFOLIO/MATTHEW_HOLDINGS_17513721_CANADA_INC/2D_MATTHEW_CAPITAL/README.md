# Matthew Capital

Matthew Capital is a planned NewCo under `17513721 Canada Inc` (Matthew
Holdings / 175).

It is not yet incorporated.

---

## Trigger

Matthew Capital will be incorporated and funded by 175 if and when `17409052
Canada Inc` (174 / Granville) reaches the preferred sale-readiness gate.

Sale proceeds from 174 at any gate flow into 175. The preferred gate triggers
the 175 investment in Matthew Capital.

---

## Objective

Matthew Capital will seek to become a registered securities dealer in Canada.

The registration path is an open question. The two candidates are:

- **Exempt market dealer (EMD):** trades securities in the exempt market,
  relying on prospectus exemptions; common path for private placement and
  alternative investment activity.
- **Restricted dealer:** a more limited registration, restricted to specific
  activities defined in registration conditions; more bespoke and less common.

No decision has been made between these paths.
