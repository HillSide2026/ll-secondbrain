# Kendal Securities

Kendal Securities is a planned NewCo under `17513721 Canada Inc` (Matthew
Holdings / 175). It is part of the broader Granville sale-proceeds and dealer
registration plan.

It is not yet incorporated.

---

## Trigger

Kendal Securities will be incorporated and funded by 175 if and when `17409052
Canada Inc` (174 / Granville) is sold or reaches the preferred sale-readiness
gate.

Sale proceeds from 174 at any gate flow into 175. The preferred gate triggers
the 175 investment in Kendal Securities. Kendal Securities will hold those
funds to support dealer-registration criteria, including capital or financial
resource expectations applicable to the chosen registration path.

---

## Objective

Kendal Securities will seek to become a registered securities dealer in Canada.

The expected registration path is exempt market dealer. Restricted dealer is
not off the table.

- **Exempt market dealer (EMD):** trades securities in the exempt market,
  relying on prospectus exemptions; expected path for the planned private
  placement and alternative investment activity.
- **Restricted dealer:** a more limited registration, restricted to specific
  activities defined in registration conditions; more bespoke and less common.

Final registration path remains subject to legal, regulatory, and commercial
assessment.

---

## Registration Roles

Kendal Securities will appoint Matthew Levine as:

- ultimate designated person
- chief compliance officer

These appointments are part of the planned dealer-registration architecture.

---

## Ownership

Owner: `17513721 Canada Inc` (Matthew Holdings / 175)

Kendal Securities will sit under 175 as the dealer-registration entity.

The broader plan also contemplates a new holding corporation, Matthew Capital,
which will acquire shares of 175. Matthew Capital is separate from Kendal
Securities and is not the dealer-registration entity.

---

## Status

Not yet incorporated. No regulatory filings made. Planning not yet started.
