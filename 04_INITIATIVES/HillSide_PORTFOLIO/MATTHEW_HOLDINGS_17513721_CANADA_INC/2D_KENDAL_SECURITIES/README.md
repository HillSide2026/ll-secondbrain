# Kendal Securities

Kendal Securities is a planned NewCo under Matthew Capital. It is part of the
Q4 Target Restructuring, the approved core Q4 2026 Granville sale-proceeds and
dealer-registration plan.

It is not yet incorporated.

---

## Trigger

Kendal Securities will be incorporated and funded by Matthew Capital if and
when `17409052 Canada Inc` (174 / Granville) is sold or reaches the preferred
sale-readiness gate.

Sale proceeds from 174 at any gate flow into 175. Under the approved Q4 2026
core scenario, Matthew Capital will acquire shares of 175, 175 will distribute
the intended capital to Matthew Capital by dividend or other approved corporate
mechanism, and Matthew Capital will capitalize Kendal Securities directly.
Kendal Securities will hold those funds to support dealer-registration
criteria, including capital or financial resource expectations applicable to
the chosen registration path.

Upon sale of 174 and / or Ontario MSB Corp, and before approval and
implementation of the Q4 Target Restructuring, 175 must assess whether capital
is better used for Kendal Securities, passive investing through Matthew
Capital, or strengthening 175's own enterprise value.

---

## Objective

Kendal Securities will seek to become a registered securities dealer in Canada.

The expected registration path is exempt market dealer. Restricted dealer is
not off the table.

- **Exempt market dealer (EMD):** trades securities in the exempt market,
  relying on prospectus exemptions; expected path for the planned private
  placement and alternative investment activity.
- **Restricted dealer:** a more limited registration, restricted to specific
  activities defined in registration conditions; more bespoke and less common.

Final registration path remains subject to legal, regulatory, and commercial
assessment.
