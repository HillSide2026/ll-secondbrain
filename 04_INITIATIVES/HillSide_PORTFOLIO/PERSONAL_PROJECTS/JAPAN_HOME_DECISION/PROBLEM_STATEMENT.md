# Problem Statement

## Problem Description

The possibility of acquiring a home in Japan is currently unresolved, which
creates ambiguity in capital allocation, life planning, and attention.

## Current State

- there is no standalone project governing the Japan home question
- the rationale, guardrails, and decision criteria are not yet explicit
- key diligence questions have not been separated from aspiration

## Impact of the Problem

- capital-allocation uncertainty
- unnecessary decision drag
- risk of making a poorly framed cross-border decision
- reduced clarity on what 2026 priorities actually require

## Root Causes

- the issue has not been turned into a governed project
- decision criteria have not been written down
- the legal, tax, financing, and operating unknowns have not yet been
  prioritized

## Why This Matters

This is not just a lifestyle thought. A Japan home decision could affect
liquidity, housing strategy, long-horizon planning, and operational focus. This
project exists to force a disciplined answer.
