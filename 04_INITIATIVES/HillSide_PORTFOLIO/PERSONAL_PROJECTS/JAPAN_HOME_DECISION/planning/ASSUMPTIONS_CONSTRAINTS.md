# Assumptions and Constraints

Project ID: `JAPAN_HOME_DECISION`
Stage: `Planning`

## Assumptions

- the Japan home question is real enough to justify planning attention
- ML1 currently reports `CAD 900,000` in a high-interest savings account and
  `CAD 560,000` in managed equities as at `2026-04-03`
- any Japan-home budget should be set inside `HBP-001_WEALTH_MANAGEMENT`
  through `planning/WEALTH_PLAN.md` and `planning/HOUSING_BUDGET_POLICY.md`
- decision quality matters more than speed or aspiration

## Constraints

- no acquisition execution is authorized during planning
- no broker-led pseudo-planning should be treated as a substitute for a real
  wealth plan
- no pursue case is viable if it breaks the liquidity rules or housing-budget
  policy set through `HBP-001`
- no pursue recommendation is viable while critical cross-border diligence gaps
  remain vague
- the project must stay a bounded decision packet and avoid open-ended diligence
  sprawl
