# Success Criteria

## Definition of Success

A fully operational personal money system consistently generates surplus cash
flow and allocates that surplus toward wealth-building assets while providing a
clear progression path through the five money-machine stages: stability,
security, independence, freedom, and abundance.

## Five-Stage Progression Success Frame

| Stage | Success Condition |
| --- | --- |
| Financial Stability | Bills are paid on time, survival debt is avoided, and a 1-3 month cushion exists |
| Financial Security | A 3-6 month reserve exists, protections are in place, and there are no immediate financial threats |
| Financial Independence | Asset-driven cash flow can cover basic living expenses |
| Financial Freedom | Asset-driven cash flow can support the desired lifestyle |
| Financial Abundance | Wealth grows beyond lifestyle needs and can support contribution, ideas, and legacy |

## Key Metrics

| KPI | Definition |
| --- | --- |
| Savings rate | Percentage of income saved or invested |
| Investment contributions per month | Amount allocated to wealth-building assets each month |
| Net worth growth | Change in total assets minus liabilities over time |
| Expense ratio | Expenses as a percentage of income |

## Targets

- save and invest at least 15-25% of income
- build a 3-6 month emergency fund
- achieve a consistent monthly surplus
- reach net worth of at least CAD 2,000,000 by `2030-12-31`
- reach net worth of at least CAD 4,000,000 by `2040-12-31`

## Measurement Method

- monthly financial review
- net worth tracking
- cash flow tracking system

## Timeline for Evaluation

- monthly reviews
- quarterly adjustments
- annual evaluation

## Initiation Exit Criteria

Initiation should be considered complete when:

- the project purpose, scope, metrics, and risks are clearly documented
- the personal money-system boundary is explicit
- the five-stage progression model is recorded as part of project scope
- the `2030-12-31` and `2040-12-31` net-worth objectives are recorded as baseline targets
- ML1 can make a clean `Initiating -> Planning` decision
