# Project Charter

**Project:** Build Money Machine
**Project ID:** `BUILD_MONEY_MACHINE`
**Project Path:** `04_INITIATIVES/HillSide_PORTFOLIO/PERSONAL_PROJECTS/BUILD_MONEY_MACHINE`
**Project Type:** Personal
**Stage:** Initiating

## Project Owner

ML1

## Purpose

Design and implement a structured, repeatable system for generating, managing,
and growing personal wealth through disciplined cash flow, intentional
spending, and long-term investing so that ML1 can progress through the five
money-machine stages: stability, security, independence, freedom, and
abundance.

## Objectives

- establish consistent income streams, including active income and future passive
  income potential
- gain full visibility and control over expenses and cash flow
- build a scalable investment system
- reduce financial stress and increase financial independence

## Baseline Long-Horizon Objectives

| Objective | Target Date | Measurement Basis |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Net worth >= CAD 2,000,000 | `2030-12-31` | Total assets less total liabilities, reviewed through the project's defined net-worth tracking system |
| Net worth >= CAD 4,000,000 | `2040-12-31` | Same measurement basis, with annual long-horizon review and periodic reforecasting |

## Financial Progression Model

| Stage | What It Means | Focus | Core Shift |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Financial Stability | Bills are paid, survival debt is avoided, and a small cushion exists | Control and awareness | Reactive -> intentional |
| Financial Security | 3-6 months of expenses are covered and protections are in place | Protection | Fragile -> resilient |
| Financial Independence | Investments can cover basic living expenses | Cash flow from assets | Working for money -> money working for you |
| Financial Freedom | Asset income supports the desired lifestyle | Lifestyle design | Enough -> choice |
| Financial Abundance | Wealth exceeds lifestyle needs and can fund impact, ideas, and legacy | Impact and legacy | Personal freedom -> contribution |

## Scope

### In Scope

- income tracking and optimization
- expense management and budgeting system
- savings and investment strategy
- financial habit development
- stage-qualification rules for the five money-machine stages
- long-horizon pathing from early stability to eventual abundance

### Out of Scope

- high-risk speculation
- short-term trading
- get-rich-quick strategies

## Deliverables

- personal cash flow system
- monthly financial dashboard
- investment plan and allocation strategy
- defined financial independence target and long-horizon net-worth targets
- documented qualification framework for the five money-machine stages

## Timeline

- initial setup: 30-60 days
- system optimization: 3-6 months
- long-term execution: ongoing

## Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Owner | Responsibility |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Project Owner | ML1 | Decision-making and execution |
| Advisor | Optional financial mentor or coach | Guidance, feedback, and optimization support if used |

## Assumptions

- income is currently being earned
- willingness to change financial habits exists
- long-term commitment to the process exists

## Constraints

- limited time and attention
- behavioral consistency
- existing financial obligations

## Stage Plan

1. define the personal money-system boundary and objectives
2. establish the income, spending, saving, and investing control model
3. define the five-stage progression framework and measurement system
4. prepare the `Initiating -> Planning` authorization packet

## Immediate Next Actions

- define the current-state cash flow and expense picture
- set the initial savings, investment, and financial independence targets
- lock the two baseline net-worth objectives for `2030-12-31` and `2040-12-31`
- define the qualification rules for stability, security, independence, freedom,
  and abundance
- choose the monthly dashboard metrics and review cadence
- prepare the initiation packet for ML1 review

## Risks

- inconsistent execution prevents the system from becoming habitual
- spending drift reduces available surplus cash flow
- time and attention constraints weaken follow-through
- market conditions create short-term noise in long-term investing outcomes

## Governance

ML1-controlled stage gate. Planning and executing work are not authorized until ML1 approves transition out of Initiating.
