# Stakeholders

## Stakeholder List

| Name | Role | Interest | Influence | Communication Needs |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Self / ML1 | Owner | Financial independence | High | Ongoing self-review |
| Family (if applicable) | Affected party | Financial security and stability | Medium | Periodic updates |
| Employer | Income source | Stable compensation and performance | Medium | Indirect |
| Financial Advisor (optional) | Guide | Optimization and external perspective | Low-Medium | Periodic consultation |

## Stakeholder Analysis

The primary driver of success is the project owner. Secondary stakeholders can
influence stability, support, and accountability, but they do not control
project outcomes.
