# Communication Plan

| Audience | Cadence | Content | Owner |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| ML1 vision and alignment review | At planning lock, then annually | Ideal day, desired lifestyle, what work stays, what work stops, and what spending aligns with the vision | ML1 |
| ML1 monthly money review | Monthly | Income, expenses, savings rate, investing activity, surplus / deficit, stage qualification, and exceptions | ML1 |
| ML1 quarterly review | Quarterly | Trend review, target-path check, passive-income coverage, category adjustments, and constraint review | ML1 |
| ML1 annual review | Annually | Net-worth progress, stage progression, target-path reforecast, and major system adjustments | ML1 |
| `TO_DO_LIST` | As needed | Action items promoted out of reviews into personal execution | ML1 |
| `BUSINESS_PROJECTS` | On trigger only | Formal handoff if a money engine becomes an actual HillSide business packet | ML1 |

## Review Rhythm

- monthly: close the month, review the dashboard, and record actions
- quarterly: adjust savings, allocation, income-engine assumptions, and expense targets if needed
- annually: assess distance to the 2030 and 2040 goals, refresh the life vision, and reforecast

## Review Inputs

- written vision statement and any updates to desired lifestyle
- monthly income and expense totals
- savings and investment contribution totals
- current asset and liability values
- current passive / portfolio income estimate
- basic living expense target and desired lifestyle expense target
- emergency-fund status
- notable risks, disruptions, or behavioral slippage

## Escalation Rule

Escalate from this personal project into `BUSINESS_PROJECTS` when a money
engine requires a formal venture packet, external operating commitments,
capital deployment beyond personal portfolio management, or ongoing business
execution outside the personal money-system scope.
