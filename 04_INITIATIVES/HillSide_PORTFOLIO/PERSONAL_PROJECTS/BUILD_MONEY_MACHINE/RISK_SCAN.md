# Risk Scan

## Identified Risks

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation Strategy |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Inconsistent habits | High | High | Build simple, repeatable systems |
| Overspending | Medium | High | Implement expense tracking |
| Income disruption | Medium | High | Build emergency fund |
| Lack of discipline | High | High | Schedule regular reviews |
| Market volatility | Medium | Medium | Use a long-term investing strategy |

## Assumptions and Dependencies

- continued income generation
- ability to track finances accurately
- access to basic financial tools such as banking and investing accounts
