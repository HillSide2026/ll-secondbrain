# Matthew 2026 Real Goals

## Purpose

This document is a HillSide-level synthesis of what Matthew's actual 2026 goals
appear to be based on the active HillSide packets.

It is not itself a stage-gate artifact and does not override the underlying
project files. Where this document uses summary language, that language is an
inference from the linked source materials unless an explicit later ML1
clarification is noted.

## Short Version

Matthew's real 2026 goal set appears to be:

1. make Levine Law a controlled cash-flow engine in 2026
2. maintain and grow the Stream Ventures relationship
3. maintain and cultivate the Andersen relationship and collaborating-firm
   path under the Andersen Consulting banner
4. stand up a narrow FinSure-led payments compliance service paired with
   bounded Rhizome implementation
5. monetize venture assets already on an identified monetization path
6. launch and commercialize selected software assets
7. build tighter personal money and wealth-control systems
8. deal with housing in Toronto and make a decision on a potential home
   acquisition in Japan

## What The 2026 Goals Do Not Seem To Be

The repo suggests the 2026 goal set is not:

- broad advisory sprawl
- random venture proliferation
- high-risk speculation or short-term trading
- off-strategy Levine Law intake growth
- treating FinSure or Rhizome as proof that every adjacent path should launch
- liquidating the whole portfolio

## Working Strategic Pattern

The dominant pattern across the HillSide materials is:

- narrow the lanes
- control the engines
- monetize what already has a path
- commercialize a few focused assets
- tighten money governance
- reduce housing uncertainty
- compound from a more stable base
