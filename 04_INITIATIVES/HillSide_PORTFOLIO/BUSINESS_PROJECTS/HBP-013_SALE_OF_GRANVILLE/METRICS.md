## Current Metric Posture

The planning packet is measuring readiness for a later sale option, not active
go-to-market sale execution.

The key metric is not software breadth.

The key metric is whether the licensed entity can be understood, diligenced,
and transferred without depending on an overbuilt support stack.

## Measurement Rule

Planning is complete when the project can answer, with specific evidence:

- is the entity licensed or license-ready?
- is the supporting stack credible but minimal?
- is the licensed entity market-ready enough for later sale launch?
- has scope discipline been maintained?
