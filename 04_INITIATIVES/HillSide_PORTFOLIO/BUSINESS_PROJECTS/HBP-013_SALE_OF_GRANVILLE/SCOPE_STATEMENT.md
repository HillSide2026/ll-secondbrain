# Scope Statement

**Project:** Sale of Granville
**Project ID:** HBP-013
**Stage:** Planning

## In Scope

This planning stage covers the work needed to:

- obtain and document FINTRAC MSB licensing status for `17409052 Canada Inc`
- clarify RPAA status, blockers, and implications
- assemble AML and compliance-readiness materials
- define the minimum supporting EMI, software, connector, and demo posture
- produce a market-ready licensed-entity diligence package
- preserve the option for a later ML1-approved sale of the licensed entity

## Out of Scope

This planning stage does not authorize:

- building and selling BaaS services
- launching a neobank, banking product, or standalone software product
- routing meaningful funds-flow volume through `174`
- migrating FinSure, Levine Law, or other operating lines into `174`
- active sale outreach, LOI negotiation, or transaction closing work

## Scope Boundary

The asset being governed is the licensed entity itself.

Supporting software, EMI relationships, connectors, sandboxes, and demos matter
only if they improve:

- licensing credibility
- market-ready posture
- buyer diligence
- transferability of the licensed entity

They are not themselves the asset being sold.

## Planning Deliverable Boundary

Planning is complete when HBP-013 has:

- a clear licensing-readiness view
- a clear supporting-stack posture
- a coherent market-ready packet for the licensed entity
- explicit criteria for when active sale launch would later be authorized
