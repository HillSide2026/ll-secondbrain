# LL Planning

**Project ID:** `HBP-008`

## Current State

- initiation packet drafted 2026-05-02
- ML1 initiation review pending
- planning not yet authorized

## Objective

Define and govern the 2027 Levine Law operating program as a HillSide oversight project
with four core goals: total firm collections of CAD 400,000, ML1 billings capped at
CAD 240,000, F02 producing 30 paid Health Checks and CAD 90,000 of direct Health
Check revenue, and activation of a part-time contractor associate with
corporate/commercial and financial services experience to deliver the incremental
CAD 160,000 - while governing margin, capacity, and client-quality controls and
staying inside approved Levine Law practice areas, solution frames, and strategy.

This folder also houses the forward annual-plan horizon for `2028` and `2029`
so the HillSide oversight layer covers the immediate leverage-validation year
and the two follow-on decision years.

## Program Context

HBP-008 is the post-HBP-007 operating year. It assumes:

- HBP-007 achieved the CAD 240,000 collection target
- The LLP-033 associate activation gate (3 consecutive months at $20k+ ML1 billings)
  has been met by late 2026
- F02 is operational as a primary acquisition channel
- F03 has produced at least early specialist revenue
- Levine Law is ready to activate a first associate delivery layer under a
  contractor-split model

Within this folder:

- `2027` is the active governed operating-year project
- `2028` is the leverage-deepening and path-choice year
- `2029` is the post-`2028` breakout-or-consolidation year

## Current Compensation, Distribution, And Income Reference

The current planning layer distinguishes between:

- the fixed annual revenue-to-owner line
- the higher total annual revenue-to-owner line after any profit distribution
  made after retained earnings
- the explicit LL planning references for `2027`, `2028`, and `2029`
- the separate HBP-001 wealth-planning comparison layer
- actual after-tax personal take-home, which is not yet modeled directly here

Current working references are:

- inherited `2026` baseline: `CAD 80,000` fixed revenue to ML and
  `CAD 80,000` total revenue to ML in HBP-007
- `2027`: `CAD 85,000` fixed revenue to ML, with `CAD 90,000-95,000` total
  revenue to ML after any year-end profit distribution, in `REVENUE_MODEL.md`
- `2028`: `CAD 90,000` fixed revenue to ML1, with `CAD 100,000` total revenue
  to ML1 after profit distribution, in `ANNUAL_PLAN_2028.md`
- `2029`: `CAD 95,000` fixed revenue to ML1, with `CAD 110,000` total revenue
  to ML1 after profit distribution, in `ANNUAL_PLAN_2029.md`

These are Levine Law revenue-to-owner references, not managing-lawyer
after-tax take-home numbers. They can later be compared against HBP-001 wealth
planning, but they are not determined by HBP-001.
