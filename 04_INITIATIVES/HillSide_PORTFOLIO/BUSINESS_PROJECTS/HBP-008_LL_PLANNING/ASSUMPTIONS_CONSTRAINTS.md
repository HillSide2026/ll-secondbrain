# Assumptions and Constraints

**Project:** LL Planning
**Project ID:** HBP-008

## Assumptions

- HBP-007 achieves at least CAD 240,000 in 2026 collections
- the LLP-033 activation gate (3 consecutive months at $20,000+ ML1 billings) is met
  by Q4 2026
- F02 is operational as a primary acquisition channel before 2027 begins
- F03 has produced at least early authority and specialist revenue by Q4 2026
- F03 fintech retainer pricing, scope, capacity, and rate posture are governed
  by LLP-039, not by HBP-008
- ML1's standard billing rate remains $480/hour for corporate/commercial work and
  $550/hour for regulatory/specialist work
- the contractor associate is engaged on the same billing rates; the 40% split
  is applied to collected fees on delegated matters
- associate billings of CAD 160,000 require approximately 333 billable hours at
  $480/hour — this is a realistic upside for a committed part-time contractor
- the floor assumption is CAD 100,000 in associate billings; total firm revenue
  at floor = CAD 340,000
- the associate is genuinely part-time (supplementing other income) and is not
  expected or required to replace a full-time salaried employee
- sourcing through referral networks is the only realistic path to find the right
  profile; job posting is not expected to produce a viable candidate

## Constraints

- ML1 monthly billings may not exceed CAD 25,000; delegation to the associate is the
  designed release valve above that ceiling
- no associate activation may occur without ML1 approval of the matter eligibility
  list, supervision model, and compensation structure, per LLP-033
- no profitability claim is valid until all cost components are modeled: associate
  compensation, direct matter costs, acquisition cost, and ML1 supervision time
- the associate may not handle specialist regulatory matters unless explicitly
  approved by ML1 for that file type
- the associate compensation structure is a 40% contractor split; no base salary
  commitment is made at initiation
- no hiring, engagement, or file delegation is authorized during the Initiating stage
- 2027 targets must be revised in Q4 2026 against actual HBP-007 performance before
  planning is authorized
- HBP-008 may not treat fintech retainer capacity as expandable beyond the
  LLP-039 solo-owner constraints without a separate ML1-approved update to the
  LLP-039 capacity model
- expanded founder investment in LL strategy, marketing, systems, service
  design, or leverage development must be justified under
  `LL_TIME_INVESTMENT_BENCHMARKS.md`; client delivery obligations are separate
  and remain mandatory once accepted

## Planning Boundary

This stage is limited to program design, economics modeling, profile definition,
and sourcing strategy. Activation of an associate requires separate ML1 approval
per LLP-033.
