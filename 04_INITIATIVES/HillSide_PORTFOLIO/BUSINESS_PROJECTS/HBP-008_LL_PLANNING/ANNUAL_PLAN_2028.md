## Planning Basis

The current firm strategy defines `2028` as the year Levine Law converts
specialist credibility into a larger, stronger Tier 5 specialty platform.

The business plan adds the key `2028` operating question:

> Can the specialty platform support selective leverage without losing control?

The `2028` annual plan therefore exists to hold together four linked themes:

1. preserve Tier 5 niche strength
2. make F02 and F03 repeatable acquisition channels rather than experiments
3. decide whether leverage is truly justified
4. improve firm durability and enterprise value without breaking capacity
   discipline

## Strategic Posture For 2028

- Levine Law remains a controlled specialty boutique, not a generalist
  volume-driven firm.
- F01 remains secondary or optional rather than the growth engine.
- F02 and F03 must both be operating as repeatable, governed channels.
- Any staffing expansion must be justified by throughput, supervision
  readiness, and unit economics.
- The firm's assetization objective becomes active rather than background:
  client relationships, delivery systems, and authority positioning must become
  less ML1-personal and more firm-owned.

## Working LL Distribution To ML1 For 2028

The LL planning packet should carry both a fixed `2028` owner line and a total
`2028` owner-revenue line.

Inherited sequence from the LL planning packet:

- `2026`: `CAD 80,000` fixed and total revenue to ML
- `2027`: `CAD 85,000` fixed revenue to ML, with `CAD 90,000-95,000` total
  revenue to ML after any approved profit distribution

`2028` is the next operating year in that sequence. It is a leverage-deepening
and path-choice year, not yet the breakout extraction year. The payout logic
therefore should be:

- raise the fixed line above the `2027` level
- allow a modest additional owner distribution only after retained earnings are
  set
- preserve room for retained profit, systems, and staffing optionality
- keep payout growth subordinate to real profit, control, and sustainability

| Line | 2028 LL planning reference | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Fixed revenue to ML1 | `CAD 90,000` | Non-discretionary annual owner line |
| Additional distribution to ML1 | about `CAD 10,000` | Profit distribution only after retained earnings |
| Total revenue to ML1 | `CAD 100,000` | Fixed line plus discretionary distribution |
| Retention rule | positive retained profit still required | `2028` is not authorized to maximize owner extraction at the expense of durability |

These are Levine Law planning lines for revenue or payout to ML1, whether later
expressed through salary, bonus, dividend, or another approved owner-payment
form. They are not after-tax take-home numbers.

These lines may later be compared against HBP-001 wealth planning, but they
are not set by HBP-001.

## Entry Conditions For 2028

The year should not be treated as fully open for expansion unless the
following are true by the end of `2027`:

- the Path A vs. Path B identity decision has been made
- F02 is the primary core-business acquisition engine
- F03 is producing repeat specialist opportunities, referrals, and first
  ongoing specialist relationships
- LLP-038 has produced usable Spine definitions, hours budgets, and delegation
  logic for the repeatable service lines
- LLP-033 has enough delivery and supervision evidence to judge whether an
  associate-lawyer layer improves rather than harms economics

If these conditions are not met, `2028` becomes a consolidation year rather
than a leverage year.

## 2028 Annual Objectives

### 1. Protect Specialty Positioning

- deepen Levine Law's standing in payments / MSB / RPAA / AML work while
  remaining inside Tier 5
- keep the market-facing message narrow, specialist, and durable
- avoid revenue moves that dilute niche authority or re-center the firm on
  reactive generalist intake

### 2. Normalize F02 As The Core Operator Channel

F02's governed design is now treated as a standing operating channel rather
than a launch-stage packet.

Required inherited F02 rules:

- use the three-tier Health Check ladder as the canonical entry structure
  (`CAD 2,000` / `CAD 3,500` / `CAD 6,000`)
- preserve the mandatory qualification-call and evidence-record gate
- keep remediation as the immediate conversion path and fractional counsel as
  the downstream relationship layer
- preserve the distinction between F02 and F03 surfaces so the firm does not
  attract the wrong inquiry mix

`2028` F02 objective:

- maintain F02 as a repeatable, high-fit core-business engine that brings in
  mature Ontario operators and converts them into remediation and ongoing
  counsel without bypassing fit or pricing discipline

### 3. Normalize F03 As The Authority And Specialist Channel

F03's planning packet is treated as the source surface for channel discipline.

Required inherited F03 rules:

- preserve ICP-03 positioning and the payments / regulatory niche
- keep website, LinkedIn, outreach, and attribution logic coherent
- preserve explicit tracking of F3-sourced consultations and matters
- keep F02 and F03 offer pages, entry logic, and messaging separate

`2028` F03 objective:

- operate F03 as a functioning authority channel that produces repeat
  specialist matters, referral flow, and ongoing counsel relationships rather
  than isolated content outputs

### 4. Make Delivery Leverage A Real Decision

`2028` is not authorized to add leverage by ambition alone.

The leverage question must be answered using live evidence from:

- LLP-038 Delivery Spine
- LLP-033 Associate Lawyer
- LLP-030 financial modeling

The decision standard is:

- delegated work must be spec-able
- supervision load must be bounded
- contribution margin under leverage must remain attractive
- increased complexity must create real enterprise value rather than merely
  more revenue with less control

### 5. Increase Firm Durability

By the end of `2028`, Levine Law should be able to show:

- more documented workflows and templates
- clearer repeatable service lines
- less dependence on purely reactive F01 intake
- more client relationships that are attached to the firm model rather than
  only to ML1 personally

## Channel Posture In 2028

| Channel | 2028 role | Operating rule |
|---|---|---|
| `F01` | Secondary or optional demand source | Do not let F01 become the main growth engine again |
| `F02` | Primary core-business acquisition engine | Keep qualification, pricing, and conversion discipline intact |
| `F03` | Primary specialist authority engine | Keep attribution, positioning, and ICP boundaries intact |

## Delivery And Staffing Posture In 2028

| Question | 2028 posture |
|---|---|
| Associate-lawyer layer | Activate only if repeatable delegated work and unit economics are proven |
| Senior-lawyer or partner layer | Evaluate only after the associate or bounded delegation model is credible, or if the matter mix independently justifies it |
| Support staffing | Add only where it removes true bottlenecks without weakening margin discipline |

## Year-End Success Conditions

At the end of `2028`, Levine Law should be able to say:

- F02 and F03 are both repeatable channels
- F01 is no longer the default growth dependency
- the firm has enough proof to choose and sustain either a controlled-boutique
  path or an early-platform path
- any leverage layer in place is justified by evidence, not aspiration
- Levine Law is stronger, deeper, and more durable inside Tier 5 than it was
  at the start of the year

## Open Decision

This annual plan does not resolve the Path A vs. Path B identity question.
That remains the key gating decision entering `2028`.
