# Metrics

Project ID: `HBP-002`
Stage: `Initiating`

## Primary SMART Goal

By `2026-06-30`, Matthew Holdings has a monthly management-reporting pack,
closed and reviewed within 10 business days of month-end, that reports:

- portfolio cash flow
- portfolio free cash flow
- entity-level cash flow and free cash flow for Levine Law, `175 / FinSure`, and any additional Matthew-established businesses
- realized YellowBricks sale proceeds and any residual post-closing cash movements until fully settled

## In-Scope Reporting Boundary

- Matthew Holdings cash, cash equivalents, and securities
- Levine Law
- `175 / FinSure`
- any additional Matthew-established businesses admitted to the HillSide branch
- YellowBricks realized sale proceeds and post-closing cash events until final settlement

## Metric Definitions

| Metric | Definition | Core Formula | Primary Source |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Portfolio cash flow | Net cash generated by holdings-level cash and securities before discretionary redeployment | portfolio cash receipts less portfolio cash expenses, taxes, interest, and other mandatory cash outflows | bank statements, broker statements, general ledger |
| Portfolio free cash flow | Cash available from the portfolio after required holding-company overhead and committed reserve funding | portfolio cash flow less committed reserve funding and mandatory holding-company overhead not already captured above | bank statements, general ledger, management reserve schedule |
| Business cash flow | Net cash generated by an operating business before discretionary owner distributions, financing inflows, and growth investment decisions | operating cash receipts less operating cash expenses, cash taxes, and working-capital movement | entity bookkeeping files, bank statements, general ledger |
| Business free cash flow | Cash an operating business generates after sustaining the business at its current service level | business cash flow less sustaining capex, mandatory debt principal, and required working-capital reserve increases | entity bookkeeping files, fixed-asset records, debt schedules |
| Realized exit cash flow | Net cash generated by a closed disposition such as YellowBricks | sale proceeds collected less post-closing costs, claims, indemnities, and residual close-out disbursements | closing statements, bank records, legal close-out file |

## Reporting Rules

- Unrealized gains and losses are excluded from cash-flow and free-cash-flow metrics.
- Intercompany transfers are eliminated from the consolidated view and tracked separately if needed for entity reconciliation.
- New investment deployments and discretionary owner distributions are shown separately from free cash flow.
- YellowBricks is not treated as an active operating business, but its realized sale proceeds and post-closing cash activity remain in scope until fully settled.
