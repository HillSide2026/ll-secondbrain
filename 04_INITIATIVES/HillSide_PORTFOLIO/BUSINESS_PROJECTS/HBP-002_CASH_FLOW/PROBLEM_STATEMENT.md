# Problem Statement

Project: `HBP-002` - Cash Flow

## Problem

HillSide needs better visibility and control over branch-level cash generation
and deployment, but the current reporting boundary, data sources, and review
discipline are not yet defined well enough to support confident decision-making.

Current gaps:

- no clearly approved scope for which entities, accounts, or cash categories are in view
- no single governed reporting view for current cash position and movement
- no defined cadence for forecast review, variance detection, or deployment decisions
- no decision-ready plan for how much systemization is actually needed

## Proposed Solution

Stand up a controlled initiation-stage strategic project to define the
cash-flow scope, reporting model, and planning boundary before building a
larger operating system around it.

## Impact of Not Solving

- capital deployment decisions may remain slower or lower-confidence than necessary
- short-term cash pressure or underutilization may be harder to detect early
- effort may be wasted on overbuilding tools before the real control problem is defined
