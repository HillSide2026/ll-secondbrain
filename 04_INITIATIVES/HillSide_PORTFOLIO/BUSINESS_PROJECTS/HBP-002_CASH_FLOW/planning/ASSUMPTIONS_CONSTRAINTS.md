# Assumptions and Constraints

Project ID: `HBP-002`
Stage: `Planning`

## Assumptions

- the approximate current asset base remains about CAD 1.6M in cash and securities under Matthew Holdings
- Levine Law and `175 / FinSure` records can be classified consistently enough for planning
- YellowBricks close-out records are available for realized exit-cash treatment
- current source systems are imperfect but sufficient to support an execution-ready model

## Constraints

- planning must remain a reporting-and-control project, not an accounting-system replacement
- accounting records remain canonical
- unrealized gains and losses are not part of cash-flow or free-cash-flow metrics
- no automation build, software purchase, or advisory spend is assumed without explicit ML1 approval
- planning should not blur portfolio cash flow with business cash flow or exit proceeds

## Locked Planning Direction

Currently locked:

- portfolio cash flow and portfolio free cash flow are in scope
- business cash flow and business free cash flow are in scope
- Levine Law, `175 / FinSure`, and YellowBricks close-out cash events are in scope
- the monthly management pack must close within 10 business days of month-end

Still not locked:

- final treatment of any edge-case intercompany movements
- exact report layout and review sequence
- whether any additional Matthew-established businesses must be added immediately

## Planning Boundary

This planning stage is limited to reporting-boundary lock, formula design,
source-record mapping, close-process design, and execution-readiness
preparation.
