# Success Criteria

Project: `HBP-002` - Cash Flow

## Criteria

| Criterion | Threshold | Verification Source |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Scope defined | In-scope entities, accounts, and cash categories are documented clearly | `PROJECT_CHARTER.md` |
| Reporting model defined | One minimum viable reporting view and review cadence are described | `PROJECT_CHARTER.md` and `STAKEHOLDERS.md` |
| Forecasting boundary clarified | Near-term forecast horizon and core assumptions are identified | `RISK_SCAN.md` and planning-gate notes |
| Planning gate packet ready | ML1 receives a credible basis for whether Planning should begin | `APPROVAL_RECORD.md` and initiation packet review |

## Completion Rule

Initiation-stage success means the project is specific enough to support an
ML1 decision on whether to invest in a fuller planning and implementation
phase. It does not require a complete cash-management system to be built.
