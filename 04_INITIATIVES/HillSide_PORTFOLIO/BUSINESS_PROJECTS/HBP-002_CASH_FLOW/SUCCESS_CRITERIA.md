# Success Criteria

Project: `HBP-002` - Cash Flow

## Criteria

| Criterion | Threshold | Verification Source |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Reporting boundary fixed | In-scope holdings, securities, and operating businesses are documented clearly, including Levine Law, `175 / FinSure`, and YellowBricks close-out activity | `PROJECT_CHARTER.md` and `METRICS.md` |
| Metric definitions fixed | Portfolio cash flow, portfolio free cash flow, business cash flow, and business free cash flow are defined with inclusions, exclusions, and primary source records | `METRICS.md` |
| Close and review model defined | The monthly close timeline, review owner, and variance process are documented clearly enough for planning | `PROJECT_CHARTER.md` and `STAKEHOLDERS.md` |
| Opening baseline documented | The initiation packet records the approximate current asset base and how key holdings and businesses are classified for reporting | `README.md`, `BUSINESS_CASE.md`, and `METRICS.md` |
| Planning gate packet ready | ML1 receives a credible basis for whether Planning should begin | `APPROVAL_RECORD.md` and initiation packet review |

## Completion Rule

Initiation-stage success means the project is specific enough to support an
ML1 decision on whether to invest in a fuller planning and implementation
phase. It does not require a complete cash-management system to be built, but
it does require real metric definitions rather than generic reporting language.
