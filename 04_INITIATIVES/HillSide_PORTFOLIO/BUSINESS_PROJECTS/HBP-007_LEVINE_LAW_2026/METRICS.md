## Monthly Control Metrics

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Metric ID | `ll_2026_monthly_oversight_pack` |
| SMART Goal | SG-03 |
| Definition | A monthly oversight pack covering all four dimensions — collections, margin, capacity, and client-quality — produced and reviewed for each calendar month in 2026 |
