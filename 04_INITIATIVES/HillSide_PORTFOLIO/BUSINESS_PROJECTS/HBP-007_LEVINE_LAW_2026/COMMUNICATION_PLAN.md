# Communication Plan

**Project:** Levine Law 2026
**Project ID:** HBP-007
**Stage:** Planning

---

## Primary Communication — Monthly Budget Report

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Report name | Monthly Budget Report |
| Producer | Levine Law accountant |
| Recipient | ML1 |
| Frequency | Monthly |
| Content | Actual collections vs budget, margin, and financial position for the month |
| Purpose | Provides the financial data that drives the monthly oversight pack review |
| Format | Accountant-determined; must separate ML1-docketed fee collections from other-fee-earner fees |

This report is the primary data source for the HBP-007 monthly oversight
pack. It is not produced by this project — it is received from the
accountant and reviewed by ML1.

---

## Monthly Oversight Pack Review

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Producer | ML1 (drawing on monthly budget report) |
| Audience | ML1 / HillSide internal |
| Frequency | Monthly |
| Content | Collections vs CAD 20,000 monthly target; margin; capacity; client-quality; ML1-docketed fee collections separated |
| Purpose | Primary execution control for HBP-007; confirms lane discipline and tracks progress toward year-end goals |

---

## Communication Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|---------------|
| Levine Law accountant | Produce monthly budget report; ensure ML1-docketed fee collections are separated from other-fee-earner fees |
| ML1 | Receive and review monthly budget report; produce and review monthly oversight pack; escalate to HillSide portfolio level if significant variance |

---

## Escalation

If cumulative collections fall more than CAD 20,000 behind the monthly
target rate at any mid-year review, ML1 reviews intake posture and
considers whether a HillSide portfolio-level adjustment is warranted.

No formal escalation path to external parties — this is an internal
HillSide oversight project.
