# Success Criteria

Project: HBP-009 - 2027 Business Ideas

## Criteria

| Criterion | Threshold | Verification Source |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Idea universe captured | Candidate 2027 business ideas are captured in a governed backlog | `IDEA_BACKLOG.md` |
| Screening logic defined | Ideas can be compared against explicit strategic and operating criteria | screening notes or planning artifact |
| Strongest candidates separated | Stronger ideas are distinguished from weak, premature, or misfiled ideas | shortlist or decision memo |
| No premature execution | No idea is promoted into active build or spend without separate ML1 approval | approval record / project charter |
| Planning-ready view produced | ML1 can decide which concepts warrant deeper planning | recommendation memo or project update |

## Completion Rule

HBP-009 is successful at the initiating stage when it produces a controlled
decision surface for 2027 business ideas, not when any particular idea is
launched.

The project should reduce noise, prevent premature execution, and make the
strongest candidates easier to compare.
