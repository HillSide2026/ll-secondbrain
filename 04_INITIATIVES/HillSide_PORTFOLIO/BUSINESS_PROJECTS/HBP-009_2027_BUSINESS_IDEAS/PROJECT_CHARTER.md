# Project Charter

**Project:** 2027 Business Ideas  
**Project ID:** HBP-009  
**Project Path:** `04_INITIATIVES/HillSide_PORTFOLIO/BUSINESS_PROJECTS/HBP-009_2027_BUSINESS_IDEAS`  
**Project Type:** Strategic  
**Stage:** Initiating

## Sponsor

ML1

## Mission

Create a clear, controlled process for identifying and shaping the most
promising 2027 HillSide business ideas before any idea is pushed into active
execution.

## Success Criteria

- 2027 idea universe is captured in one governed place
- screening logic is defined clearly enough to compare ideas
- the strongest ideas are separated from weak or premature ideas
- ML1 receives a planning-ready view of which concepts warrant deeper work

## Stage Plan

1. capture and group candidate 2027 business ideas
2. define screening criteria and decision factors
3. narrow to the strongest candidates for validating and modeling
4. prepare a planning authorization packet for ML1

## Immediate Next Actions

- identify the core 2027 idea themes to include in scope
- define the screen for strategic fit, capital needs, and execution complexity
- separate ideas that belong in HillSide from ideas that belong elsewhere
- define what evidence is needed before deeper planning begins

## Risks

- scope drift if the project becomes an undifferentiated catch-all for every
  possible business idea
- schedule drag if ideas are not narrowed early enough for comparison
- budget exposure if research, advisory spend, or build work starts before
  ideas are screened properly

## Governance

ML1-controlled stage gate. No planning or executing work is authorized until
ML1 approves the transition out of Initiating.
