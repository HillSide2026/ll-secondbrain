# Risk Scan

## Scope Risks

- the project may become too broad if every possible business concept is mixed
  into one undifferentiated list
- the project may blur idea capture, validating, and actual execution-stage
  work

## Schedule Risks

- screening may stall if ideas are not grouped and narrowed quickly
- planning may be delayed if the decision framework is not defined early

## Budget Risks

- advisory spend or exploratory work could start before ideas are screened
- management attention could be consumed by low-quality or premature concepts

## Current Risk Posture

The biggest immediate risk is scope drift. The project should stay focused on
capturing, screening, and prioritizing 2027 business ideas rather than acting
like an execution project.
