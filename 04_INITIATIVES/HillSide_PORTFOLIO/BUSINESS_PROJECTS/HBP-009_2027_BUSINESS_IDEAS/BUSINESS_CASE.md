# Business Case

Project: HBP-009 - 2027 Business Ideas

## Strategic Rationale

HBP-009 exists because HillSide has multiple plausible 2027 business ideas, but
uncontrolled idea capture can create distraction, premature build work, and
unclear entity boundaries.

The business case is to create a governed screening layer before capital,
partner time, or ML1 execution capacity is committed.

## Thesis

The highest-value output is not a long idea list. It is a disciplined shortlist
of ideas that can be compared by:

- strategic fit;
- capital requirement;
- ML1 time burden;
- delegability;
- entity fit;
- regulatory or professional-boundary risk;
- speed to validation; and
- potential enterprise value.

## Expected Value

| Value Driver | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Focus | Prevents every attractive idea from becoming an active project |
| Capital discipline | Keeps spend and provider diligence gated until ideas survive screening |
| Entity clarity | Separates HillSide, 175, Levine Law, and personal ideas before execution |
| Better sequencing | Helps ML1 choose which ideas deserve attention in 2027 |
| Reusable screen | Creates a repeatable method for evaluating new business concepts |

## Failure Mode Avoided

Without HBP-009, the portfolio risks:

- mixing legal-practice ideas with HillSide business ideas;
- overinvesting in interesting but non-delegable concepts;
- pursuing ideas that require more capital or operating capacity than expected;
- starting diligence before the business case is clear; and
- losing the best ideas inside an undifferentiated backlog.

## Recommendation

Proceed as an initiating-stage strategic screen. HBP-009 should not itself
authorize execution. It should produce a shortlist and decision basis for ML1 to
promote, defer, merge, or close specific 2027 business ideas.
