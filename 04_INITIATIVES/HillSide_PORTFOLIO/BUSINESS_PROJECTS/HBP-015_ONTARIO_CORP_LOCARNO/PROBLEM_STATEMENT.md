# Problem Statement

**Project:** HBP-015 — Ontario Corp / Locarno

## Problem

`1001494374 Ontario Corp` holds two regulatory assets — a FINTRAC MSB registration and an RPAA registration — that have real sale value but no controlled plan for realizing it.

Current gaps:

- No confirmed sale track: the entity can be sold as a shell (Track A), built into a regulated infrastructure asset (Track B), or taken further with a direct sponsor-bank relationship (Track C). Without a decision, the entity sits as an undirected regulatory holding.
- No verified FINTRAC standing: registration was obtained but current compliance status (outstanding deficiencies, nil-reporting obligations, AML program adequacy for a dormant entity) has not been formally confirmed.
- No EMI relationship: Track B and C require a direct EMI agreement. ConnectPay is the primary target. No written indication of willingness to onboard has been obtained.
- No market-ready package: a buyer cannot currently evaluate the entity without a coherent diligence narrative covering regulatory status, structure, and any supporting infrastructure.
- Project Locarno existed as a strategy note in the entity branch, not as a governed project. No sale thesis, value thesis, or execution plan was controlled.

## Impact of Not Solving

- The entity remains a stranded regulatory asset with no disposition path.
- FINTRAC standing may drift into deficiency if dormant-period obligations go unmonitored.
- Track B/C value — up to CAD $650,000+ — is forfeited if the entity is sold as a shell by default rather than by choice.
- The Q4 2026 restructuring (Matthew Capital, Kendal Securities) depends in part on proceeds from this sale. Undefined disposition timing creates downstream restructuring risk.
- ML1 time may be spent on ad hoc counterparty conversations not connected to a sale thesis.

## Proposed Solution

HBP-015 provides a governed project structure for `1001494374 Ontario Corp`: confirm FINTRAC standing, select the sale track, build the market-ready package for that track, and sell the entity on ML1 direction.
