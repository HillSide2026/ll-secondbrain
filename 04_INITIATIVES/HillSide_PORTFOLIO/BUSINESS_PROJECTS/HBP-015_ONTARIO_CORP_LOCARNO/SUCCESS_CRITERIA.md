# Success Criteria

**Project:** HBP-015 — Ontario Corp / Locarno

## Primary Success Threshold

`1001494374 Ontario Corp` is sold on ML1 direction at the highest achievable track, with proceeds flowing into `17513721 Canada Inc` on the Q4 2026 restructuring timeline.

## Success by Track

| Track | Success Definition | Working Value Marker |
|---|---|---|
| Track A | Entity sold as a clean MSB shell with verified FINTRAC standing, clean AML program, and no legacy liabilities | CAD $50,000 |
| Track B Minimum | Entity sold as more than a shell — RPAA-ready, EMI indication obtained, AML framework complete | CAD $50,000 |
| Track B Preferred | Entity sold as a regulated payments infrastructure asset — RPAA registered, EMI agreement signed, safeguarding documented, ledger and AML demo-ready | CAD $200,000 |
| Track B Stretch | Fully contracted institutional stack — EMI signed, compliance tested, flows documented, buyer can inherit or replace all relationships | CAD $650,000 |
| Track C | Track B Stretch plus direct sponsor-bank relationship contracted and referenceable | TBD above $650,000 |

## Minimum Viable Outcome

Track A completion: FINTRAC standing verified, AML program documented, corporate records clean, buyer handoff memo complete.

## Process Success Criteria

| Criterion | Target |
|---|---|
| Track selected | ML1 decides A, B, or C before Q3 2026 |
| Market-ready package assembled | Before active sale outreach |
| No overbuild | Software and infrastructure spend remains subordinate to the sale thesis |
| Proceeds received | Into `17513721 Canada Inc` on ML1-approved timetable |
| Restructuring compatibility | Disposition timing is compatible with Q4 2026 Matthew Capital / Kendal Securities restructuring |

## Failure Conditions

- FINTRAC standing is found to be deficient and cannot be remediated before sale
- Entity is sold at Track A value by default when Track B was achievable
- The project drifts into building an operating payments business
- EMI or sponsor-bank terms impair transferability and reduce buyer attractiveness
- Proceeds do not arrive in time to support the Q4 2026 restructuring
