# Risk Scan

Project: `HBP-010` - Rhizome White Label

## Strategic Risks

- partner objectives or incentives may diverge from Matthew Holdings once terms become concrete
- the model may create structural dependency on a single external channel or platform

## Financial Risks

- revenue share or margin logic may be too weak to justify the dependency created
- customization or support requirements may erode the economics before launch

## Scope Risks

- the phrase "white label" may hide materially different business models that should not be treated as one path
- the project may drift into implementation assumptions before the decision boundary is locked

## Schedule Risks

- partner coordination may delay clarity on responsibilities, economics, or support obligations
- the decision may stall if the opportunity is not narrowed early enough into real options

## Budget Risks

- diligence, advisory, or prototype spend may begin before the model deserves deeper investment

## Key Assumptions

- there is enough preliminary signal to justify structured evaluation
- no external commitment has been made and no launch timeline is currently authorized
- the first useful outcome is a cleaner decision, not a faster implementation

## Go / No-Go Judgment

Proceed to Planning only if the model, economics, client ownership, and
dependency controls are clear enough to support a bounded next stage.
