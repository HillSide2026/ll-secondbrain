# Assumptions and Constraints

Project ID: `HBP-010`
Stage: `Planning`

## Assumptions

- there is enough genuine opportunity signal to justify deeper planning
- at least one Rhizome-linked structure can be described concretely enough for comparison
- the most important near-term output is decision quality, not speed to launch
- partner-side clarity can be obtained without committing to a deal structure prematurely

## Constraints

- no external commitment, launch promise, or commercial term is authorized during planning
- no build or integration work is authorized during planning
- any path that leaves client ownership or support ownership materially ambiguous should be treated as non-viable
- material spend must remain tightly bounded until ML1 approves a next-step path

## Planning Boundary

This planning stage is limited to option definition, control-boundary framing,
economics, dependency assessment, and ML1 decision preparation.
