# Assumptions and Constraints

Project ID: `HBP-010`
Stage: `Planning`

## Assumptions

- there is enough genuine opportunity signal to justify deeper planning
- a services-led wrapper remains the most credible way to capture value without ceding full client control
- the most important near-term output is decision quality, not speed to launch
- partner-side clarity can be obtained without committing to a deal structure prematurely

## Constraints

- no product or service-delivery execution work should be pulled into `HBP-010`
- no FinSure build-out, CAMLO staffing, or payment services delivery work belongs inside this project packet
- any path that leaves client ownership or support ownership materially ambiguous should be treated as non-viable
- material spend must remain tightly bounded until ML1 approves a next-step path

## Locked Planning Direction

Planning has now selected a Rhizome services-led wrapper.

Currently locked:

- `HBP-010` execution is limited to Rhizome partner structure, control boundary, dependency protection, economics, and implementation planning
- service-delivery execution remains outside this project
- `HBP-011` remains the packet for FinSure and payment services compliance service work

Still not locked:

- final Rhizome commercial terms
- precise support and escalation commitments
- dependency protection and unwind mechanics
