# Risk Register

Project ID: `HBP-010`
Stage: `Planning`

| Risk | Category | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Partner incentives diverge once economics become concrete | Strategic | M | H | Compare options against explicit control and incentive criteria before any go-forward recommendation |
| Dependency on one partner becomes too great for the available upside | Strategic | M | H | Define acceptable dependency protections and treat missing protections as a no-go trigger |
| Commercial model does not justify support or customization burden | Financial | M | H | Establish minimum acceptable economics in `METRICS.md` and compare each viable option against them |
| The project drifts from option framing into premature implementation design | Scope | M | M | Keep planning limited to decision preparation and avoid execution design detail |
| Partner or internal clarification takes longer than expected | Schedule | M | M | Narrow the option set early and escalate unresolved blockers to ML1 quickly |
| Diligence or exploratory spend expands before a viable option is identified | Budget | L | M | Keep spend bounded until one option survives the planning thresholds |
