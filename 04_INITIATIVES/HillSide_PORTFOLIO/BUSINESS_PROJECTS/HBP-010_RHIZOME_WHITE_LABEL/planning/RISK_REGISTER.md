# Risk Register

Project ID: `HBP-010`
Stage: `Planning`

| Risk | Category | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Partner incentives diverge once commercial terms become concrete | Strategic | M | H | Keep partner negotiation tied to explicit control, economics, and exit criteria |
| Dependency on one partner becomes too great for the available upside | Strategic | M | H | Define dependency protections and treat missing protections as a no-go trigger |
| Commercial model does not justify support or customization burden | Financial | M | H | Keep minimum acceptable economics explicit and reject terms that miss the baseline |
| `HBP-010` drifts into `HBP-011` service-delivery execution | Scope | M | H | Keep Rhizome partner-structure work separate from FinSure and payment-services delivery work |
| Partner or internal clarification takes longer than expected | Schedule | M | M | Escalate unresolved partner-term blockers quickly and keep scope narrow |
| Diligence or exploratory spend expands before the wrapper model is protected adequately | Budget | L | M | Keep spend bounded until dependency protections and minimum commercial terms are explicit |
