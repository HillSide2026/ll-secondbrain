# Problem Statement

Project: `HBP-010` - Rhizome White Label

## Problem

A Rhizome-linked white-label opportunity has been identified, but the actual
model is still too ambiguous to support a clean go / hold / no-go decision.

Current gaps:

- it is unclear whether the opportunity is best understood as software white label, services wrapper, referral arrangement, or hybrid
- client ownership, delivery responsibility, support expectations, and compliance boundary are not yet defined clearly
- the economics, dependency exposure, and integration burden are not yet decision-ready
- the opportunity is not yet framed inside a governed project packet

## Proposed Solution

Stand up a controlled initiation-stage decision project to define the realistic
model options, surface the main risks and constraints, and prepare an ML1
decision on whether this path should advance.

## Impact of Not Solving

- partner conversations could outrun internal clarity on structure and economics
- Matthew Holdings could drift into channel dependency without enough control
- integration or customization effort could be implied before the opportunity deserves it
