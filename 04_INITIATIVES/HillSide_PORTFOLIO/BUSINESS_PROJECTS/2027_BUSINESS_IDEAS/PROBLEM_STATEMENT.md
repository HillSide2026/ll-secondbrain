# Problem Statement

HillSide needs a structured way to capture, compare, and refine potential 2027
business ideas.

Right now, early concepts can drift between informal notes, disconnected
opportunities, and live project conversations. That creates three problems:

- weak ideas can consume attention before they are screened properly
- strong ideas can remain underdeveloped because they are not compared against
  a clear decision framework
- capital, partner time, or execution energy can be pulled forward before a
  concept is decision-ready

This project exists to create a controlled front-end for 2027 business idea
formation so the strongest opportunities can be identified and developed
deliberately.

If the project is not created, HillSide risks mixing idea generation,
validation, and execution in a way that reduces clarity and increases wasted
effort.
