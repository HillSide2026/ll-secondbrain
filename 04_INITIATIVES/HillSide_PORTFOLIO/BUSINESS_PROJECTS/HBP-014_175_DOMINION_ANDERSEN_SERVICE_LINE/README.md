# 175 Dominion Andersen Service Line

**Project ID:** `HBP-014`

This project governs the build-out of `17513721 Canada Inc`, operating as
Dominion Partners, as an Andersen Consulting collaborating firm for
international trade advisory services.

## Purpose

Build 175 as the Andersen Consulting collaborating firm for international trade
advisory services across five service lines: trade remedies, market access,
data centers, mining, and payment services.

## Current State

- project opened on `2026-05-23`
- entity home exists under `MATTHEW_HOLDINGS_17513721_CANADA_INC`
- Dominion Platform architecture and rate schedule already exist
- Andersen file-level pipeline is tracked in Levine Law matters
- no converted Andersen cash has been recorded yet in the current matter files

## Strategic Positioning

`17513721 Canada Inc` is being positioned as Dominion Partners, an Andersen
Consulting collaborating firm focused on international trade advisory services.

Dominion / 175 has five working service lines:

- trade remedies
- market access
- data centers
- mining
- payment services

Each service line should be positioned for Andersen-facing purposes before it
is advanced.

This project is the controlled HillSide project for turning that positioning
into a repeatable business line owned by 175.

## In Scope

- collaborating-firm strategy and governance
- Dominion Partners service-line positioning
- classification and positioning of the five Dominion / 175 service lines
- initiative design inside the approved service-line structure
- Andersen / Sidera relationship management at the business-line level
- rate-card, staffing, and delivery-model refinement
- business-development pipeline governance for 175-owned international trade
  advisory services
- coordination with Dominion Platform assets

## Out of Scope

- Levine Law legal-service delivery, except as a linked operating matter
- individual client files once formally opened under Levine Law or Andersen
- sale of `17409052 Canada Inc` / Granville
- sale of Ontario MSB Corp
- FinSure execution except where it supports the 175 / Dominion platform story
