# Scope Statement

Project: `HBP-014` - 175 Dominion Andersen Service Line

## In Scope

HBP-014 governs the build-out of `17513721 Canada Inc` / Dominion Partners as
an Andersen Consulting collaborating firm across five service lines:

- trade remedies
- market access
- data centers
- mining
- payment services

The project includes:

- Andersen-facing service-line positioning
- service-line qualification criteria
- rate, staffing, and delivery-model planning
- collaborating-firm agreement review and constraint capture
- Sidera relationship strategy and channel-conflict control
- Dominion Platform alignment
- opportunity qualification and pipeline governance
- cross-entity economics rules for 175 and Levine Law

## Out of Scope

HBP-014 does not govern:

- individual client-file legal delivery once a matter is opened
- external launch or public positioning changes without later approval
- client commitments or staffing commitments before the relevant gate
- sale of Granville / `17409052 Canada Inc`
- sale of Ontario MSB Corp
- FinSure execution except where it supports the Dominion / 175 service-line
  story

## Boundaries

HBP-014 is the business-line strategy project. The related Andersen matters
remain the matter-level operating records:

- `26-1639-00001` - Andersen relationship management
- `26-1639-00002` - Andersen trade remedies
- `26-1639-00003` - Andersen market access

Cash receipts through 175 and operating credit to Levine Law must remain
separately tracked.

## Planning Deliverable

Planning should produce a controlled package that lets ML1 decide whether HBP-014
is ready to move into execution, including:

- approved service-line positioning
- first active service-line priority
- opportunity qualification standard
- rate and staffing assumptions
- agreement / brand-permission constraints
- relationship-management guardrails
- metrics for pipeline and economics
