# Communication Plan

Project: `HBP-014` - 175 Dominion Andersen Service Line

## Communication Principles

- Keep Andersen-facing language disciplined and consistent with the
  collaborating-firm agreement.
- Protect the Sidera relationship before expanding ally-building activity.
- Do not overstate trade-remedies capability, payment-services authority, or
  staffing depth.
- Keep 175 / Dominion business-line strategy separate from Levine Law matter
  delivery.

| Audience | Purpose | Channel / Artifact | Cadence |

| ML1 | Stage-gate decisions, service-line approval, risk decisions | HBP-014 artifacts and approval record | As needed |

| Levine Law matter records | Matter-level Andersen activity alignment | `26-1639-00001`, `26-1639-00002`, `26-1639-00003` | When opportunity status changes |

## External Communications

No external communication is authorized by the Planning gate alone.

External communications require ML1 approval and should be checked against:

- Andersen collaborating-firm agreement
- Sidera relationship strategy
- service-line positioning
- conflict / fee-path considerations

## Message Control

Approved high-level positioning:

> Dominion Partners is being built as the 17513721 Canada Inc platform for
> Andersen-routed international trade advisory work across trade remedies,
> market access, data centers, mining, and payment services.

Avoid:

- unsupported Andersen brand claims
- implying operational payment-services activity without scope confirmation
- presenting 175 as a deep trade-remedies bench before support is confirmed
- mixing 175 receipts with Levine Law receipts
