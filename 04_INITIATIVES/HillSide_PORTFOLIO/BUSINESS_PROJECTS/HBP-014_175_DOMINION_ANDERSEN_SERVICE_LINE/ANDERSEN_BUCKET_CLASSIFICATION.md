# Andersen Service-Line Classification

## Rule

For HBP-014, 175 / Dominion has five service lines:

1. **Trade Remedies**
2. **Market Access**
3. **Data Centers**
4. **Mining**
5. **Payment Services**

Each initiative should be positioned inside one of those service lines before
it is advanced.

## Working Classification

175 / Dominion has five working service lines:

- trade remedies
- market access
- data centers
- mining
- payment services

Those service lines are the HBP-014 service architecture.

| Dominion Service Line | Positioning | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Trade Remedies | Anti-dumping, countervailing, safeguards, CITT participation, exclusions, injury, monitoring, and related proceedings | Includes complainant, respondent, importer, downstream user, interested-party, exclusion, or monitoring work |
| Market Access | Market entry, Canada trade-readiness, global trade diversification, non-U.S. market development, approvals, routes to market, and regulatory barriers | Includes Canadian market entry and global trade diversification diagnostics |
| Data Centers | Data-centre and critical-infrastructure advisory tied to Andersen opportunities | Includes regulatory, project, infrastructure, energy, and cross-border advisory angles where data centres are the industry focus |
| Mining | Lowest-priority service line unless a named-company mandate appears | Includes mining, ESG mining disputes, Latin America mining, junior-miner advisory, TSX Venture / LatAm opportunities, and ESG / forced-labour reporting angles where mining is the industry focus |
| Payment Services | Stablecoin adoption, cross-border treasury, payment rails, licensing, registration, and regulatory compliance for payment services | Includes HBP-011 Payment Services Consulting Line as the execution packet for the regulated-scope consulting offer; current lead magnet is "Is Your MSB Ready for FINTRAC Effectiveness Review?" |

## Classification Test

Before advancing an initiative, record:

- proposed initiative name
- service line
- client problem
- jurisdiction or proceeding
- decision-maker / channel contact
- concrete scope
- timing driver
- fee path through Andersen / 175

## Open Classification Questions

- Whether data centers and mining should be positioned as industry verticals
  with sub-mapping into market access / trade remedies when needed.
- Whether payment services should sit as a standalone service line in all
  Andersen-facing materials or be presented only when tied to cross-border
  treasury, stablecoin adoption, or regulatory compliance.
