# Problem Statement

Project: `HBP-014` - 175 Dominion Andersen Service Line

## Problem

175 has a strategically valuable Andersen Consulting collaborating-firm
opportunity, but the business line is not yet governed by a dedicated HillSide
project.

Current gaps:

- 175 / Dominion Partners has entity and platform records, but no project-level
  business-line control
- Andersen operational matters exist, but they are matter-level records rather
  than a 175 enterprise-value strategy
- the five Dominion / 175 service lines are not yet consistently positioned
  and governed as a single Andersen-facing business line
- Sidera relationship value and channel-conflict risk need deliberate handling
- economics must be tracked without double-counting 175 cash receipts and
  Levine Law operating credit

## Proposed Solution

Create a controlled HillSide project for the 175 / Dominion / Andersen service
line. The project will define the service-line strategy, governance, offer,
pipeline qualification standard, rate and staffing model, and first conversion
path for Andersen-routed work.

## Impact of Not Solving

- 175 may remain a passive receipt or holding entity instead of developing
  enterprise value
- Andersen relationship work may drift across matters without a clear corporate
  strategy
- Dominion Partners may lack a crisp offer when partner or client conversations
  arise
- channel conflict with Sidera or Andersen participants may increase
- valuable Andersen-routed opportunities may fail to convert across the five
  service lines
