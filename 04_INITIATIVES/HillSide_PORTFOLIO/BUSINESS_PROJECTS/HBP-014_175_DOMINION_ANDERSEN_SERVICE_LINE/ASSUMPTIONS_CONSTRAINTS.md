# Assumptions and Constraints

## Assumptions

- `17513721 Canada Inc` is the intended Dominion Partners entity.
- 175 is the vehicle for the Andersen Consulting collaborating-firm business
  line.
- Andersen-related fee receipts land in 175.
- Linked operational legal matters may still be tracked inside Levine Law.
- The five Dominion / 175 service lines are trade remedies, market access,
  data centers, mining, and payment services.
- Each service line and related initiative must be positioned clearly before it
  is advanced.
- The existing Dominion Platform materials are reusable but need sharper
  service-line packaging.

## Constraints

- Andersen brand usage must comply with the collaborating-firm agreement.
- Sidera relationship trust must not be damaged by ally-building or outreach.
- 175 should not hold itself out as having trade-remedies bench depth that it
  cannot credibly deliver or supervise.
- Initiatives should not be presented to Andersen outside the approved
  service-line structure without ML1 approval.
- 175 cash receipts and Levine Law planning credit must not be double-counted.
- Client commitments require clear scope, fee path, conflicts review, and
  service-delivery capacity.
