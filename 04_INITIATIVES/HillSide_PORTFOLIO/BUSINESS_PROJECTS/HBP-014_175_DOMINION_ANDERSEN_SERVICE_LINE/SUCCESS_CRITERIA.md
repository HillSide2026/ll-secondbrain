# Success Criteria

Project: `HBP-014` - 175 Dominion Andersen Service Line

## Initiation Success

Initiation is complete when:

- the project is registered as `HBP-014`
- the five service lines are recorded:
  - trade remedies
  - market access
  - data centers
  - mining
  - payment services
- the project charter, problem statement, success criteria, stakeholders, risk
  scan, approval record, and business case are represented
- HBP-014 is linked to the 175 / Dominion entity records and Andersen matters

## Planning Success

Planning is complete when:

- the Andersen-facing positioning for each service line is clear enough for
  partner conversations
- the collaborating-firm agreement has been reviewed for role, brand, fee, and
  permission constraints
- a qualification standard exists for Andersen-routed opportunities
- the core offer has been identified
- the first active service-line push is selected
- mining is either parked as lowest priority or tied to a named-company
  opportunity
- rate, staffing, and delivery assumptions are aligned with the Dominion rate
  schedule and Andersen economics
- delegated fee-earning work is defined so 175 can build enterprise value
  beyond ML1 personal delivery

## 2026 Strategic Success

By `2026-12-31`, HBP-014 succeeds if 175 / Dominion Partners has:

- a credible Andersen-facing service-line structure
- at least one qualified Andersen-routed opportunity
- a practical path for converting opportunities into revenue received by
  `17513721 Canada Inc`
- no material unresolved governance issue around Andersen permissions, Sidera
  relationship management, or cross-entity double-counting
