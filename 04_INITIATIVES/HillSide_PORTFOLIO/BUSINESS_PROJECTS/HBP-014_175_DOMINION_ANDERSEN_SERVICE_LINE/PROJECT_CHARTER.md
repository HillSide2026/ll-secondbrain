# Project Charter

**Project:** 175 Dominion Andersen Service Line  
**Project ID:** HBP-014  
**Project Path:** `04_INITIATIVES/HillSide_PORTFOLIO/BUSINESS_PROJECTS/HBP-014_175_DOMINION_ANDERSEN_SERVICE_LINE`  
**Project Type:** Strategic  
**Stage:** Planning

## Sponsor

ML1 (Matthew / HillSide)

## Mission

Build `17513721 Canada Inc`, operating as Dominion Partners, as the Andersen
Consulting collaborating firm for international trade advisory services across
five service lines: trade remedies, market access, data centers, mining, and
payment services.

## Primary Strategic Goal

By `2026-12-31`, establish 175 / Dominion Partners as a credible Andersen
Consulting collaborating-firm platform with a defined international trade
advisory service-line structure, a usable rate and staffing model, and at
least one qualified Andersen-routed opportunity.

## Service-Line Thesis

175 should not be treated merely as a passive fee-receipt entity. It is the
strategic vehicle for the Andersen-channel business line.

The five Dominion / 175 service lines are:

- trade remedies
- market access
- data centers
- mining
- payment services

Those are the service lines for the HBP-014 business line.

## Current Operating Architecture

| Layer | Role |
| --- | --- |

| Dominion Platform | Public intake, intelligence, and payments platform |

## Stage Plan

1. Confirm the collaborating-firm agreement status, permissions, and constraints.
2. Refine the Dominion Partners service menu for international trade advisory.
3. Define the Andersen-facing positioning for each of the five service lines.
4. Define the qualification standard for Andersen-routed opportunities inside
   each service line.
5. Build a partner-safe Andersen / Sidera relationship plan.
6. Align rate card, staffing model, and delivery model.
7. Identify the first qualified opportunity and conversion path.

- Lock the project framing in the HillSide register.
- Review the Andersen Consulting collaborating-firm agreement.

  - `26-1639-00001` relationship management
  - `26-1639-00002` trade remedies

## Risks

- overreliance on Andersen brand permission before agreement terms are settled
- Sidera channel conflict if ally-building is handled poorly
- thin internal trade-remedies bench relative to the desired service line
- pipeline drift into broad consulting without a concrete legal or advisory ask
- double-counting economics between 175 and Levine Law planning

## Governance

ML1-controlled stage gate. No external commitment, client promise, brand usage,
or staffing commitment should be made unless it is consistent with the
collaborating-firm agreement and ML1-approved service-line positioning.
