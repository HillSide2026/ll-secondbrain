## Decision Use

Use this file to sequence planning work for the Payment Services Consulting
Agency and define what must remain true as execution proceeds.

## Planning Objective

Maintain a controlled Payment Services Consulting Line under `17513721 Canada
Inc`, using two entry points and keeping the service ladder, provider bench,
pricing, and Levine Law boundary clear.

The operating model is an agency business model: 175 owns the offer and client
relationship, coordinates outsourced delivery where appropriate, and keeps
Levine Law referral pathways outside the 175 service ladder.

## Current Planning Focus

1. lock the service boundary relative to Levine Law
2. maintain the tier 1-6 non-legal services ladder
3. diligence outsourced providers for tiers 4-5
4. reprice tier 6 CAMLO sourcing before activation
