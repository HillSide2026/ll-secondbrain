# Project Plan

## Decision Use

Use this file to sequence planning work for the agency decision project and to
define what must be true before ML1 closes the planning stage.

## Planning Objective

Produce a decision-ready recommendation on whether a payments regulatory and
compliance consulting agency should advance, narrow into a payment services
compliance service, be re-scoped further, or be closed.

## Current Planning Focus

1. lock the service boundary relative to Levine Law
2. test whether a narrower payment services compliance service is more credible than a broad agency path
3. test differentiation, channel access, and economics
4. assemble a clean ML1 decision packet

## Immediate Planning Sprint

- define the strict boundary between consulting agency work and Levine Law legal work
- determine whether a payment services compliance service is a better boundary than a broad agency
- define the minimum believable lead source and operating model
- capture how FinSure changes the option set, and where it does not
- identify no-go triggers that would stop the project before any launch path is pursued

## Completion Condition

Planning is complete when ML1 has a clear basis to choose one of three
outcomes:

- authorize a next-step execution path
- reclassify into a different project type
- close the opportunity as a no-go

## Planning Outcome

Planning has now produced a proceed decision.

Authorized direction:

- proceed with a payment services compliance service rather than a broad agency
- operate through `17513721 Canada Inc`
- use FinSure as the entry-offer wedge
- treat the Payment Services Consulting Line as the operating business line
- consider `FinSure` as the business name
- start with:
  - program design
  - CAMLO services

Excluded from `HBP-011`:

- Rhizome white-label negotiation or implementation
- separate partner or distribution project work

Execution-stage refinement still required:

- pricing model
- exact service inclusions and exclusions
- decision on whether a second, infrastructure-focused model should be offered
  where the client's in-house personnel acts as CAMLO
- business-name decision and go-to-market framing around FinSure
