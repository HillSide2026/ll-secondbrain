# Channel Evaluation Without Data

Project: `HBP-011` - Payment Services Consulting Line

## Core Problem

`HBP-011`, `Funnel 3`, and `fintechlawyer.ca` address overlapping buyer pain
around MSB, AML, FINTRAC, payment services, and compliance readiness.

The repo does not yet have live performance data sufficient to conclude which
channel should receive the most investment.

Until data exists, the decision should be treated as a decision under
uncertainty rather than as a proven funnel conclusion.

## Current Channel Map

| Channel | Function | Offer | Entity | Role |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Funnel 3 | Levine Law conversion funnel | Legal consults / AML and payments mandates | Levine Professional Corp, dba Levine Law | Authority-to-consult pipeline |
| fintechlawyer.ca | Discovery / authority layer inside Funnel 3 | Content trust, issue education, lead capture | Levine Professional Corp, dba Levine Law | Not currently treated as a standalone channel |
| HBP-011 / FinSure | Payment Services Consulting Line | Productized compliance, readiness, STR, managed compliance, CAMS/licensing/CAMLO support | `17513721 Canada Inc` | 175 enterprise value channel |

## Strategic Distinction

The live strategic question is not whether `Funnel 3` beats
`fintechlawyer.ca`. The repo currently treats `fintechlawyer.ca` as an
authority layer inside `Funnel 3`.

The harder question is whether the same payment-compliance buyer should be
routed into:

- Levine Law legal work; or
- `17513721 Canada Inc` agency / productized compliance work.

## Buyer Segmentation

| Buyer Type | What They Think They Need | Real Problem | Best Route |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Legal buyer | legal advice, registration analysis, regulatory interpretation, legal opinion, FINTRAC response, defensible position | legal uncertainty, regulatory exposure, enforcement risk, board/investor concern | Levine Law / Funnel 3 |
| Compliance buyer | readiness, documentation cleanup, review prep, workflow support, outsourced compliance help | operational gaps, weak evidence, messy workflows, under-resourced AML function | HBP-011 / FinSure |
| Founder / operator buyer | launch support, risk reduction, bankability, regulatory path, business continuity | mixed legal, compliance, banking, licensing, and operational readiness issue | paid triage, then route |
| Enterprise / strategic buyer | market entry, payment infrastructure strategy, partner/vendor execution | cross-border market access, regulatory structuring, operational partner strategy | HBP-014 parent strategy, with HBP-011 as the payment services line |

## 30-Day Smoke Tests

The purpose of the first test period is to measure willingness to pay, not
traffic or content engagement.

| Test | Minimum Asset | Success Signal |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Legal buyer test | fintechlawyer.ca / Funnel 3 consult CTA | qualified legal consults booked |
| Compliance buyer test | FinSure readiness landing page | paid readiness calls or strong inbound replies |
| STR product test | portal waitlist or gated interest form | buyers request access or pay for the STR report |
| Provider-backed services test | direct outreach to MSBs / PSPs | interest in managed compliance, licensing support, CAMS operations, or CAMLO sourcing |

## Measurement Priorities

| Metric | Why It Matters |
| --- | --- |
| Source-tagged leads | separates Funnel 3, fintechlawyer.ca, FinSure, lead magnet, referral, and direct outreach |
| Booking conversion rate | shows whether the channel creates real sales conversations |
| Consult-to-paid conversion | separates curiosity from revenue |
| Average revenue per conversion | compares legal mandates against agency products |
| ML1 time per dollar | shows whether the channel creates leverage or more personal workload |
| Delegability | determines whether the channel can build 175 enterprise value |
| CAC / effort cost | captures paid spend and manual business-development burden |
| 90-day revenue signal | provides the first investment decision point |

## Interim Bias

In the absence of data, HBP-011 should prioritize tests that are:

- delegable;
- tied to the approved 175 service ladder;
- capable of producing 175 enterprise value;
- clear about the non-legal agency boundary; and
- capable of creating willingness-to-pay evidence within 30 to 90 days.

Funnel 3 and `fintechlawyer.ca` should continue to capture higher-trust legal
buyers, but HBP-011 should not be subordinated to legal-funnel logic if the
strategic objective is 175 enterprise value.

## Required Next Control

Before any channel can be judged as superior, all relevant leads must be tagged
at intake by:

- source;
- buyer type;
- route chosen;
- offer shown;
- price quoted;
- outcome;
- ML1 time required; and
- delegability.

Without this evidence, the repo can frame the choice but cannot validate the
choice.
