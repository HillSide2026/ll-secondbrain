# Risk Register

Project ID: `HBP-011`
Stage: `Planning`

| Risk | Category | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| The agency remains too close to Levine Law to sustain a clean service boundary | Strategic | M | H | Define hard boundary rules and treat unresolved overlap as a no-go trigger |
| The offer does not differentiate clearly from existing consulting alternatives | Strategic | M | H | Force a narrower niche and reject paths that depend on generic positioning |
| Economics are too thin after delivery and business-development effort | Financial | M | H | Define minimum viable economics and compare each credible option against them |
| The concept expands into an overly broad services business | Scope | M | H | Keep the planning boundary narrow and reject option creep |
| The buyer/channel thesis takes too long to clarify | Schedule | M | M | Narrow options early and escalate unresolved market assumptions quickly |
| Branding, advisory, or operating spend begins before a viable path is identified | Budget | L | M | Keep spend bounded until one option survives the planning thresholds |
