# Outsourced Provider Bench

Project ID: `HBP-011`
Stage: `Planning`

## Purpose

Track outsourced providers that may support FinSure / Payment Services
Consulting Agency delivery under `17513721 Canada Inc`.

This bench is meant to prevent the FinSure agency offer from becoming pure ML1
delivery. Provider use must preserve service boundaries, pricing discipline,
and the separation between 175 non-legal services and Levine Law legal services.

## Managed Compliance Split

Tier 3 managed compliance may split into two separately priced delivery tracks
in the value ladder:

| Tier | Track | Use Case | Boundary |
|---|---|---|---|
| 4 | CAMS operational consulting | AML/KYC operations, compliance workflow, policy implementation support, operational readiness | No CAMLO provision, no delegated decision-making, no regulator representation |
| 5 | Licensing | High-ticket licensing / registration support for payment-services clients | Requires legal/regulatory boundary review before offer |

## Provider Diligence Fields

Before a provider is used in pricing or client materials, record:

- provider name and contact
- track supported: licensing or CAMS operational consulting
- jurisdictions covered
- credentials and relevant experience
- scope they will and will not perform
- pricing / margin assumptions
- subcontracting and confidentiality terms
- conflict and client-ownership constraints
- whether they create any regulatory, legal-practice, or unauthorized-practice
  concern
