# Problem Statement

Project: `HBP-011` - Payments Regulatory & Compliance Consulting Agency

## Problem

The idea of a payments regulatory and compliance consulting agency has
strategic appeal, but the concept is not yet shaped well enough to support a
disciplined go / hold / no-go decision.

Current gaps:

- the boundary between a consulting agency and Levine Law legal services is not yet defined clearly
- the target buyer, offer set, and differentiation thesis are still broad
- the minimum delivery model, capability needs, and economics are not yet decision-ready
- the idea is not yet framed inside a governed project packet

## Proposed Solution

Stand up a controlled initiation-stage decision project to define the agency
concept, test the main boundary and economics questions, and prepare an ML1
decision on whether this path should advance.

## Impact of Not Solving

- HillSide could drift into a launch concept without enough clarity on service boundary or channel fit
- Levine Law and Matthew Holdings positioning could overlap in an uncontrolled way
- time and money could be spent building an agency model before its differentiation is credible
