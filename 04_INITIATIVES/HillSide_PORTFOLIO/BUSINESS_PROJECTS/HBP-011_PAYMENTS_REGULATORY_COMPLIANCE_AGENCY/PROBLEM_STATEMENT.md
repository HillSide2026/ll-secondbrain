# Problem Statement

Project: `HBP-011` - Payment Services Consulting Line

## Problem

The payment-services opportunity has narrowed from a broad regulatory /
compliance agency idea into a Payment Services Consulting Line under
`17513721 Canada Inc`. The current task is to keep the agency commercially useful
without blurring legal services, outsourced provider work, CAMLO sourcing, and
Rhizome implementation into one uncontrolled offer.

Current gaps:

- tier boundaries must stay clear, especially between managed compliance, CAMS operational consulting, licensing support, and CAMLO sourcing
- Levine Law referral pathways must remain outside the 175 service ladder
- outsourced providers must be diligenced before pricing or client commitments
- tier 6 CAMLO sourcing may be underpriced at the current `$3,000 CAD/month` reference price

## Proposed Solution

Operate `HBP-011` as the controlled project packet for the Payment Services
Consulting Agency, using the following non-legal services ladder:

| Tier | Description | Entity | Price |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | FinSure STR report, consulting only | 17513721 Canada Inc. | $297 |
| 2 | Rhizome white label | 17513721 Canada Inc. / HBP-010 | TBD |
| 3 | Managed compliance, no CAMLO provision | 17513721 Canada Inc. | TBD |
| 4 | CAMS operational consulting, outsourced operations track | 17513721 Canada Inc. | TBD |
| 5 | Licensing support, outsourced high-ticket track | 17513721 Canada Inc. | TBD |
| 6 | CAMLO sourcing, 1-year contract minimum | 17513721 Canada Inc. | $3,000 CAD/month reference price, flagged as possibly too low |

## Impact of Not Solving

- HillSide could drift into a launch concept without enough clarity on service boundary or channel fit
- Levine Law and Matthew Holdings positioning could overlap in an uncontrolled way
- outsourced-provider work could be sold before scope, pricing, margin, and responsibility are clear
