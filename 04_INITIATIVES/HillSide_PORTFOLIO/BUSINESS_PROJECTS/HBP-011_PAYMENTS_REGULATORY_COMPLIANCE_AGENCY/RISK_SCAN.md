# Risk Scan

Project: `HBP-011` - Payments Regulatory & Compliance Consulting Agency

## Strategic Risks

- the agency may not differentiate clearly enough from Levine Law or from existing market alternatives
- the model may create channel or positioning conflict across the broader portfolio

## Financial Risks

- revenue ramp may be slower than expected relative to the capability required
- the economics may be too thin once delivery support and business development effort are accounted for

## Scope Risks

- the agency concept may sprawl from a narrow consulting thesis into a diffuse services business
- legal, compliance, operational, and implementation work may be blended too early into one unclear offer

## Schedule Risks

- launch-readiness timing may slip if service boundary and channel thesis are not narrowed quickly
- validation may stall if too many adjacent offer ideas are explored at once

## Budget Risks

- branding, tooling, advisory, or business-development spend may begin before the model deserves it

## Key Assumptions

- there is enough strategic signal to justify disciplined evaluation
- the most useful near-term output is a cleaner decision, not a faster launch
- any eventual go-forward path will need a narrower operating model than the concept currently implies

## Go / No-Go Judgment

Proceed to Planning only if the service boundary, differentiation thesis,
channel path, and minimum economics are clear enough to justify deeper work.
