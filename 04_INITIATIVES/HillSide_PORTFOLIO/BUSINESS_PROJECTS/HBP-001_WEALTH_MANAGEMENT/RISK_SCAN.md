# Risk Scan

Project: `HBP-001` - Wealth Management

## Scope Risks

- The project may blur internal wealth management with unrelated venture activity or advisory-business ideas.
- The net-worth baseline may stay too loose if asset values and liabilities are not consolidated consistently.

## Schedule Risks

- Baseline preparation may take longer than expected if business-equity values or liabilities are harder to assemble.
- Planning authorization may slip if the target gap and review model are not quantified early.

## Budget Risks

- Capital, advisory spend, or management time could be committed before hurdle rates and liquidity rules are fixed.

## Key Assumptions

- the primary objective is consolidated net worth of at least CAD 2,000,000 by `2030-12-31`
- the initiative is internal to ML1's personal wealth-governance layer and is
  not a client-facing business
- in-scope assets and liabilities can be measured reliably enough to support planning

## Go / No-Go Judgment

Proceed to Planning only if the opening baseline, the gap to target, and the
capital-governance model are specific enough to support a credible next stage.
