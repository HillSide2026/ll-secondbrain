# Success Criteria

Project: `HBP-001` - Wealth Management

## Criteria

| Criterion | Threshold | Verification Source |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Target model defined | One primary model selected from internal capital management, external offering, or hybrid | `PROJECT_CHARTER.md` and `BUSINESS_CASE.md` |
| Regulatory posture clarified | Decision-ready summary of the licensing, registration, and jurisdictional boundary is documented | `BUSINESS_CASE.md` and planning-gate notes |
| Capital and operating structure framed | Initial capital envelope, ownership structure, and partner/platform assumptions are documented | `PROJECT_CHARTER.md` and `BUSINESS_CASE.md` |
| Planning gate packet ready | ML1 receives a credible go / hold / no-go basis for whether Planning should begin | `APPROVAL_RECORD.md` and initiation packet review |

## Completion Rule

Initiation-stage success means the project is narrowed enough to support an
ML1 decision on whether formal planning should begin. It does not authorize
capital deployment, client-facing launch, or external commitments.
