# Success Criteria

Project: `HBP-001` - Wealth Management

## Criteria

| Criterion | Threshold | Verification Source |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Goal targets fixed | The project states two goals: consolidated net worth ≥ CAD 2,000,000 by `2030-12-31` (SG-01) and ≥ CAD 4,000,000 by `2040-12-31` (SG-02), with the measurement basis documented clearly | `PROJECT_CHARTER.md` and `BUSINESS_CASE.md` |
| Opening baseline defined | A first-pass consolidated net-worth baseline is documented, including known valuation caveats and the current gap to target | `BUSINESS_CASE.md` and planning-gate notes |
| Capital governance framed | Initial capital-allocation guardrails, liquidity rules, and review cadence are documented clearly enough for planning | `PROJECT_CHARTER.md` and `RISK_SCAN.md` |
| Compounding path quantified | A required annual accumulation rate or equivalent gap-closing path to `2030-12-31` and `2040-12-31` is documented | `PROJECT_CHARTER.md` and `BUSINESS_CASE.md` |
| Planning gate packet ready | ML1 receives a credible go / hold / no-go basis for whether Planning should begin | `APPROVAL_RECORD.md` and initiation packet review |

## Completion Rule

Initiation-stage success means the `2030-12-31` and `2040-12-31` targets, opening baseline, and
capital-governance model are specific enough to support an ML1 decision on
whether formal planning should begin. It does not authorize a public-facing
wealth-management business or capital deployment outside existing authority.
