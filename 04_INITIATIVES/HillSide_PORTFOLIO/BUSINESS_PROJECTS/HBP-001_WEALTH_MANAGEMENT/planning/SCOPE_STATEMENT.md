# Scope Statement

Project ID: `HBP-001`
Project Path: `04_INITIATIVES/HillSide_PORTFOLIO/BUSINESS_PROJECTS/HBP-001_WEALTH_MANAGEMENT`
Stage: `Planning`

## Planning Use

Use this file to define the planning boundary for the internal wealth-management
model and to prevent scope drift into unrelated businesses or public offerings.

## In Scope

- consolidated net-worth measurement boundary for Matthew Holdings / HillSide
- opening balance-sheet view of in-scope assets and liabilities
- treatment of liquid holdings, securities, and business equity
- target-gap calculation relative to CAD 2,000,000 by `2030-12-31` (SG-01) and CAD 4,000,000 by `2040-12-31` (SG-02)
- capital-allocation guardrails and liquidity-reserve design
- quarterly review cadence and annual reforecast logic
- decision-useful scenario analysis for the main wealth engines

## Out of Scope

- public-facing wealth-management services
- outside-client advisory offerings
- legal, tax, or structuring implementation beyond planning assumptions
- speculative venture incubation not tied to the 2030 or 2040 targets
- day-to-day operating management of each in-scope business

## Required Planning Outputs

- fixed baseline methodology
- quantified gap-to-target path
- capital-governance framework
- review and reporting cadence
- execution recommendation for the next stage

## Gate Criteria for Executing Authorization

- the baseline can be reproduced on a consistent basis
- the target gap and path are quantified clearly enough for decision use
- capital-allocation and liquidity rules are explicit
- dependencies and risks are narrow enough to support controlled execution
