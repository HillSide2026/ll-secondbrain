# Project Charter

**Project:** Wealth Management  
**Project ID:** HBP-001  
**Project Path:** `04_INITIATIVES/HillSide_PORTFOLIO/BUSINESS_PROJECTS/WEALTH_MANAGEMENT`  
**Project Type:** Strategic  
**Stage:** Initiating

## Sponsor

ML1 (Matthew Holdings)

## Mission

Define a clear roadmap to financial independence, financial freedom, and
financial abundance through a controlled wealth management initiative under
Matthew Holdings.

## Success Criteria

- target wealth management model defined
- regulatory path clarified at decision-ready level
- operating and capital structure defined
- ML1 receives a planning-ready go / hold / no-go packet

## Stage Plan

1. define scope and target model
2. clarify regulatory and licensing path
3. define structure, capital, and partner assumptions
4. prepare planning authorization packet

## Immediate Next Actions

- clarify whether the project is internal capital management, external offering, or hybrid
- define target customer / beneficiary and service boundary
- identify jurisdictional and licensing implications
- define initial capital envelope and operating assumptions

## Risks

- scope ambiguity, including operating complexity and partner dependency
- planning delay if the target model is not narrowed early
- budget exposure or capital lock-up before the model is validated

## Governance

ML1-controlled stage gate. No planning or executing work is authorized
until ML1 approves the transition out of Initiating.
