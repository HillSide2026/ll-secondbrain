# Risk Scan

Project: `HBP-001` - Wealth Management

## Scope Risks

- The project may blur internal capital management, investment vehicle design, and external service delivery.
- The target operating model may remain too broad, with operating complexity and partner dependency assumed before the service boundary is fixed.

## Schedule Risks

- The project may take longer than expected if the initial model requires more structuring work than expected.
- Decision-ready planning authorization may slip if the project scope is not narrowed early and key assumptions remain unresolved.

## Budget Risks

- Capital, advisory spend, or management time could be committed too early against an unvalidated structure.

## Key Assumptions

- the initiative is still in controlled definition mode
- no capital commitment or external offering is authorized by creating this project folder
- external platform / advisor dependency is likely but not yet fixed

## Go / No-Go Judgment

Proceed to Planning only if the scope can be narrowed enough to support a
credible schedule and budget for the next stage.
