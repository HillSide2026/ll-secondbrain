# Risk Scan

Project: HBP-004 - Develop and Launch Micro SaaS (TariffLookup.ca)
Project Type: Strategic (tracked with scope/schedule/budget controls)

## Top 5 Risks (Grouped)

### Scope

1. Product-market mismatch
No meaningful demand for the selected problem, leading to low activation after launch.

2. Scope expansion
MVP expands beyond a small practice project and weakens focus on a launchable core product.

### Schedule

3. Distribution failure
Launch occurs but customer acquisition does not produce enough early users for validation.

4. Technical quality debt
Speed-first build creates reliability/security issues that require rework and delay launch stabilization.

### Budget

5. Launch cost overrun
Infrastructure/tooling costs expand beyond plan during build and launch.

## Key Assumptions

- The project stays intentionally small and tightly scoped.
- A narrow ICP can be identified quickly.
- Build resources are available to ship an MVP without major delays.
- Basic legal/compliance requirements can be satisfied within current operating capacity.
- Pilot users can be onboarded quickly after launch.

## Go / No-Go Judgment

Decision: Proceed with conditions

Conditions:
- lock one narrow problem and ICP before build
- enforce a strict MVP scope and launch deadline
- define launch readiness checklist requirements from day 1
- review traction at fixed checkpoints before further investment
