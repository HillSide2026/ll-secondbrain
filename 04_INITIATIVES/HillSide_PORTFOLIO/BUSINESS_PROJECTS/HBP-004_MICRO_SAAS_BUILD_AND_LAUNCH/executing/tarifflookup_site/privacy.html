Privacy at a glance

TariffLookup.ca is in early release preparation. This page provides a simple current-position statement for the launch homepage.

What information we may receive

  • If you email us, we receive the information you include in that email.
  • Basic server logs may be collected by the hosting provider for security and operations.
  • Additional analytics should only be treated as active once they are expressly added to the site.

How information may be used

  • To respond to early-access or product inquiries.
  • To understand launch interest and prioritize product development.
  • To maintain site security and hosting operations.

Current boundary

The homepage is a marketing and launch-interest page. It is not a client portal, legal service portal, or customs filing system.
