Why this exists

Export-market tariff checks are still too manual.

Teams evaluating a new market often need to bounce between tariff schedules, trade-agreement materials, and internal notes before they can answer a basic question: what rate applies here, and why?

TariffLookup is designed to compress that first-pass research into a simpler decision surface.
