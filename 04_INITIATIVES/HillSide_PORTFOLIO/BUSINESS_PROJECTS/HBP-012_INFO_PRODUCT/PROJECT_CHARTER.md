# Project Charter — HBP-012 — Launch, Market, Sell and Monetize Information Product

**Project ID:** HBP-012
**Entity:** 17513721 Canada Inc (Matthew Holdings)
**Project Type:** Strategic
**Stage:** Executing
**Owner:** ML1

## Sponsor

ML1

## Mission

Launch and monetize a done-for-you legal template pack for early-stage founders
and small business owners who need legal protection but cannot afford legal counsel.
Generate first revenue within 48 hours of launch using a constrained warm-outreach
strategy, and validate whether this product and ICP support a repeatable revenue channel.

## Product Definition

**Product name:** [TBD — ML1 to confirm]
**Format:** Digital template pack — done-for-you legal toolkit
**Contents:** 4–5 standalone contract templates, each with a plain-English cover page
explaining when and how to use it
**Length:** 15–25 pages total
**Delivery:** Automated digital delivery on purchase confirmation via GHL + Stripe

**Content status:** Mostly written. Existing drafts need cleanup, plain-English
headers need to be added, and client-specific details need to be stripped.
Estimated production effort: 5–8 hours (ML1 + VA).

## Target Buyer

**ICP:** Early-stage founders and small business owners who need basic legal
protection — contracts, liability protection, standard commercial agreements —
but cannot afford traditional legal counsel for drafting.

**Geography:** Canada-connected (primary); broader English-language market (secondary)

**Funnel position:** Standalone revenue play. Upsell path exists (contract review
service or retainer at higher price point) but is unproven and out of scope for
this project phase.

**Relationship to existing funnels:** This is a new ICP — distinct from F2
(corporate operators >$1M revenue) and F3 (payments/MSB operators). No crossover
intended for this phase.

## Revenue Model

**Model:** One-time purchase
**Price:** $197 CAD
**Platform:** GHL funnel with Stripe payment processing and automated email delivery

## Launch Strategy

**Timeline:** 48 hours from go-decision — intentional constraint to force
execution over perfection.
**Traffic plan (launch window):**
- 20 personal direct messages (warm outreach)
- 2 community posts
- 1 LinkedIn post

## Stage Plan

1. Complete initiation artifacts and register project (current)
2. Clean up templates, add plain-English cover pages, strip client details — 5–8 hrs
3. Build GHL funnel + Stripe checkout + automated delivery sequence
4. Execute warm outreach and LinkedIn post
5. Collect first sales, track against success criteria
6. Decide: close, maintain, or scale based on 30-day result

## Risks

- Production takes longer than 5–8 hours (single point of failure — ML1 only)
- 48-hour launch window creates execution pressure that may compromise quality
- Warm outreach is finite — no scalable traffic plan for beyond launch window
- Price point ($197) untested for this ICP
- Legal disclaimer and scope-of-product positioning must be handled carefully
  to avoid any implication of legal advice or professional engagement

## Governance

ML1-controlled stage gate. No external commitments, marketing, or spend beyond
GHL/Stripe infrastructure authorized until ML1 approves Initiating → Planning.
