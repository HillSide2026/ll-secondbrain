# Success Criteria — HBP-012 — Info Product

## Primary Success Threshold

**$1,000 revenue within 30 days of launch.**
This requires 6 units sold at $197 (≈ $1,182 gross before Stripe fees).
This is the ML1-defined go/no-go signal for whether to invest further in this product.

## Success Criteria Table

| Criterion | Target | Window | Signal |
|---|---|---|---|
| First sale | ≥1 unit | Within 7 days of launch | Product-market proof of concept |
| Revenue threshold | ≥$1,000 gross | Within 30 days | Validated revenue channel |
| Units sold | ≥6 | Within 30 days | Confirms price point works |
| Upsell inquiry | ≥1 inbound contact after purchase | Within 30 days | Backend path signal |
| Launch execution | All outreach executed within 48 hrs | Launch window | Execution discipline |

## Minimum Viable Outcome

At least 1 paying customer within 7 days of executing the launch outreach.
Zero sales after full outreach execution is a signal to stop, not optimize.

## Scale Trigger

If primary threshold is met, the project advances to a scale decision:
- Is there a repeatable traffic source beyond warm outreach?
- Does the upsell path justify building out?
- Should this product be revised, repriced, or rebundled?

Scale decision is an explicit gate — it is not automatic on hitting $1,000.

## Failure Conditions

- Zero sales within 7 days of full outreach execution
- Production overruns consume more than 12 hours of ML1 time without a shippable product
- GHL/Stripe setup takes more than 4 hours and delays launch beyond 48-hour window

## Out of Scope for This Phase

- Backend upsell path (contract review service or retainer)
- Paid advertising or organic SEO traffic
- List-building or email nurture sequence
- Product iteration or v2 planning
