# Common Artifacts: FINTRAC_RESPONSE

## Core Artifacts

- **FINTRAC Correspondence Log** — Chronological record of FINTRAC communications
- **Document Production Index** — Inventory of documents provided to FINTRAC
- **Response Memorandum** — Summary of response strategy and key positions
- **Draft Response Letter** — Prepared correspondence to FINTRAC
- **Issue & Gap List** — Compliance gaps identified during response preparation
