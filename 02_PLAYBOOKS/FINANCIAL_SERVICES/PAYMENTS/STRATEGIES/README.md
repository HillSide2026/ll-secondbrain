# Payments Strategies

Strategy layer for the Payments practice area.

Strategies are high-value engagement architectures that may orchestrate multiple solutions. They require ML1 approval to create.

---

## Required Strategy Files

Each strategy folder must include:

- STRATEGY_SCOPE.md
- STRATEGY_ARCHITECTURE.md
- COMPONENT_SOLUTIONS.md
- RISK_SURFACE.md
- VERSION.md
