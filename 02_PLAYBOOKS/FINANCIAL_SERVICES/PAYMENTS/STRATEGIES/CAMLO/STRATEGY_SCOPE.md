# Strategy Scope: CAMLO Services

> **Firm Preference:** Levine Law's preference is NOT to act as CAMLO directly.
> CAMLO engagements are the domain of FinSure.
> This strategy exists to document routing, referral logic, and any boundary cases
> where LL may provide limited supporting legal counsel to a CAMLO engagement
> originating at FinSure.

---

## Purpose

Chief Anti-Money Laundering Officer (CAMLO) services involve an individual or firm
acting as the designated CAMLO for a regulated entity under FINTRAC requirements.

Levine Law does not seek to provide CAMLO services as a primary offering. Where a
client requires a CAMLO, the engagement is to be routed to FinSure.

A CAMLO appointment may raise barriers to LL conducting the mandatory PCMLTFA
Effectiveness Review (required every two years under s. 9.6 of the PCMLTFA
regulations) for that client. This is a reason to decline CAMLO appointments.

See: `02_PLAYBOOKS/FINANCIAL_SERVICES/SOLUTIONS/PCMLTFA_EFFECTIVENESS_REVIEW/SOLUTION_SCOPE.md`

---

## In Scope (LL role, if any)

- Supporting legal counsel to a FinSure-led CAMLO engagement
- Legal review of CAMLO-related compliance program documents
- Regulatory escalation support where legal advice (not compliance execution) is required

## Out of Scope (LL)

- Acting as named CAMLO for any regulated entity
- Providing ongoing CAMLO execution services
- Any engagement where LL would bear CAMLO regulatory accountability

## Routing Rule

- Client inquiry involving CAMLO → route to FinSure
- If client is already a FinSure CAMLO client and requires legal counsel → may open as LL matter under supporting counsel scope above
