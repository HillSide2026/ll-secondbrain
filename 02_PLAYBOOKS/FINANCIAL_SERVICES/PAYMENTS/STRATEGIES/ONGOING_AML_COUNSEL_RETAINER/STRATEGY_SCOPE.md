# Strategy Scope: Ongoing AML Counsel Retainer

## Purpose

Ongoing legal counsel retainer for clients requiring continuous AML/KYC advisory
support. Typically entered following completion of a core AML engagement
(AML_HEALTH_CHECK, PCMLTFA_EFFECTIVENESS_REVIEW, or MSB_INTAKE_AND_REGISTRATION).

Billing type: Subscription or Hourly (Retainer)

---

## In Scope

- Ongoing AML/KYC regulatory advice
- Review and update of compliance policies and procedures
- FINTRAC correspondence review and advisory
- Regulatory monitoring and change alerts
- Advisory support for internal compliance team or CAMLO
- Ad hoc legal opinions on AML/KYC matters

## Out of Scope

- Acting as CAMLO (see CAMLO strategy)
- FINTRAC examination execution (separate solution: FINTRAC_RESPONSE)
- PCMLTFA Effectiveness Review (separate solution; independence constraint applies)

## Typical Entry Points

- Post AML_HEALTH_CHECK
- Post PCMLTFA_EFFECTIVENESS_REVIEW
- Post MSB_INTAKE_AND_REGISTRATION
