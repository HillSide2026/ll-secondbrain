# Strategy Scope: Ongoing Payments Counsel

## Purpose

Ongoing legal counsel retainer for clients requiring continuous payments regulatory
advisory support across the full regulatory landscape (RPAA, PCMLTFA, banking
relationships, payment rail access). Broader in scope than Ongoing AML Counsel
Retainer — covers the payments business holistically.

Billing type: Subscription or Hourly (Retainer)

---

## In Scope

- Ongoing payments regulatory advice (RPAA, PCMLTFA, Bank Act, payment rail rules)
- Annual RPAA report support (RPAA_REPORT)
- Banking relationship advisory (BANK_ONBOARD, BANK_REVIEW)
- Regulatory monitoring and change alerts across payments landscape
- Ad hoc legal opinions on payments regulatory matters
- Product and business model change assessment

## Out of Scope

- Acting as CAMLO (see CAMLO strategy)
- FINTRAC examination execution (separate solution: FINTRAC_RESPONSE)
- AML program design or redesign (separate engagement)

## Typical Entry Points

- Post MSB_INTAKE_AND_REGISTRATION
- Post RPAA_REGISTRATION
- Post any core payments solution where ongoing regulatory exposure identified
