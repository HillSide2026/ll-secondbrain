# Strategy Scope: CARF Program (Minimum Viable but Defensible)

## Purpose

A structured program to bring a crypto-asset reporting entity into compliance with
the OECD Crypto-Asset Reporting Framework (CARF) as implemented by the CRA.
Designed for entities that need to be defensible in a CRA review without building
an enterprise compliance function.

The program is organized around 10 components, each a discrete solution. Together
they constitute a complete, minimum viable, but legally defensible CARF program.

Billing type: Flat Fee (program) or modular per component

---

## In Scope

- All 10 CARF_PROGRAM component solutions (see COMPONENT_SOLUTIONS.md)
- Entities in scope: crypto-asset service providers with CRA reporting obligations
  (exchanges, brokerages, custodians, wallet providers, other)

## Out of Scope

- Tax filing or tax advice to end users of the reporting entity
- AML/KYC program build (separate engagement; integration is in scope)
- FINTRAC compliance (separate solutions: MSB_INTAKE_AND_REGISTRATION, FINTRAC_RESPONSE)

## Typical Entry Points

- New crypto-asset reporting entity preparing for first CARF reporting cycle
- Existing entity with no CARF program in place
- Entity with informal CARF practices seeking to formalize and document defensibility
