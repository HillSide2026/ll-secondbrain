# Solution Scope: CARF Governance

Component 1 of CARF_PROGRAM. Must be completed before all other components.

## Purpose

Establishes the minimum governance layer for a defensible CARF program.
Formal committees are not required. Responsibility and accountability must be clear.

## Included

- Named compliance lead designation
- Written mandate covering:
  - Responsibility for CARF classification decisions
  - Responsibility for reporting accuracy
  - Escalation rule: defined threshold for seeking external advice
- Deliverables:
  - 3–5 page CARF policy document
  - One-page roles and responsibilities document

## Excluded

- Scoping and classification (CARF_SCOPING_AND_CLASSIFICATION)
- Formal governance committee structure

## Notes

Functions as a minimal governance layer analogous to AML oversight under the PCMLTFA.
