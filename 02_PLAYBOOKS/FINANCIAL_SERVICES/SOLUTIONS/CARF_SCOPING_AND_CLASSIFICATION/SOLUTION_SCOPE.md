# Solution Scope: CARF Scoping and Classification

Component 2 of CARF_PROGRAM. Primary control point — must be completed before
components 3–8. Errors here invalidate downstream processes.

## Purpose

Defines the entity's reportable scope under CARF. Produces the foundational
classification memorandum on which all downstream components depend.

## Included

- Classification of services provided: exchange, brokerage, custody, wallet, or other
- Scope of reportable transactions
- Scope of reportable users
- Deliverables:
  - Internal classification memorandum
  - Explicit positions on in-scope activities
  - Explicit positions on out-of-scope activities, with rationale

## Excluded

- Governance layer (CARF_GOVERNANCE)
- Operational implementation (components 3–8)
