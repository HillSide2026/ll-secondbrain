# Solution Scope: CARF Reporting Output

Component 6 of CARF_PROGRAM. Depends on CARF_SCOPING_AND_CLASSIFICATION and
CARF_TRANSACTION_CLASSIFICATION.

## Purpose

Produces the annual CRA-facing CARF report per reportable user.

## Required Report Content

- Identifying information
- Transaction summaries
- Valuation data

## Included

- Structured export (CSV or JSON)
- Reproducibility requirement
- Audit traceability

## Excluded

- Recordkeeping infrastructure (CARF_RECORDKEEPING)
- Tax advice to end users
