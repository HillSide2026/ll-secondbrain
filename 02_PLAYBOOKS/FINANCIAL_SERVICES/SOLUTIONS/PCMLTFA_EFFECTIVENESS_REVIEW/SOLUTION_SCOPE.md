# Solution Scope: PCMLTFA Effectiveness Review

## Regulatory Basis

Under the Proceeds of Crime (Money Laundering) and Terrorist Financing Act (PCMLTFA)
regulations (s. 9.6), every reporting entity must have the effectiveness of its
compliance program reviewed every two years. The review must be conducted by an
independent party — not the compliance officer or anyone responsible for the
compliance program.

Levine Law may act as the independent reviewer for this obligation.

## Included

- Review of the client's AML/KYC compliance policies, procedures, and controls
- Assessment of effectiveness against PCMLTFA regulatory requirements
- Formal effectiveness review report suitable for regulatory purposes
- Identification of gaps and remediation recommendations

## Excluded

- Drafting or redesigning the compliance program (separate engagement)
- Acting as CAMLO or ongoing compliance officer
- FINTRAC examination support (separate solution: FINTRAC_RESPONSE)

## Recurrence

- Mandatory every two years per PCMLTFA regulations
- Schedulable and recurring — eligible for flat fee or retainer pricing
