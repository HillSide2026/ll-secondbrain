# Solution Scope: AML Health Check

## Purpose

A structured diagnostic of a client's AML/KYC compliance program. Produces an
assessment of current program health, identifies gaps, and provides prioritized
recommendations.

This is a practitioner-led advisory review — it is not a formal effectiveness
review under PCMLTFA s. 9.6 and does not carry the independence requirements of
that obligation. It may serve as a precursor to a PCMLTFA Effectiveness Review,
MSB_REVIEW, or FINTRAC_RESPONSE engagement.

---

## Included

- Review of AML/KYC policies and procedures
- Assessment against PCMLTFA and applicable regulatory requirements
- Gap identification and prioritized remediation recommendations
- Written health check report

## Excluded

- Formal PCMLTFA Effectiveness Review (separate solution: PCMLTFA_EFFECTIVENESS_REVIEW)
- Compliance program redesign or drafting (separate engagement)
- FINTRAC examination support (separate solution: FINTRAC_RESPONSE)
- Ongoing compliance monitoring

## Recurrence

- Typically one-time or ad hoc; not a mandated periodic obligation
- May be offered as a recurring annual check-in under retainer
