# Solution Scope: RPAA Report

## Regulatory Basis

Under the Retail Payment Activities Act (RPAA), registered payment service providers
(PSPs) are required to submit an annual report to the Bank of Canada covering their
payment activities, safeguarding measures, and operational risk management practices.

This is a mandatory, recurring annual obligation for all registered PSPs.

---

## Included

- Preparation and review of the annual RPAA report for submission to the Bank of Canada
- Assessment of reportable payment activities and safeguarding arrangements
- Review of operational risk management disclosures
- Coordination of required data and supporting documentation
- Final report ready for PSP submission

## Excluded

- Initial RPAA registration (separate solution: RPAA_REGISTRATION)
- Three-year supervisory review (separate solution: RPAA_THREE_YEAR_REVIEW)
- Remediation of identified compliance gaps (separate engagement)

## Recurrence

- Annual obligation
- Schedulable and recurring — eligible for flat fee or retainer pricing
