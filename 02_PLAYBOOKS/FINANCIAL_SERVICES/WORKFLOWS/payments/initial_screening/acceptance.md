# Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Trigger fact patterns for token, stablecoin, custody, marketplace, and hybrid matters are explicitly identified.
- [ ] The screening output separates securities, money-services, and payment-services lenses instead of collapsing them into one regime.
- [ ] The workflow records a risk rating, touchability assessment, and next routing action.
- [ ] The workflow does not treat any single heuristic, label, or decentralization claim as dispositive.
- [ ] ML1 escalation is required where securities overlap remains unresolved or materially risky.
