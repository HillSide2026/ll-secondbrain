# Common Artifacts: Shareholder Change

Categories of documents typically evaluated in this solution space.

---

## Transactional Documents

- Share purchase agreements
- Subscription agreements
- Share exchange agreements
- Share redemption agreements

---

## Governance Documents

- Shareholder agreements (new or amended)
- Unanimous shareholder agreements
- Voting agreements or pooling arrangements
- Director/officer resolutions
- Shareholder resolutions (ordinary or special)

---

## Corporate Records

- Share registers (before and after)
- Share certificates (issued or cancelled)
- Articles of amendment (if share structure changes)
- Directors' registers

---

## Regulatory Filings

- Articles of amendment (corporate)
- Annual return updates (if timing coincides)
- Securities filings (if applicable)
- Tax elections (if applicable)

---

## Disclosure Documents

- Disclosure letters
- Representations and warranties schedules
- Officer certificates

---

## Disclaimer

Presence of an artifact category does not imply necessity in any specific transaction.

Absence of an artifact does not imply defect.

The applicable artifacts are determined by the specific facts and structure of each transaction.
