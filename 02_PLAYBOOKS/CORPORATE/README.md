# Corporate Playbooks

Practice-area playbooks for corporate/commercial law matters.

---

## Structure

```
CORPORATE/
├── STRATEGIES/             # High-value engagement architectures
├── SOLUTIONS/              # Solution frames (productized scopes)
│   ├── INCORPORATION/
│   ├── SHAREHOLDER_CHANGE/
│   ├── SHAREHOLDER_CONFLICT/
│   ├── BUSINESS_ACQUISITION/
│   └── CORPORATE_ADVISORY/
├── ISSUE_MAPS/             # Common issue taxonomies
├── FAILURE_MODES/          # Known pitfalls and mitigations
├── REGULATORY_SURFACES/    # Compliance touchpoints
├── QUESTION_BANKS/         # Intake and diligence questions
└── DECISION_LENSES/        # Analytical frameworks
```

---

## STRATEGIES

High-value engagement architectures that may orchestrate multiple solutions. Strategies require ML1 approval to create and live under `STRATEGIES/`.

## SOLUTIONS

Solution frames for specific corporate problem spaces. Each solution includes:
- Scope definition
- Assembly logic
- Common artifacts
- Risk profile

## ISSUE_MAPS

Taxonomies of legal issues by subject matter:
- Governance issues
- Shareholder disputes
- Regulatory compliance
- Contract risks

## FAILURE_MODES

Documented patterns of what goes wrong:
- Missed deadlines
- Incomplete minute books
- Unsigned resolutions
- Improper share issuances

## REGULATORY_SURFACES

Compliance touchpoints and filing requirements:
- Provincial registries
- Federal filings
- Industry-specific regulators

## QUESTION_BANKS

Structured questions for:
- Client intake
- Due diligence
- Risk assessment

## DECISION_LENSES

Analytical frameworks for:
- Structure selection
- Risk evaluation
- Cost-benefit analysis
