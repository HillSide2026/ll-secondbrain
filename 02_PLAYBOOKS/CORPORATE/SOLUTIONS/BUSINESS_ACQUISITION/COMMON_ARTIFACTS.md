## Artifact Discipline

- Artifacts are reused across matters and must not be reinvented
- Level 1 artifacts reflect the lawyer's advisory role; lawyer-drafted documents at Level 1 are out of scope unless specifically instructed
- Presence of an artifact category does not imply necessity in every matter
- Absence does not imply defect
- Conditional artifacts (If applicable) require a documented factual trigger
