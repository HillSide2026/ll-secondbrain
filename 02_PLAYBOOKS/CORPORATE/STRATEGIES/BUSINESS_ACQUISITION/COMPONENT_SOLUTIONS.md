# Component Solutions: Business Acquisition

## Solutions Referenced

This Strategy may invoke the following Tier 1 and Tier 2 Solutions depending on the deal facts.

| Solution | Tier | When Invoked |
|----------|------|--------------|
| INCORPORATION | Tier 1 Solution | Buyer requires a new acquisition vehicle or holding company |
| SHAREHOLDER_AGREEMENT | Tier 1 Solution | Multiple co-buyers require a shareholder agreement for the acquisition vehicle |
| SHAREHOLDER_CHANGE | Tier 1 Solution | Post-closing share transfers or reorganization of buyer entity |
| BUSINESS_ACQUISITION | Tier 2 Solution | Reference for Level 1/2 methodology; artifact templates reused where applicable |

## Solution Independence

Each invoked Solution is a discrete engagement. Invoking a Solution within this Strategy does not merge the mandates — scope, fee, and authorization for each Solution must be separately confirmed.

## Strategy-Specific Artifacts

The following artifacts are generated at the Strategy level and are not part of any component Solution:

- Non-binding LOI
- Diligence summary memo (S-Levels 2–4)
- Working capital adjustment mechanics
- Earnout agreement or schedule
- Rep and warranty insurance assessment (S-Levels 3–4)
- Management / consulting agreements (S-Level 4)
- Formal data room index (S-Level 4)
