# Stage 3.3 — Coverage List Types

## Points to Cover
A flat list of topics that may need to be addressed.

## Questions to Answer
A flat list of open questions implied by the context.

## Risks to Flag
A flat list of potential issues or areas of exposure.
No severity, likelihood, or mitigation.

## Follow-ups
A flat list of possible next items or loose ends.

## Likely Misunderstandings
A flat list of ways the recipient might misinterpret or miss something.

## Global Rules
- Lists only
- No ordering unless explicitly requested
- No annotations beyond the list item itself
