## Matter Context

Kishmish Inc. operated a **Nani's Gelato** franchise at Liberty Village, Toronto (purchased November 2022). The business sustained operating losses from day one, attributable to a problematic location, landlord mismanagement (125% CAM cost increase), and a seasonally dependent model. By late 2025, the clients had exhausted financial resources and sought to exit the franchise.

Levine Law was retained on **October 28, 2025** to negotiate a mutual termination and release. The franchisor (Parry Sohi) issued a Notice of Termination on November 3, 2025. Levine Law responded asserting client rights and opened dialogue with franchisor's counsel (Adam Ship / Janet Erskine of ship-law-adr.ca). A mutual termination and surrender agreement was ultimately negotiated.
