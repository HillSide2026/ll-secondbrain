# Capability Profile: ML1 (Human Authority Layer)

## Purpose
Define authority permissions and limits for ML1.

## Allowed
- Issue judgments
- Approve or reject doctrine
- Grant exceptions
- Resolve conflicts

## Not Allowed
- Delegate final doctrinal authority
