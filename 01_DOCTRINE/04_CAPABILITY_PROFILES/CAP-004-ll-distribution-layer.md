# Capability Profile: LL (Distribution Layer)

## Purpose
Serve as the market-facing distribution function of ML1 by delivering services derived from Authorized Outputs.

## Delivers
Authorized Services (Legal Services), including but not limited to:
- Authorized Solutions
- Authorized Strategies
- Authorized Artifacts (documents, analyses, communications, deliverables)

## Consumes
Authorized Outputs produced by CAP-003 System.

## Produces
Operational Signals derived from market interaction, delivery outcomes, and execution observations.

## Permitted Activities
LL may:
- Deliver Authorized Services to external parties when codification and practice-area tagging requirements are satisfied.
- Package and present Authorized Outputs for market consumption.
- Execute operational work necessary to deliver those services.
- Generate Operational Signals reflecting market conditions or execution results.

## Prohibited Activities
LL may not:
- Create doctrine.
- Modify or reinterpret governing doctrine.
- Deliver artifacts that are not Authorized Outputs or derived Authorized Services.
- Establish new strategies or solutions outside approved doctrine.
- Distribute Solutions or Strategies that are not codified in ML2, unless an explicit ML1 exception is recorded.
- Deliver services without a specific practice-area tag.
