# LLPOL-009: Pre-Conversion Interaction Policy

Policy Statement: Pre-conversion interactions with prospects are diagnostic. Legal analysis, regulatory assessment, and structuring advice are not permitted before a retainer clears.

Authority (Principles referenced): LLPRN-003 (Lead-Client Distinction), LLPRN-005 (Demonstrate Understanding Before Probing)

Related policies: LLPOL-002 (Purse First), LLPOL-003 (New Client Retainer Floor), LLPRO-001 (Intake Qualification Protocol)

---

## 1. Policy Purpose

LLPOL-002 governs when work may begin. This policy governs what the pre-conversion interaction is. The two operate in sequence: this policy defines the format; LLPOL-002 defines the gate. The diagnosis model prevents the pre-conversion interaction from becoming free legal work.

---

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all interactions with prospects, inquiries, referrals, and intake calls before a retainer has cleared.

---

## 3. Rule

| Interaction type | Permitted pre-conversion |
|---|---|
| Emotional analysis (fit assessment, reading the prospect) | Yes |
| Financial analysis (ability to pay, retainer sizing) | Yes |
| Non-legal business analysis (operations, model, problem space) | Yes |
| Domain competence demonstration | Yes |
| Legal analysis of the prospect's situation | No |
| Regulatory characterization or exposure assessment | No |
| Structuring recommendations | No |
| Any work product | No |

---

## 4. Interaction Format

The pre-conversion interaction is a diagnosis: demonstrate domain command first; invite specifics second. The prospect should leave the interaction understanding they have a problem and that this firm can solve it — not with a free analysis of how.

This policy applies regardless of how substantive the conversation was or how strong the relationship signal.

---

## 5. Limits

This policy does not prohibit general educational conversation about the regulatory landscape, published positions, or publicly available information. It prohibits applying that analysis to the prospect's specific situation before a retainer clears.
