# LLPOL-003: New Client Retainer Floor

Policy Statement: New clients pay a minimum retainer of CAD $5,000 before any engagement commences. Below-floor retainers require ML1-approved justification.

Authority (Principles referenced): LLPRN-007 (Standard Retainer Floor), LLPRN-006 (Retainer Precedence)

Related policies: LLPOL-001 (Firm Self-Interest), LLPOL-002 (Purse First)

---

## 1. Policy Purpose

A retainer floor screens for clients who are serious, qualified, and prepared to pay. It protects against scope creep on low-value matters and establishes economic expectations from the first interaction.

---

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all new client engagements. A new client is any person or entity without a currently active matter at the firm.

This policy does not apply to:
- Additional matters opened for an existing client under an active retainer
- Referral matters where a separate retainer structure is ML1-approved

---

## 3. Rule

| Condition | Minimum retainer |
|---|---|
| New client, standard engagement | CAD $5,000 cleared before work begins |
| New client, below-floor exception | ML1 approval required; documented at the matter level |

The $5,000 floor is the minimum, not the target. Complex matters, urgent timelines, or high-risk work should carry a higher retainer calibrated to the matter.

---

## 4. Below-Floor Exceptions

Exceptions are permitted only when:
- ML1 makes a considered commercial or strategic judgment
- The exception is documented in the matter record with the rationale

Exceptions are not granted by the system, agents, or administrative staff. ML1 decides.
