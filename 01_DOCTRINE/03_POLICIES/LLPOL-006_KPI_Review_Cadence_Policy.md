# LLPOL-006: KPI Review Cadence Policy

Policy Statement: ML1 reviews the firm's operating KPI dashboard monthly. The review is mandatory, not discretionary.

Authority (Principles referenced): LLPRN-024 (KPI-Governed Scaling)

Related policies: LLPOL-001 (Firm Self-Interest), LLPRO-004 (KPI Dashboard Review Protocol)

---

## 1. Policy Purpose

The operating targets are defined in LLPRN-024. This policy creates the review obligation. A target without a review cadence is not a governance instrument.

---

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to firm-level operating KPIs. It does not govern matter-level reporting or client-level economics (see LLPRO-005).

---

## 3. Rule

| Cadence | Obligation |
|---|---|
| Monthly | ML1 reviews all eight KPIs against targets |
| Monthly | At least one decision output recorded: intake, pricing, staffing, or collections |
| On breach | A KPI outside target range triggers a documented response before the next review cycle |

The review is a decision-making exercise, not a reporting exercise. If all KPIs are within range, that is the decision output.

---

## 4. KPI Set

The eight KPIs and targets are defined in LLPRN-024:

| KPI | Target |
|---|---|
| Realization rate | 85–95% |
| Collection rate | 90%+ |
| A/R days | Under 45 |
| Recurring revenue % | 40–70% |
| Utilization | 70–80% productive time |
| Client concentration | No single client over 15% |
| Gross margin | 40–50% |
| Write-offs | Under 5% of billings |
