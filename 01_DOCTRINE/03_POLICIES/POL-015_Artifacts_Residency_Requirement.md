# POL-015 — Artifacts Residency Requirement

Policy Statement: All governing artifacts (playbooks, templates, runs, and integration specifications) must reside in ML2.
Authority (Principles referenced): PRN-005, PRN-009, PRN-018, PRN-021
Enforcement expectation: Governing artifacts maintained outside ML2 are non-authoritative operational copies and must not be treated as canonical.
