# POL-025 — No Direct External Access by Workers

Policy Statement: Workers may not access external tools, services, or platforms except through the agent-mediated integration path defined by policy and protocol.
Authority (Principles referenced): PRN-022, PRN-024, PRN-027
Enforcement expectation: Direct worker access to external systems is non-compliant and must be blocked.
