# POL-048 — Off-Template Asset Compliance and Retirement Policy

Policy Statement: Any LL asset created outside the approved template system must undergo compliance review before distribution. Non-compliant assets must be corrected or retired.

Authority (Principles referenced): PRN-030, PRN-031, PRN-032, PRN-033, PRN-034, PRN-035  
Enforcement expectation: Off-template assets lacking review evidence or failing compliance checks are non-authorized for distribution.  
Supersedes: None

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines mandatory controls for assets created outside the approved template system.

---

## 2. Scope

Applies to any brand-relevant LL asset not derived from an approved template, including Canva designs, social graphics, one-pagers, slides, and report visuals.

---

## 3. Mandatory Compliance Review

Before distribution, each off-template asset must be reviewed for:
- color palette compliance
- typography system compliance
- layout structure compliance
- tone alignment compliance

Review evidence must be recorded in run or review artifacts.

---

## 4. Disposition Rules

If compliant:
- asset may proceed to normal approval flow.

If non-compliant:
- asset must be corrected and re-reviewed, or
- asset must be retired from active use.

---

## 5. Authorization Boundary

Passing compliance review does not by itself authorize external use.  
Distribution authority remains subject to existing ML1 approval and output authorization policies.

---

## 6. Relationship to Existing Doctrine

This policy operates with:
- `POL-004` (External Output Approval Requirement)
- `POL-046` (Canva Template Enforcement Policy)
- `POL-047` (LL Brand Governance and ML2 Custody Policy)
- `POL-049` (LL Brand Color Palette Policy)

---

## 7. Summary

Off-template assets are permitted only under controlled review.  
Non-compliant assets are corrected or retired, never silently distributed.
