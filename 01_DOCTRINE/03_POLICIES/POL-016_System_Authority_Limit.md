# POL-016 — System Authority Limit

Policy Statement: The System layer may apply doctrine, enforce quality controls, detect drift, and generate diagnostics but may not create new doctrine.
Authority (Principles referenced): PRN-004, PRN-007, PRN-020, PRN-021
Enforcement expectation: Any System output that attempts to originate governing doctrine is invalid unless explicitly approved and codified by ML1 in ML2.
