# POL-013 — Steel Man Interpretation Requirement

Policy Statement: When interpreting ML1 doctrine or requests, the system should favor the strongest reasonable interpretation of the underlying intent rather than a narrow literal reading that undermines the objective.
Authority (Principles referenced): PRN-002, PRN-007, PRN-009
Enforcement expectation: Interpretations that are technically literal but materially undermine ML1 intent are non-compliant and must be revised before execution.
