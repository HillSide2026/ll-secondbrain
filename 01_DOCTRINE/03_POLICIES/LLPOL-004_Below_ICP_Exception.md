# LLPOL-004: Below-ICP Exception Policy

Policy Statement: A matter that does not meet ICP-01 or ICP-02 may not be accepted without ML1 approval and a documented rationale at the matter level.

Authority (Principles referenced): LLPRN-002 (Client Quality Precedes Revenue)

Related policies: LLPOL-001 (Firm Self-Interest), LLPOL-003 (New Client Retainer Floor), LLPRO-001 (Intake Qualification Protocol)

---

## 1. Policy Purpose

Closes the gap between the ICP filter as a principle and its operational enforcement at intake. Exceptions are permitted; they require a decision, not a default.

---

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all new matter intake and to any existing client opening a matter outside their established ICP classification.

---

## 3. ICP Definitions

**ICP-01:** Ontario operator, CAD $5M+ cash flow, accountant involved, not in acute crisis.

**ICP-02:** Payments/MSB/fintech operator with Canadian regulatory exposure.

---

## 4. Rule

| Condition | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Matter fits ICP-01 or ICP-02 | Proceed through standard intake |
| Matter does not fit either ICP | ML1 approval required before proceeding |
| Exception approved | Rationale documented at the matter level before engagement letter issued |
| Exception not approved | Refer or decline |

Exceptions do not set precedent. Each exception is matter-specific and does not lower the ICP filter for future intake.

---

## 5. Limits

This policy does not prevent ML1 from accepting strategic, relationship, or pro bono matters outside ICP. It requires that the decision be conscious and documented, not default.
