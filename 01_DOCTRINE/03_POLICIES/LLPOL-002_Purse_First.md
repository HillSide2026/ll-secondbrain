# LLPOL-002: Purse First

Policy Statement: No legal work, advice, analysis, or professional engagement commences before the retainer is received. Receipt means funds cleared — not invoiced, not promised.

Authority (Principles referenced): LLPRN-006 (Retainer Precedence)

Related policies: LLPOL-001 (Firm Self-Interest), LLPOL-003 (New Client Retainer Floor)

---

## 1. Policy Purpose

Work without payment is a gift. This policy closes the gap between instruction and payment so that no legal obligation runs before economic protection does.

---

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to:
- New client engagements
- New matters with existing clients
- Scope expansions on active matters where additional retainer is required

---

## 3. Rule

| Stage | Rule |
|---|---|
| Intake conversation | Permitted — scoping, conflict check, relationship discussion |
| Legal advice | Not permitted until retainer is cleared |
| Work product | Not permitted until retainer is cleared |
| Analysis or assessment | Not permitted until retainer is cleared |
| Time on file | Not permitted until retainer is cleared |

Cleared means the funds are in the trust account or the payment method has confirmed. An invoice, a verbal commitment, or an e-transfer that has not settled does not satisfy this policy.

---

## 4. Limits

Intake and pre-retainer conversations do not constitute legal advice and are not governed by this policy. What constitutes a pre-retainer intake conversation versus advice is ML1's judgment call. When in doubt, get the retainer first.
