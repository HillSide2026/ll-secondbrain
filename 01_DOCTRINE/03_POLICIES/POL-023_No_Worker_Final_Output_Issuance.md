# POL-023 — No Worker Final Output Issuance

Policy Statement: Workers (including subagents) may generate intermediate artifacts, drafts, classifications, extractions, or QA findings, but may not issue final outputs.
Authority (Principles referenced): PRN-025, PRN-027
Enforcement expectation: Any worker-produced artifact labeled or treated as a final output is non-compliant and must be rejected or reclassified as an intermediate artifact.
