# POL-031 — Escalation on Boundary Failure

Policy Statement: If a required task cannot be completed within assigned capability boundaries, the system must escalate rather than expand authority implicitly.
Authority (Principles referenced): PRN-020, PRN-028
Enforcement expectation: Implicit authority expansion when boundaries fail is non-compliant and must trigger escalation handling.
