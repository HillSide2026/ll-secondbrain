# LLPOL-007: Hiring Gate Policy

Policy Statement: No new hire is authorized until the firm can forecast demand, margin, and collections behavior with reasonable confidence. Hiring follows demonstrated, forecastable demand — it does not precede it.

Authority (Principles referenced): LLPRN-024 (KPI-Governed Scaling), LLPRN-001 (Capacity Ceiling)

Related policies: LLPOL-001 (Firm Self-Interest), LLPOL-006 (KPI Review Cadence Policy)

---

## 1. Policy Purpose

Hiring to absorb capacity pressure before the operating model is stable amplifies instability. Growth amplifies what already exists; it does not cure it.

---

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all new hires: setter, junior associate, intermediate lawyer, support staff, or any engagement that adds fixed labor cost.

---

## 3. Preconditions

All four preconditions must be satisfied before a hiring decision is authorized:

| Precondition | Standard |
|---|---|
| Demand | Pipeline is stable and forecastable over a 90-day horizon |
| Margin | Gross margin at or above target (40–50%) for at least two consecutive monthly review cycles |
| Collections | Collection rate at or above target (90%+) and A/R days within target (under 45) for at least two consecutive monthly review cycles |
| Capacity | ML1 capacity ceiling is persistently hit despite intake discipline at ICP standards |

---

## 4. Limits

No precondition is waivable without ML1-documented rationale at the decision record. Hiring under emergency capacity pressure without satisfying preconditions requires explicit ML1 exception.
