# POL-023 — No Worker Output Authorization

Policy Statement: Workers (including subagents) may generate intermediate artifacts, drafts, classifications, extractions, or QA findings, but may not issue Authorized Outputs.
Authority (Principles referenced): PRN-025, PRN-027
Enforcement expectation: Any worker-produced artifact labeled or treated as an Authorized Output is non-compliant and must be rejected or reclassified.
