# POL-033 — Conversion Qualification and Revenue Trigger

Policy Statement: Conversion (Onboarding) status may be assigned only when objective conversion evidence exists and is recorded in governed artifacts.

Qualified conversion evidence includes one or more of:
- Engagement agreement signed
- Retainer received
- Invoice paid

Classification rule:
- Inquiry (Intake) indicates initiated contact and does not itself qualify as Conversion/Onboarding.
- Conversion/Onboarding is the terminal stage of marketing and is the revenue trigger boundary for marketing reporting.

Evidence requirement:
- Every Conversion/Onboarding classification must include evidence reference(s) to the governing artifact basis.

Authority (Principles referenced): PRN-008, PRN-026, PRN-029
Enforcement expectation: Conversion/Onboarding classification without objective evidence is non-compliant and must be blocked or reverted to Inquiry/Intake pending evidence.
