# PRN-013 - Risk Lifecycle Governance

Title: Risk Lifecycle Governance

Statement:
Risk governance should be lifecycle-complete, schema-explicit, and gate-reviewed by ML1.

Rationale:
Risk quality degrades when capture is ad hoc, late, or unaudited. A lifecycle discipline preserves comparability, auditability, and decision quality across project types.

Implications:
- Every project stage should expose risk state through a defined artifact.
- Risk artifacts should use explicit schemas, not free-form narrative.
- Stage gates should require human judgment at closure.
- Closing should convert realized issues into reusable lessons.
