# PRN-037 — Wealth Building

Title: Wealth Building

Statement:
Every HillSide initiative must be evaluated against its contribution to building wealth. Initiatives that do not build wealth — directly or by enabling it — must be justified or deprioritized.

Rationale:
Wealth creation is the primary purpose of the HillSide portfolio. Resources, time, and attention are finite. Allocating them to non-wealth-building activity without justification is a form of drift.
