# PRN-033 - Levine Law Image Style Restraint

Title: Levine Law Image Style Restraint

Statement:
Levine Law visuals should avoid legal cliches (including gavels, courthouse stock photography, handshake tropes, and generic skyline imagery) and favor calm, intelligent, restrained imagery such as architectural minimalism, geometric form language, quiet environments, and editorial-style photography.

Rationale:
Image restraint reinforces differentiated authority and reduces generic legal-brand signaling that weakens trust and perceived sophistication.
