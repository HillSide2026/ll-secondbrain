# PRN-020 - Authority-Boundary Clarity

Title: Authority-Boundary Clarity

Statement:
Keep layer roles explicit in interpretation and execution: ML1 decides, ML2 records, System enforces, LL executes. If an action crosses layer scope, route it to the proper authority.
Matter relevance to ML1 does not by itself create an ML1 action requirement, and delegated fulfillment remains delegated unless escalation criteria are met.

Rationale:
Explicit boundary discipline prevents role confusion, authority creep, invalid cross-layer actions, and the mistaken conversion of visibility into intervention.
