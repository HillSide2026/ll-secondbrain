# PRN-017 - Progressive Formalization

Title: Progressive Formalization

Statement:
Repeated decisions or interpretations should gradually be formalized into doctrine when patterns emerge.

Rationale:
Codifying recurring patterns improves consistency, reduces ambiguity, and strengthens institutional memory.

Implication:
This principle encourages the system to surface candidates for codification.
