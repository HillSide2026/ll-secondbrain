# LLPRN-007 — Standard Retainer Floor

Title: Standard Retainer Floor

Statement:
The standard LL retainer is CAD $5,000. Any retainer below this amount requires explicit justification approved by ML1.

Rationale:
A retainer floor is an economic discipline mechanism. Accepting retainers below the floor without justification degrades revenue quality, signals low confidence in the firm's value, and undermines the pricing discipline that LLPRN-018 through LLPRN-023 are designed to protect. A below-floor retainer is not automatically wrong — but it must be a deliberate decision, not a default.

Application Notes:
1. The floor applies to all matter types and service tiers unless a specific exception is approved.
2. Justification for below-floor retainers may include: strategic client relationship, referral source, pro bono designation, or transitional arrangement — but must be recorded.
3. A discounted retainer is distinct from a below-floor retainer: a discount from a higher amount that lands above $5,000 is not subject to this principle.
4. This principle does not govern fintech retainer mandates, which are separately governed by LLPRN-008 and their own tier structure.
