# LLPRN-029 - Levine Law Voice and Copy Alignment

Title: Levine Law Voice and Copy Alignment

Statement:
Levine Law visual outputs should use copy that is direct, structured, analytical, and confident, and should avoid hype language, marketing cliches, and exaggerated claims.

Rationale:
Brand trust depends on coherence between visual identity and written expression. Consistent voice discipline preserves authority and reduces signal drift across channels.
