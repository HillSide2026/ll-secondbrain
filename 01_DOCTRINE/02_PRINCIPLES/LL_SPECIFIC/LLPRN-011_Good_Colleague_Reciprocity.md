# LLPRN-011 - Good Colleague Reciprocity

Title: Good Colleague Reciprocity

Statement:
Good colleagues get quid pro quo: value given, value returned. Reciprocity operates on a running account over a reasonable horizon, not transaction by transaction. Referral quality matters: a colleague who sends poor-fit work is not providing value. Behavior within the good-colleague network is highly observable and compounds through network reputation (cross-references LLPRN-017).

Rationale:
Reciprocity calibrated to demonstrated value maintains professional equity and prevents exploitation of relationship goodwill. Unconditional reciprocity extended to any colleague dilutes the signal value of the good-colleague relationship and misallocates professional resources.
