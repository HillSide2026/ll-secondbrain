# PRN-014 - Deterministic Preference

Title: Deterministic Preference

Statement:
When multiple reasoning paths are possible, prefer interpretations that produce consistent results across repeated executions.

Rationale:
Consistency improves reliability, comparability, and trust in governed system behavior.
