# RN-013 - Reversibility

Title: Reversibility

Statement:
System changes should prefer reversible operations and preserve the ability to restore prior states when feasible.

Rationale:
Reversibility reduces downside risk, supports safe iteration, and improves governance resilience when assumptions prove wrong.
