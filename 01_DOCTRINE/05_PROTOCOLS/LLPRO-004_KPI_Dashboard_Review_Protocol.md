# LLPRO-004 — KPI Dashboard Review Protocol

**Trigger:** Monthly — following the A/R Review (LLPRO-003).

---

## Purpose

Implements LLPOL-006's mandatory monthly review obligation. Defines the metrics reviewed, the targets applied, and the decision outputs required. The review is a decision-making exercise, not a reporting exercise.

---

## Metrics and Targets

From LLPRN-024:

| KPI | Target |
|---|---|
| Realization rate | 85–95% |
| Collection rate | 90%+ |
| A/R days | Under 45 |
| Recurring revenue % | 40–70% |
| Utilization | 70–80% productive time |
| Client concentration | No single client over 15% of revenue |
| Gross margin | 40–50% |
| Write-offs | Under 5% of billings |

---

## Steps

**Step 1 — Compile current metrics.**
Pull current values for all eight KPIs. A/R days feeds from LLPRO-003 output.

**Step 2 — Compare to targets.**
Flag any KPI outside its target range.

**Step 3 — Generate decision outputs.**
For each KPI in breach, identify and record the decision response:

| KPI area | Decision output |
|---|---|
| Realization or margin declining | Review pricing; adjust retainer sizing or billing rates |
| A/R days or collection rate degrading | Escalate collections; tighten LLPOL-005 enforcement |
| Recurring revenue below target | Review pipeline; assess retainer renewal and BD activity |
| Utilization outside range | Adjust intake; review matter mix or capacity allocation |
| Client concentration above limit | No new mandates with that client; active diversification |
| Write-offs above target | Review intake qualification; tighten scope discipline |
| Capacity ceiling persistently hit | Flag for LLPOL-007 hiring gate assessment |

**Step 4 — Record outcome.**
Document metrics reviewed and decisions taken. If all KPIs are within range, record that as the outcome.

---

## Output

Decision record: metrics reviewed, any breaches noted, decisions taken. Retained at the firm level.
