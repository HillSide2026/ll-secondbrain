# LLPRO-002 — Engagement Risk Assessment Protocol

**Trigger:** After LLPRO-001 passes — before the engagement letter is issued.

---

## Purpose

Requires ML1 to assess the five LLINV-004 business risk categories against the specific matter before committing. LLINV-004 states that assessment is required; this protocol defines when and at what level of formality.

---

## Assessment

For each category, ML1 considers whether the matter presents material risk. No formal scoring is required. For high-complexity or high-value matters, the assessment is noted at the matter level.

### 1. Revenue Risk
Does this matter add concentration risk? Does it depend on a single engagement spike with no recurring component?

### 2. Realization Risk
Is scope defined well enough to bill and collect predictably? Is the client's payment behavior known or assessable? Are there signals of future write-off risk?

### 3. Capacity Risk
Does accepting this matter displace higher-value work currently in or near the pipeline? Is there a delegation path, or is this fully founder-dependent?

### 4. Scope Risk
Is the mandate clearly bounded? Are client expectations aligned with the engagement terms as understood at intake? Is there ambiguity that should be resolved before the engagement letter is issued?

### 5. Professional and Regulatory Risk
Does the matter carry unusual professional liability exposure, conflict risk, or regulatory sensitivity? Are there LSOO compliance considerations specific to this engagement?

---

## Formality Standard

| Matter type | Assessment standard |
|---|---|
| Routine, standard scope | Mental assessment sufficient; no documentation required |
| High complexity, high value, or elevated risk on any category | Note at the matter level before issuing engagement letter |

---

## Outputs

| Result | Condition |
|---|---|
| Proceed to engagement letter | No material risk flags, or flags are manageable within engagement terms |
| Proceed with conditions | Risk present but manageable — specific conditions documented (scope clarification, higher retainer, explicit exclusions) |
| Decline | Risk unacceptable on one or more categories |
