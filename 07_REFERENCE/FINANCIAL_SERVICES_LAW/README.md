# Reference — Financial Services Law

## Purpose

Stable, authoritative reference material for financial services law matters.
Covers statutes, regulations, guidance, and canonical interpretations relevant
to the firm's Financial Services / Payments practice area.

---

## Regulatory Landscape

| Regime | Statute / Instrument | Regulator | Relevance |
|--------|---------------------|-----------|-----------|
| AML / KYC | Proceeds of Crime (Money Laundering) and Terrorist Financing Act (PCMLTFA) | FINTRAC | MSB registration, compliance programs, effectiveness reviews, STR obligations |
| Payments Supervision | Retail Payment Activities Act (RPAA) | Bank of Canada | PSP registration, safeguarding, annual reporting, three-year review |
| Crypto Tax Reporting | Crypto-Asset Reporting Framework (CARF) | CRA | Reporting obligations for crypto-asset service providers |
| Payment Rail Access | Various (Payments Canada, card network rules, correspondent banking) | Multiple | Bank onboarding, rail access, de-risking |
