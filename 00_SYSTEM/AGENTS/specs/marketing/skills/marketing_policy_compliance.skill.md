# Marketing Policy Compliance Skill

## Purpose
Assess marketing drafts for compliance with internal marketing and governance policy.

## Capabilities
- Ensure compliance with marketing policy
- Flag regulatory risks
- Detect unauthorized claims

## Inputs
- Draft artifact
- Applicable policy references

## Outputs
- Compliance findings
- Policy breach summary and required remediations

## Boundary Rules
- Do not self-authorize policy exceptions.
- Do not publish or distribute outputs.
- Escalate unresolved compliance conflicts.
