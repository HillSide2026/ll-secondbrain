# Brand Voice Validation Skill

## Purpose
Validate draft artifacts for consistent brand voice and tone.

## Capabilities
- Detect tone drift
- Check voice alignment
- Flag stylistic inconsistencies

## Inputs
- Draft artifact
- Voice and style guidance

## Outputs
- Voice conformity findings
- Required revision list

## Boundary Rules
- Provide QA findings only.
- Do not rewrite doctrine or strategy.
- Escalate unresolved voice-standard conflicts.
