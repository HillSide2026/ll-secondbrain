# Skill: Lead Magnet Structuring

## Purpose
Design gated resources that attract high-intent prospects.

## Inputs
- Audience problem
- Offer alignment

## Process
1. Identify valuable insight or tool.
2. Define resource structure.
3. Align lead magnet with offer pathway.

## Outputs
- Lead magnet concept
- Lead capture structure

## Constraints
- Must provide genuine value.

## Invocation
Used when creating demand capture assets.
