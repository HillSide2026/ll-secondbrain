# Factual Claim Validation Skill

## Purpose
Check drafts for unsupported, exaggerated, or uncertain factual claims.

## Capabilities
- Detect unsupported claims
- Flag exaggerations
- Identify factual uncertainty

## Inputs
- Draft content
- Available supporting evidence or references

## Outputs
- Claim validation report
- Evidence gap list

## Boundary Rules
- No fabrication or assumption of missing evidence.
- Findings are advisory for promotion decisions.
- Escalate critical uncertainty when evidence is absent.
