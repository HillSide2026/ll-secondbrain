# Content Adaptation Skill

## Purpose
Adapt source content into channel-specific draft formats without changing strategic intent.

## Capabilities
- Convert long form to social
- Convert article to newsletter
- Convert strategy to script

## Inputs
- Approved source draft
- Channel or format requirements

## Outputs
- Adapted draft variants by channel
- Transformation notes

## Boundary Rules
- Preserve approved messaging intent.
- Do not introduce new unsupported claims.
- Escalate if adaptation requires strategy-level changes.
