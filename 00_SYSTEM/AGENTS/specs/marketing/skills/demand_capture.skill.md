# Skill: Demand Capture

## Purpose
Identify and intercept high-intent market demand and route it into conversion pathways.

## Inputs
- Market demand signals
- Keyword or search patterns
- Audience behavior indicators
- Available offers

## Process
1. Identify expressions of existing demand:
   - search queries
   - inbound inquiries
   - industry conversations
2. Map demand signals to relevant offers.
3. Define capture mechanisms:
   - landing pages
   - inquiry forms
   - booking links
   - gated resources
4. Align messaging with buyer intent.
5. Route captured demand into appropriate funnel stages.

## Outputs
- Demand capture pathway
- Intent-aligned messaging
- Conversion entry points

## Constraints
- Must target high-intent signals where possible.
- Capture mechanisms must align with offer structure.
- Cannot create misleading or deceptive entry points.

## Invocation
Used by funnel or marketing strategy agents to convert existing market demand into leads or engagements.
