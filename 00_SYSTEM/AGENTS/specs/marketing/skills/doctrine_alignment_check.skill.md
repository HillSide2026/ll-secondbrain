# Doctrine Alignment Check Skill

## Purpose
Verify marketing drafts align with governing doctrine and approved strategy.

## Capabilities
- Compare draft against doctrine
- Flag positioning violations
- Identify messaging conflicts

## Inputs
- Draft artifact
- Referenced doctrine and strategy artifacts

## Outputs
- Doctrine alignment findings
- Blocking and non-blocking issues

## Boundary Rules
- Do not reinterpret doctrine beyond explicit text.
- Do not grant policy exceptions.
- Escalate ambiguity to authorized authority layers.
