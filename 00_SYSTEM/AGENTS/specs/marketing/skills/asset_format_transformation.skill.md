# Skill: Asset Format Transformation

## Purpose
Convert marketing assets into alternative formats required for specific channels.

## Inputs
- Approved content artifact
- Target format specification

## Process
1. Identify required format.
2. Extract relevant content components.
3. Transform structure to target format.
4. Preserve messaging consistency.

## Outputs
- Reformatted marketing asset
- Channel-compatible content variant

## Constraints
- No new messaging may be introduced.
- Transformations must preserve intent.

## Invocation
Used when adapting content for multiple distribution channels.
