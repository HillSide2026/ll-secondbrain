# Skill: Asset Version Management

## Purpose
Maintain version history of marketing artifacts.

## Inputs
- Artifact revision
- Existing version record

## Process
1. Compare artifact versions.
2. Assign new version identifier.
3. Update version lineage.

## Outputs
- Versioned artifact record
- Updated lineage log

## Constraints
- Historical records must not be overwritten.

## Invocation
Used when artifacts are revised or promoted.
