# Skill: Platform Voice Calibration

## Purpose
Adjust messaging tone to match platform norms.

## Inputs
- Content artifact
- Target platform

## Process
1. Evaluate platform tone conventions.
2. Adjust phrasing and structure.
3. Maintain message clarity.

## Outputs
- Platform-appropriate content version

## Constraints
- Cannot alter core meaning.

## Invocation
Used when preparing social content for specific platforms.
