# Skill: Performance Signal Reporting

## Purpose
Produce structured reports summarizing operational marketing signals.

## Inputs
- Engagement analysis
- Drift observations
- Campaign data

## Process
1. Consolidate signal observations.
2. Highlight significant patterns.
3. Identify candidates for strategy review.

## Outputs
- Operational signal report
- Strategy refinement candidates

## Constraints
- Reports only.
- No strategy modification authority.

## Invocation
Used by Market Signal Agent for ML1/System reporting.
