# Skill: Short Form Narrative Structuring

## Purpose
Convert ideas into concise, high-impact short-form content.

## Inputs
- Source content
- Audience definition

## Process
1. Extract central insight.
2. Simplify narrative structure.
3. Emphasize clarity and engagement.

## Outputs
- Short-form post structure

## Constraints
- Must preserve factual accuracy.

## Invocation
Used when producing short-form social assets.
