# Skill: Revenue Attribution

## Purpose
Connect marketing activity to revenue outcomes by identifying which marketing inputs contributed to client acquisition or transaction revenue.

## Inputs
- Lead source data
- Campaign identifiers
- Conversion events
- Client engagement records
- Revenue records

## Process
1. Identify revenue-generating events:
   - signed engagement
   - completed transaction
   - subscription initiation
2. Trace the client acquisition pathway.
3. Identify marketing touchpoints involved in the pathway.
4. Assign attribution according to defined model:
   - first-touch
   - last-touch
   - multi-touch
5. Produce attribution summary linking marketing activity to revenue.

## Outputs
- Revenue attribution report
- Campaign revenue contribution analysis
- Marketing ROI indicators

## Constraints
- Attribution must be based on observable data.
- Attribution models must be documented and consistent.
- Cannot infer causation without supporting evidence.

## Invocation
Used by market signal or analytics agents when evaluating marketing performance and campaign effectiveness.
