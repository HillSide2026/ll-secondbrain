# Skill: Brand Kit Enforcement

## Purpose
Enforce approved brand kit constraints across generated design artifacts.

## Inputs
- Populated design draft
- Brand kit rules
- Logo and palette references

## Process
1. Validate color usage against approved palette.
2. Validate logo variant and placement rules.
3. Check typography and hierarchy constraints.
4. Flag or correct non-compliant brand elements.

## Outputs
- Brand compliance result
- Corrected design draft (allowed adjustments only)
- Brand deviation log

## Constraints
- Never introduce non-approved brand assets.
- Do not alter substantive messaging content.
- Major conflicts must escalate for review.

## Invocation
Used before legal and policy preflight checks.
