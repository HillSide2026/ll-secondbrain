# Payment Services Law Analysis Skill

## Purpose
Analyze whether a Funnel 3 fact pattern likely raises Canadian payment-services,
RPAA, safeguarding, operational-risk, or registration issues.

## Capabilities
- Assess whether a fact pattern likely triggers payment-function / PSP analysis
- Map business functions to likely RPAA and related federal payment-services relevance
- Distinguish pure payment-services issues from MSB or securities overlap
- Rate client-model risk and touchability in commercially useful terms

## Inputs
- Fact pattern or entity-model summary
- Business functions and payment model
- Jurisdictional hooks
- Available source documents

## Outputs
- Payment-services issue map
- Authority applicability assessment
- Risk rating and touchability assessment
- ML1 next-decision recommendation

## Boundary Rules
- Do not collapse preliminary issue spotting into definitive legal conclusions.
- Do not ignore missing facts; mark confidence accordingly.
- Do not optimize toward permissive answers.
- Escalate when the model appears too risky to touch without major change.
