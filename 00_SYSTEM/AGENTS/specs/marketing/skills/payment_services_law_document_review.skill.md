# Payment Services Law Document Review Skill

## Purpose
Review payment-services-adjacent documents for PSP, RPAA, safeguarding, and
registration-risk signals relevant to Funnel 3.

## Capabilities
- Review drafts, webpages, intake notes, flow descriptions, and business-model
  summaries for payment-services-relevant issues
- Flag language that understates payment-function, safeguarding, operational-risk,
  or registration issues
- Identify where missing assumptions, caveats, or facts materially affect the
  assessment

## Inputs
- Draft document or artifact
- Supporting fact pattern or source references
- Applicable regulatory lens where known

## Outputs
- Document review findings
- Perimeter-risk and safeguarding-risk flags
- Recommended guardrails or revisions

## Boundary Rules
- Do not approve outward-facing claims without broader governance review.
- Do not treat document review as a substitute for full fact-pattern analysis.
- Do not soften findings to preserve marketing appeal.
- Escalate when the document suggests a model that may be too risky to touch.
