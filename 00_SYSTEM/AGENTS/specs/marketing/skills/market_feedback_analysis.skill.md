# Skill: Market Feedback Analysis

## Purpose
Interpret engagement signals to identify patterns and audience reactions.

## Inputs
- Engagement dataset
- Campaign objectives

## Process
1. Evaluate engagement patterns.
2. Identify content resonance signals.
3. Detect anomalies or weak performance areas.

## Outputs
- Market response analysis
- Engagement interpretation summary

## Constraints
- Observational analysis only.
- Cannot change strategy.

## Invocation
Used by Market Signal Agent after data collection.
