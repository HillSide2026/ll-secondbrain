# Skill: Differentiation Detection

## Purpose
Identify opportunities for distinctive positioning.

## Inputs
- Market position map
- Firm capabilities
- Competitor messaging

## Process
1. Identify overcrowded messaging themes.
2. Identify underused positioning areas.
3. Evaluate alignment with firm strengths.

## Outputs
- Differentiation opportunity report

## Constraints
- Cannot invent unsupported positioning claims.

## Invocation
Used when evaluating strategic differentiation opportunities.
