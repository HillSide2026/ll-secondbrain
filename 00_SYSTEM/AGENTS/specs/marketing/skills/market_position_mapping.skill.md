# Skill: Market Position Mapping

## Purpose
Map positioning of firms or offers within a market.

## Inputs
- Competitor set
- Messaging themes

## Process
1. Identify positioning dimensions.
2. Map competitors across dimensions.
3. Identify positioning clusters.

## Outputs
- Market positioning map

## Constraints
- Must reflect observable positioning signals.

## Invocation
Used to understand competitive landscape.
