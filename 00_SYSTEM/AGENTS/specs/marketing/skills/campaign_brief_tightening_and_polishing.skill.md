# Campaign Brief Tightening And Polishing Skill

## Purpose
Refine an existing campaign brief so the objective, audience, boundaries,
claims, and execution guidance are more precise and easier to use.

## Capabilities
- Tighten brief language and decision framing
- Clarify audience, scope, and execution boundaries
- Remove ambiguity, drift, or unnecessary softness
- Improve internal usability without changing the approved campaign thesis

## Inputs
- Existing campaign brief
- Approved strategy or doctrine references
- Known constraints, blockers, or guardrails

## Outputs
- Tightened campaign brief
- Brief-polishing notes
- Ambiguity and drift flags

## Boundary Rules
- Do not create campaign strategy from scratch.
- Do not alter governing doctrine through polish edits.
- Do not broaden the audience or claims without authority.
- Escalate when the brief needs redesign rather than refinement.
