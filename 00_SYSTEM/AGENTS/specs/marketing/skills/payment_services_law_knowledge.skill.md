# Payment Services Law Knowledge Skill

## Purpose
Provide bounded working knowledge of Canadian payment-services regulation
relevant to Funnel 3 PSP and RPAA-adjacent fact patterns.

## Capabilities
- Recall the practical role of core payment-services concepts such as
  `payment function`, `PSP`, `end-user funds`, `safeguarding`,
  `operational risk`, and `registration`
- Identify likely RPAA / Bank of Canada relevance for an entity model
- Distinguish payment-services issues from MSB or securities issues without
  collapsing them together

## Inputs
- Entity or business-model description
- Relevant guidance, legislation, or source doctrine
- Funnel 3 context or target artifact

## Outputs
- Payment-services concept map
- Core authority relevance note
- Initial issue inventory

## Boundary Rules
- Do not assume all payment models fall within RPAA.
- Do not assume RPAA inapplicability without grounded analysis.
- Do not present internal knowledge output as final legal advice.
- Escalate mixed-regime issues for deeper analysis.
