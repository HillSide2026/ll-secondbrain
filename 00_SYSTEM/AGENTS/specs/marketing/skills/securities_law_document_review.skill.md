# Securities Law Document Review Skill

## Purpose
Review securities-adjacent documents for claim risk, perimeter risk, and
regulatory-structure signals relevant to Funnel 3.

## Capabilities
- Review drafts, decks, webpages, intake notes, token descriptions, and
  business-model summaries for securities-law-relevant issues
- Flag language that overstates non-security status or understates platform,
  custody, distribution, or resale risk
- Identify where missing disclosures, assumptions, or caveats materially affect
  risk assessment

## Inputs
- Draft document or artifact
- Any supporting fact pattern or source references
- Applicable regulatory lens or instruments where known

## Outputs
- Document review findings
- Claim-risk and perimeter-risk flags
- Recommended guardrails or revisions

## Boundary Rules
- Do not approve outward-facing claims without broader governance review.
- Do not treat document review as a substitute for full fact-pattern analysis.
- Do not soften findings to preserve marketing appeal.
- Escalate when the document suggests a model that may be too risky to touch.
