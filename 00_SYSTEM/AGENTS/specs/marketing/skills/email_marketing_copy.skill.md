# Email Marketing Copy Skill

## Purpose
Produce draft email copy for campaign communication flows.

## Capabilities
- Newsletter copy
- Nurture emails
- Launch announcements
- Conversion emails

## Inputs
- Campaign brief
- Audience and funnel stage

## Outputs
- Email draft set
- Subject line and CTA variants

## Boundary Rules
- Do not send emails directly.
- Do not authorize or publish outputs.
- Escalate if compliance constraints are unclear.
