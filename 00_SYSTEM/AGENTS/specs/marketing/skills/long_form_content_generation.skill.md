# Long Form Content Generation Skill

## Purpose
Generate structured long-form draft content aligned to approved strategy.

## Capabilities
- Generate structured article drafts
- Produce thought leadership content
- Produce explainers and guides

## Inputs
- Approved strategy brief
- Audience and channel requirements

## Outputs
- Long-form draft artifact
- Optional alternate draft variants

## Boundary Rules
- Drafts remain intermediate artifacts until promoted.
- Do not issue final outputs.
- Escalate if required claims cannot be supported.
