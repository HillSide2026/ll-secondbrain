# Campaign Brief Generation Skill

## Purpose
Convert strategy decisions into an execution-ready campaign brief.

## Capabilities
- Convert strategy into execution brief
- Specify deliverables
- Specify downstream agent tasks
- Produce structured campaign brief artifacts

## Inputs
- Strategy packet
- Funnel and channel definitions

## Outputs
- Structured campaign brief
- Downstream work package list

## Boundary Rules
- Briefs are planning artifacts, not final outputs.
- Do not redefine strategy scope autonomously.
- Escalate missing approvals or contradictory requirements.
