# Skill: Positioning Drift Detection

## Purpose
Identify divergence between intended positioning and market interpretation.

## Inputs
- Approved campaign positioning
- Audience feedback signals

## Process
1. Compare intended message to audience interpretation.
2. Identify confusion or misinterpretation.
3. Detect unintended associations.

## Outputs
- Positioning drift report
- Message clarity observations

## Constraints
- Cannot modify positioning.

## Invocation
Used by Market Signal Agent when analyzing market response.
