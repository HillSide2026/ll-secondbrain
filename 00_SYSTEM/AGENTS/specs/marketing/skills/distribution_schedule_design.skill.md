# Skill: Distribution Schedule Design

## Purpose
Design a structured publishing sequence for campaign assets across channels.

## Inputs
- Campaign strategy
- Channel list
- Asset inventory
- Campaign timeline

## Process
1. Identify campaign phases.
2. Map assets to phases.
3. Determine optimal channel sequence.
4. Schedule publishing cadence.
5. Ensure cross-channel coordination.

## Outputs
- Distribution schedule
- Channel publishing timeline
- Campaign rollout plan

## Constraints
- Must respect campaign strategy.
- Cannot modify campaign objectives.
- Cannot publish content directly.

## Invocation
Used by Distribution Orchestration Agent to coordinate campaign deployment.
