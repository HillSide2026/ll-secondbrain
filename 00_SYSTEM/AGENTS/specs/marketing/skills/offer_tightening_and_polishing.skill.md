# Offer Tightening And Polishing Skill

## Purpose
Refine an already-defined offer so its framing, scope language, exclusions, and
decision-usefulness are sharper, more disciplined, and easier to defend.

## Capabilities
- Tighten offer framing without changing the underlying doctrine
- Clarify scope boundaries, exclusions, and caveats
- Improve how an offer reads to a sophisticated buyer or internal reviewer
- Remove loose, promotional, or overbroad phrasing

## Inputs
- Existing offer draft or scope note
- Governing doctrine and positioning references
- Known risks, constraints, or claim boundaries

## Outputs
- Tightened offer draft
- Polishing notes
- Boundary-language recommendations

## Boundary Rules
- Do not invent a net-new offer.
- Do not expand scope by implication.
- Do not soften important exclusions for marketing appeal.
- Escalate if the offer is structurally unclear rather than merely under-polished.
