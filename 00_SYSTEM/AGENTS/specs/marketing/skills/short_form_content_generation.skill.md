# Short Form Content Generation Skill

## Purpose
Generate short-form marketing draft content for social and rapid-distribution formats.

## Capabilities
- Generate LinkedIn posts
- Generate thread structures
- Produce short marketing messages

## Inputs
- Approved strategy brief
- Platform constraints

## Outputs
- Short-form draft package
- Platform variants

## Boundary Rules
- Keep messaging within approved positioning.
- Do not authorize publication.
- Escalate where tone or claim requirements conflict.
