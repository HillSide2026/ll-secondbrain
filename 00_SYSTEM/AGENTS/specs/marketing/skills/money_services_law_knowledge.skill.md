# Money Services Law Knowledge Skill

## Purpose
Provide bounded working knowledge of Canadian money-services regulation
relevant to Funnel 3 fact patterns.

## Capabilities
- Recall the practical role of core money-services concepts such as `MSB`,
  `foreign MSB`, `money transfer`, `foreign exchange`, `virtual currency`,
  `compliance program`, and `reporting`
- Identify likely FINTRAC / PCMLTFA relevance for an entity model
- Distinguish money-services issues from adjacent securities or payment-services
  issues without collapsing them together

## Inputs
- Entity or business-model description
- Relevant guidance, legislation, or source doctrine
- Funnel 3 context or target artifact

## Outputs
- Money-services concept map
- Core authority relevance note
- Initial issue inventory

## Boundary Rules
- Do not assume all payments businesses are MSBs.
- Do not assume FINTRAC inapplicability without grounded analysis.
- Do not present internal knowledge output as final legal advice.
- Escalate mixed-regime issues for deeper analysis.
