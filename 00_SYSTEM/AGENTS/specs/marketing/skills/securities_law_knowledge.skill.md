# Securities Law Knowledge Skill

## Purpose
Provide bounded working knowledge of Canadian securities-law concepts relevant
to Funnel 3 payments, stablecoin, token, platform, and marketplace-adjacent
fact patterns.

## Capabilities
- Recall the practical role of core securities-law concepts such as `dealer`,
  `adviser`, `CTP`, `marketplace`, `custody`, `prospectus`, and `resale`
- Identify the likely relevance of core national instruments and CSA notices
- Distinguish securities-law concepts from FINTRAC / AML / stablecoin-framework
  concepts without collapsing them together

## Inputs
- Entity or business-model description
- Relevant instruments, notices, or source doctrine
- Funnel 3 context or target artifact

## Outputs
- Securities-law concept map
- Core instrument relevance note
- Initial issue inventory

## Boundary Rules
- Do not assume all crypto assets are securities.
- Do not assume securities-law inapplicability without grounded analysis.
- Do not present internal knowledge output as final legal advice.
- Escalate unclear or mixed-regime issues for deeper analysis.
