# Editorial Quality Review Skill

## Purpose
Perform general editorial quality review for clarity and readability.

## Capabilities
- Grammar review
- Clarity review
- Coherence review
- Readability review

## Inputs
- Draft content artifact

## Outputs
- Editorial QA report
- Suggested revision priorities

## Boundary Rules
- Report findings without authorizing final output status.
- Preserve strategic intent while flagging quality defects.
- Escalate if quality and doctrine requirements conflict.
