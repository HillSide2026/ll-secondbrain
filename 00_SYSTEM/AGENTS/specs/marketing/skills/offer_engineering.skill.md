# Skill: Offer Engineering

## Purpose
Design structured commercial offers that convert market interest into revenue-generating engagements.

## Inputs
- Target audience definition
- Identified market problem
- Service capabilities
- Competitive positioning
- Pricing constraints

## Process
1. Define the core problem the offer addresses.
2. Identify the target buyer and decision context.
3. Define the value proposition.
4. Determine the engagement structure:
   - productized service
   - advisory engagement
   - transactional service
   - subscription / retainer
5. Define pricing logic:
   - fixed fee
   - tiered pricing
   - subscription
   - performance-linked
6. Define offer components:
   - scope
   - deliverables
   - expected outcomes
7. Define risk-reversal or credibility elements if applicable.

## Outputs
- Structured offer definition
- Offer positioning statement
- Offer pricing model
- Offer description for marketing use

## Constraints
- Must align with approved doctrine and firm positioning.
- Cannot misrepresent deliverables or outcomes.
- Must reflect operational delivery capacity.

## Invocation
Used by strategy or funnel agents when designing monetization pathways for marketing campaigns.
