# Skill: Channel Asset Packaging

## Purpose
Transform approved marketing content into channel-specific deliverables suitable for distribution platforms.

## Inputs
- Approved content artifact
- Target channel(s)
- Campaign context
- Asset requirements

## Process
1. Identify distribution channel requirements.
2. Adapt content structure to platform format.
3. Extract or generate required components:
   - headline
   - summary
   - call-to-action
   - supporting copy
4. Package assets in structured deliverables.

## Outputs
- Channel-ready asset package
- Structured distribution artifact
- Platform-formatted content variants

## Constraints
- Content must be approved before packaging.
- Messaging must remain consistent with approved strategy.
- No substantive modification of claims or positioning.

## Invocation
Used by Distribution Orchestration Agent when preparing assets for channel deployment.
