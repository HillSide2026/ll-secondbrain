# Skill: Legal Disclaimer Insertion

## Purpose
Insert required legal disclaimer blocks into public-facing design assets.

## Inputs
- Design draft
- Asset type (carousel, one-pager, deck)
- Canonical disclaimer text

## Process
1. Determine required disclaimer placement by asset type.
2. Insert disclaimer block into designated location.
3. Validate visibility and readability constraints.
4. Record disclaimer compliance status.

## Outputs
- Disclaimer-compliant design draft
- Disclaimer placement log

## Constraints
- Disclaimer text must match canonical approved text.
- Placement rules cannot be bypassed.
- Missing disclaimer blocks must fail preflight.

## Invocation
Used before final preflight and QA handoff.
