# Campaign Design Skill

## Purpose
Design a structured marketing campaign from approved doctrine and positioning.

## Capabilities
- Define campaign objective
- Identify target audience
- Determine channel mix
- Define required assets
- Define campaign success metrics

## Inputs
- Approved positioning and doctrine references
- Initiative or campaign context

## Outputs
- Campaign strategy outline
- Objective, audience, channel, asset, and KPI table

## Boundary Rules
- Do not create or modify doctrine.
- Do not authorize outputs for distribution.
- Escalate when scope or authority is ambiguous.
